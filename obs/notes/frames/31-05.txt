===== Jesus Called Peter [31-05] =====

{{:en:obs:obs-31-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **Peter** said to **Jesus**, “**Master****Bold Text**, **if it is you**, command me to come to you on the water.” Jesus told Peter, “**Come**!”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **if it is you** - That is, "if it is really you, and not a ghost."
  * **Come!** - That is, "Come here" or, "Come to me" or, "Come, walk to me."
