====== Story #3: The Flood ======

故事#3： 洪水

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

理解性问答

  - **Why did God decide to destroy the world? 为什么神决定毁灭这世界？**
      * //People had become very wicked and violent. [03-01] //
      * //人已变得很邪恶和暴力.。//
  - **How did God plan to destroy the world? 神是怎样计划****毁灭这世界的？**
      * //He would send a flood. [03-01] //
      * //他将用洪水。//
  - **Why did Noah find favor in God's eyes? 为什么挪亚在神的眼里是蒙恩的?**
      * //He was a righteous man. [03-02]//.
      * 他是个义人.
  - **What did God tell Noah to do? 神告诉****挪亚做了什么?**
      * //Build a huge boat. [03-03] //
      * //建造一艘巨大的船. //
  - **What was the purpose of the boat? （建造）此船的目的是什么?**
      * //To keep Noah, his family, and the animals safe during the flood. [03-03] //
      * //为了挪亚、他的家人和牲畜的安全。//
  - **How did the other people react when Noah told them that the flood would come? 当挪亚告诉其他人有洪水来临时, 他们是如何反应的？**
      * //They did not believe him. [03-04] //
      * //他们不相信他.//
  - **Which animals came into the boat before the flood? 哪些牲畜在洪水来之前进入了船?**
      * //A male and a female of every kind of animal, and seven males and seven females of every type of animal that could be used for sacrifice. [03-06] //
      * //每种动物一公一母, 可用于献祭的每种动物七公七母。//
  - **Who closed the door of the boat after Noah's family and the animals came inside? **//挪亚的家人和动物进入船后, 谁把门关上了?//
      * //God closed the door. [03-06] //
      * //神把门关上了。//
  - **How long did it rain? 雨下了多长时间?**
      * //Forty days and forty nights. [03-07] //
      * //四十昼夜。//
  - **How high did the water of the flood reach? 洪水有多高?**
      * //It covered everything in the whole world, even the highest mountains. [03-08] //
      * //将整个世上的一切都淹没了, 连最高的山也淹没了。//
  - **What happened to everything that lived on land? 地上的万物发生了什么?**
      * //Everything died. [03-08] //
      * //万物都死了。//
  - **How did Noah know the water had dried up? 挪亚是怎么知道水干了?**
      * //He sent out a dove and it did not return. [03-12] //
      * //他放了一只鸽子出去，鸽子没有回来.。//
  - **What did God tell Noah and his family to do when they left the boat? 当他们离开船后, 神****告诉****挪亚****和他的家人要做什么****?**
      * //He told them to have many children and grandchildren and to fill the earth. [03-13] //
      * //他告诉他们要生养众多, 遍满全地。//
  - **How did Noah worship God after he got off the boat? ****挪亚下船后是如何敬拜神的?**
      * //He built an altar and sacrificed some of the animals. [03-14] //
      * //他筑了一座祭坛, 献上一些动物做幡祭。//
  - **What did God promise he would never do again? 神承诺了什么？说他不再做了?**
      * //Curse the ground or destroy the world with a flood. [03-15] //
      * //（不再）咒诅地上或用洪水毁灭世界。//
  - **What did God make as a sign of his promise? 神****造了什么作为他****许诺的记号****?**
      * //He made a rainbow in the sky. [03-16] //
      * //他在天上造了一道彩虹.//
