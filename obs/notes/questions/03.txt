====== Story #3: The Flood ======

故事#3： 洪水

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

问题解答

  - **Why did God decide to destroy the world? 为什么上帝决定毁灭世界？**
      * //People had become very wicked and violent. [03-01] 人变得很邪恶和暴力.//
  - **How did God plan to destroy the world? ****上帝计划怎样****毁灭这世界？**
      * //He would send a flood. [03-01] 他将用水灾.//
  - **Why did Noah find favor in God's eyes? 为什么挪亚在上帝面前蒙恩?**
      * //He was a righteous man. [03-02]//. 他是个义人.
  - **What did God tell Noah to do? ****上帝叫挪亚做什么?**
      * //Build a huge boat. [03-03] 造一条巨大的船. //
  - **What was the purpose of the boat? 此船的目的是什么?**
      * //To keep Noah, his family, and the animals safe during the flood. [03-03] 洪水期间, 保持挪亚,他的家人和动物的安全.//
  - **How did the other people react when Noah told them that the flood would come? 当挪亚告诉他们有洪水来临时, 其他人是如何反应的.**
      * //They did not believe him. [03-04] 他们不相信他.//
  - **Which animals came into the boat before the flood? 哪些动物在洪水前进入了船?**
      * //A male and a female of every kind of animal, and seven males and seven females of every type of animal that could be used for sacrifice. [03-06] 每种动物一雌一雄, 但用于献祭的每种动物为七雌七雄.//
  - **Who closed the door of the boat after Noah's family and the animals came inside? **//挪亚的家人和动物进入船后, 谁将门关上了?//
      * //God closed the door. [03-06] 上帝将门关上了.//
  - **How long did it rain? 雨下了多久?**
      * //Forty days and forty nights. [03-07] 四十昼夜.//
  - **How high did the water of the flood reach? 洪水多高?**
      * //It covered everything in the whole world, even the highest mountains. [03-08] 将世上的一切都淹没了, 连最高的山也淹没了.//
  - **What happened to everything that lived on land? 所有地上的活物发生了什么?**
      * //Everything died. [03-08] 都死了.//
  - **How did Noah know the water had dried up? 挪亚是怎么知道水干了?**
      * //He sent out a dove and it did not return. [03-12] 他放鸽子出去但它没有回来. //
  - **What did God tell Noah and his family to do when they left the boat? 当他们离开船后, ****上帝告诉****挪亚****和他的家人做什么****?**
      * //He told them to have many children and grandchildren and to fill the earth. [03-13] 他告诉他们生养众多, 遍满全地.//
  - **How did Noah worship God after he got off the boat? ****挪亚下船后是如何敬拜上帝的?**
      * //He built an altar and sacrificed some of the animals. [03-14] 他建了一座坛, 献上一些动物.//
  - **What did God promise he would never do again? 上帝许诺说他不再做什么?**
      * //Curse the ground or destroy the world with a flood. [03-15] 咒诅地或用洪水毁灭世界.//
  - **What did God make as a sign of his promise? ****上帝造了什么作为他****许诺的记号?**
      * //He made a rainbow in the sky. [03-16] 他在天上造了一道彩虹.//
