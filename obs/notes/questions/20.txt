====== Story #20: The Exile and Return 故事# 20:流亡和返回 ======

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Comprehension Questions and Answer</font>    **综合理解问答**

   - **What was the warning that the prophets spoke to the people? **  先知们警告人们什么？
      * //They told them to repent and worship God. [20-01] //  他们告诉人们要悔改和敬拜上帝。
  - **How did the people respond to the message from the prophets? **  人们对先知的警告反应如何？
      * //The people refused to obey. [20-01] //  人们拒绝服从。
  - **What enemy destroyed the kingdom of Israel? ****什么（敌）人毁灭**  了以色列王国？
      * //The Assyrian Empire. [20-02] //  亚述帝国。
  - **Who were the Samaritans?**  谁是撒玛利亚人？
      * //The descendants of the Israelites who married the foreigners brought to the land by the Assyrians. [20-04] //  以色列人与亚述人带来此地的外国人结婚的后代。
  - **Did the people of the kingdom of Judah obey God when they saw how he had punished the kingdom of Israel? ** 当犹大王国的人们看到上帝如何惩罚以色列的国时，他们顺从神了吗？
      * //No, they continued worshiping idols. [20-05] // 不,他们继续崇拜偶像。
  - **Who did the king of Judah agree to serve? ** 犹大王同意服务谁？
      * //King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon. [20-06] // 巴比伦的尼布甲尼撒王。
  - **When the king of Judah rebelled, what did Nebuchadnezzar’s soldiers do to him? ** 当犹大王反叛，尼布甲尼撒的士兵对他做了什么?
      * //They killed the king’s sons in front of him, made him blind, and took him away to die in prison in Babylon. [20-08] //
  - **What do we call the period of time when God’s people were forced to leave the Promised Land?**
      * //The Exile. [20-09]//
  - **What did God promise to the people during the Exile?**
      * //God promised that after seventy years, they would return to the Promised Land. [20-10]//
  - **Who was the king who defeated the Babylonians?**
      * //King Cyrus of the Persian Empire. [20-11]//
  - **What order did King Cyrus give concerning the Jews?**
      * //Any Jew who wanted to return to Judah could go there. [20-12]//
  - **What did the Jews do when they returned to Jerusalem?**
      * //They rebuilt the Temple and the wall around the city. [20-13]//
