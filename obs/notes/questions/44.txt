====== Story #44: Peter and John Heal a Beggar  故事#44 约    翰治愈一个乞丐 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers 理解性问题和解答 =====

  - **What did the crippled man expect Peter and John to give him? ** 一个跛子希望从彼得和  翰那里得到什么？​
      * //Money. [44-01] 钱//
  - **What did Peter give to the crippled man instead? ** 彼得给了这个跛子什么而代之？​
      * //Peter healed him in the name of Jesus. [44-02] // 彼得以耶 稣    的名   治好了他的腿​。
  - **What did Peter say caused the crippled man to be healed? ** 彼得 说是   什么治好了残疾人的腿？​
      * //The power of Jesus and the faith that Jesus gives. [44-04] 耶稣的能力和// 对//耶稣的信。//
  - **Who did Peter say killed Jesus? ** 彼得说谁杀   了耶
      * //He said the people in the crowd asked the Roman governor to kill Jesus. [44-05] // 他 说那些在   人群要求罗马总   督 杀   耶 稣的人
  - **What prophecies did the people fulfill when they killed Jesus? ** 当人们 杀耶 稣时，他们实现了   什么 预   言？​
      * //The prophecies that the Messiah would suffer and die. [44-05] // 弥 赛亚   将受苦和被 杀 的 预 言。
  - **What did Peter tell the people that they should do? ** 彼得告 诉    众人 应 当做什么？​
      * //They should repent and turn to God, so that their sins would be washed away. [44-05] // 人   们应 当 悔改转向上帝，这样他们的罪会被洗掉。
  - **How many men believed in Jesus as a result of Peter’s message? ** 彼得的信息使多少人信了耶稣？
      * //About 5000. [44-06] 大约5000人。//
  - **What is the only way to be saved? **  得救的 唯一方法 是什么？​
      * //The only way to be saved is through the power of Jesus. [44-08] // 得救的 唯一方法 是通过耶稣的大 能 ​
  - **Why were the Jewish leaders surprised at the preaching of Peter and John? ** 为什么犹太人的领袖们对彼得和约翰的传道很惊讶？
      * //Peter and John were ordinary, uneducated men. [44-09] // 彼得和约翰是没有受过教育的普通人。
  - **What did the Jewish leaders finally decide to do with Peter and John? **  犹太人的领袖们最后决定如何对待彼得和约翰？​
      * //They threatened them and let them go. [44-09] // 他们威胁了彼得和约翰，并让他们离开。
