====== Story #2: Sin Enters the World ======

故事#2： 罪进入世界

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

综合理解问答

  - **Why were Adam and Eve not ashamed that they were naked? 为什么亚当和夏娃赤身裸体并不感到羞耻？**
      * //There was no sin in the world. [02-01] //
      * //那时世上没有罪。//
  - **What was the snake's first question to Eve? 蛇对夏娃问的第一个问题是什么？**
      * //Did God really tell you not to eat the fruit from any of the trees in the garden? [02-02] //
      * //神真的告诉你不可吃园中任何树上的果子吗？//
  - **From Which Tree Adam and Eve were not allowed to eat? ****哪棵树上的（果子）是****不允许****亚当和夏娃吃的****?**
      * //They were not to eat from tree of the knowledge of good and evil. [02-03] //
      * //他们不能吃善恶智慧树上的果子。//
  - **What did God say would happen if Adam and his wife ate from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil? ****神说****如果亚当和他的妻子吃了善恶知识上的果子将会发生****什么****？ **
      * //He said they would die. [02-03] //
      * //他说他们会死。//
  - **What did the snake say was the reason God did not want them to eat the fruit? 蛇说什么是神不要他们吃这一果子的原因？**
      * //He said God was lying because God did not want them to understand things like he did. [02-04] //
      * //他说神在说谎，因为神不想让他们象他一样知道善恶。//
  - **Why did the woman eat the fruit? 为什么那女人吃了果子？**
      * //She saw that it was beautiful and delicious, and she wanted to be wise. [02-05] //
      * //她看见这果子很漂亮（悦人眼目），也很好吃，而且她想要有智慧。//
  - **Were Adam and his wife forced to eat the fruit? 亚当和夏娃是被迫吃那果子的吗？**
      * //No, they chose freely to eat the fruit and disobey God. [02-05] //
      * //不是，他们自己选择吃那果子，悖逆了神。//
  - **What did the man and the woman do when they realized they were naked? ****当那男人和女人意识到他们****赤身裸体时，**** 他们做了什么？**
      * //They sewed leaves together to make clothes. [02-06] //
      * //他们将（无花果树）////树叶缝在一起做成衣服。//
  - **How did the man respond when God confronted him about his sin? 当神怒斥他问他的罪时， 那男人是怎么回答的？**
      * //He blamed the woman. [02-08] //
      * //他责备那女人。//
  - **How did the woman respond when God confronted her about her sin? ****当神怒斥那女人****问她的罪时，**** 那女人是怎么回答？**
      * //She blamed the snake. [02-08]//
      * //她责备那蛇。//
  - **What was God's curse on the snake? ****神****对蛇的咒诅是什么？**
      * //You will slide on your belly, and the woman's descendent will crush your head. [02-09] //
      * //你必用肚子行走，并且女人的后裔会打碎你的头。//
  - **What was God's curse on the woman? ****神****对女人的咒诅是什么？**
      * //You will have painful childbirth, and although your desire will be for your husband, he will rule over you. [02-10] //
      * //你生产儿女时将会痛苦。 虽然你的心思意念在你丈夫身上，但他必管辖你。 //
  - **What was God's curse on the man? ****神****对男人的咒诅是什么？**
      * //You will work hard to grow food, and you will die and return to the dirt. [02-11] //
      * //你将辛勤耕耘才获得食物，而且将会死，归于尘土。//
  - **How did God prevent Adam and Eve from living forever?神怎样阻止亚当和夏娃永远活下去？**
      * //He sent them out of the garden and guarded the entrance with powerful angels. [02-12] //
      * //他将他们赶出园子，并由大有能力的天使把守（园子的）入口处 。//
