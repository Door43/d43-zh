====== Story #4: God's Covenant with Abraham ======

故事#4： 上帝与亚伯拉罕的约

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

问题解答

  - **After the flood, did the people fill the earth as God had commanded? 洪水后，人们按上帝的指示遍布地球？**
      * //No. They gathered together and built a city. [04-01] 不，他们聚集在一起， 并建了一座城。//
  - **How many different languages were there in the world at that time? 那时世上有多少种语言?**
      * //There was only one language. [04-01] 只有一种。//
  - **What did the people work together to do instead of filling the earth? ****人们没有****遍布地球而是在一起做什么？**
      * //The started to build a tall tower to reach heaven. [04-02] 他们开始建一座通天的塔。//
  - **What did God do to cause the people to spread out all over the world? 上帝做了什么使人们分散到世界各地？**
      * //He changed their language into many different languages. [04-03] 他将他们的语言变成了不同语言。//
  - **What was the name of the city that they were building? 他们在建的城的名字叫什么？**
      * //Babel. [04-03] 巴别。//
  - **What does the name "Babel" mean? 巴别名字的意思是什么？**
      * //Confused. [04-03] 混淆。//
  - **What did God tell Abram to do? 上帝叫亚伯兰做什么？**
      * //God told Abram to leave his country and family and go to another land. [04-04] 上帝叫亚伯兰离开本乡本族去他乡。//
  - **What did God promise to do for Abram? ****上帝向****亚伯兰承诺（做）什么？**
      * //He promised to give Abram all the land he could see, to make his name great, to make his descendants a great nation, and to bless all the families on earth through him. [04-04] 他承诺给亚伯兰所有他所能看到的土地，使你的名为大，使他的后裔成为一个大国，并且地上万族（所有的家庭）都要因他而的福。//
  - **To which land did Abram go? ****亚伯兰去哪地？**
      * //The land of Canaan. [04-05] 迦南地。//
  - **What promise did God make to Abram about Canaan? **
      * //God would give it to Abram and his descendants as an inheritance. [04-06]//
  - **Who was Melchizedek?**
      * //He was the priest of God Most High. [04-07]//
  - **What did Melchizedek do for Abram?**
      * //He blessed Abram. [04-07]//
  - **What did Abram give to Melchizedek?**
      * //A tenth of all he owned. [04-07]//
  - **What did God promise Abram after he had lived in Canaan for many years?**
      * //Abram would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. [04-08]//
  - **Why did God say Abram was righteous?**
      * //Because Abram believed God's promise. [04-08]//
  - **What is a covenant?**
      * //An agreement between two parties. [04-09]//
