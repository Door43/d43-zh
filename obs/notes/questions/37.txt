====== Story #37: Jesus Raises Lazarus from the Dead ======

故事# 37:耶稣将拉撒路从死里复活

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers ===== 领悟问题和答案

  - **What did Jesus say would be the end result of Lazarus’ sickness?**  耶稣说拉撒路的病最后将是什么结果？
      * //It would be for the glory of God. [37-01]// 这将是神的荣耀。 
  - **What did Jesus do after he heard about Lazarus’ sickness?** 耶稣听说关于拉撒路的病后他做了什么?
      * //He waited where he was for two more days. [37-01]// 他在原地（他所在的地方）多待了两天。
  - **Why were the disciples concerned when Jesus decided to go to Judea?** 当耶稣决定去犹太时，为什么门徒很忧虑？
      * //Because the people there wanted to kill him. [37-02]// 因为那里的人想杀他。
  - **What did the disciples think Jesus meant when he said that Lazarus had fallen asleep?** 当耶稣基督说拉撒路已经睡着了时，门徒们怎么想的？
      * //They thought he was just asleep and would get better. [37-03]// 他们认为他只是睡着了然后会好起来
  - **What did Jesus plainly tell the disciples about Lazarus?**耶稣直白地告诉门徒拉撒路怎么了？
      * //Jesus said that Lazarus had died. [37-03]// 耶稣说拉撒路已经死了。
  - **Why was Jesus glad that he was not there when Lazarus died?** 为什么耶稣因为拉撒路死时他不在那里而高兴？
      * //Because something would happen that would make the disciples believe in Jesus. [37-03]//因为将会发生的事情会使门徒们相信耶稣。
  - **Did Martha believe that Jesus could have healed Lazarus?** 马大是否相信耶稣能医治拉撒路？
      * //Yes. [37-04]//是的。
  - **What will happen to believers because Jesus is the Resurrection and the Life?** 因为耶稣就是复活和生命，信徒将来会发生什么？
      * //Whoever believes in him will live, even though he dies, and everyone who believes in him will never die. [37-05]// 谁相信他谁就活，即使他死了（也不例外），每个相信他的人将不再死亡。 
  - **Who did Martha believe that Jesus was?** 马大相信耶稣是谁?
      * //The Messiah, the Son of God. [37-05]// 弥赛亚，神的儿子。
  - **How long had Lazarus been dead?** 拉撒路已经死了多久?
      * //He had been dead for four days. [37-04], [37-07]// 他已经死了四天。
  - **Why did Jesus thank the Father out loud?** 为什么耶稣大声感谢父亲？
      * //He did that so the people would believe that the Father had sent him. [37-09]// 他这样做是为了让人们相信他是父亲派来的。
  - **What did Jesus command Lazarus to do?**  耶稣让拉撒路做了什么?
      * //He commanded Lazarus to come out of the tomb! [37-09]// 他吩咐拉撒路走出坟墓! 
  - **How did the religious leaders of the Jews react when they saw the miracle?**  当犹太人的宗教领袖们看到奇迹时是如何反应的？
      * //They became jealous and planned to kill Jesus and Lazarus. [37-11]// 他们变得嫉妒，并打算杀了耶稣和拉撒路。
