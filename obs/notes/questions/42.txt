====== Story #42: Jesus Returns to Heaven 故事#42：耶稣 返回天国 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers 理解性问题和解答 =====

  - **What were the two disciples talking about as they walked along the road? **  两个门   徒行 在路上    在 谈论 什么？  
    * //They were talking about what had happened to Jesus. [42-01] //  他 们 在 谈论 耶 稣 身上 发 生了什么事​。
  - **Who did the two disciples think Jesus was when he approached them on the road? **  当耶 稣在路上 走近他 们 时 ，两个 门徒 认为他 是 谁？ 
    * //They thought he was a visitor who did not know what had happened in Jerusalem. [42-02] //  他 们认为他是一个 不知道在耶路撒冷 发生了什么事的来客。
  - **What had the prophets said would happen to the Messiah? **  先知预言说什么将会 发生在 弥赛亚的身上？
    * //The Messiah would suffer and be killed, but would rise again on the third day. [42-03] //  弥赛亚将会受苦并被杀， 但在第三日会复活
  - **What was Jesus doing when the two disciples recognized him? **  当两个门徒认出他时 耶 稣正在 做什么？ 
    * //He was breaking a loaf of bread and thanking God for it. [42-04] //  他正在掰开一个面包和感谢神。
  - **What did the two disciples do after Jesus left them? **  耶稣离开他们之后，两个门徒做了什么？ 
    * //They returned to Jerusalem and told the other disciples that Jesus was alive and that they had seen him. [42-05] //  他们返回耶路撒冷告诉其他门徒们 耶稣活了 他们已见过他。
  - **How did Jesus prove to the disciples that he was not a ghost? **  耶稣怎样向门徒们证明他不是一个鬼魂？ 
    * //He took a piece of cooked fish and ate it. [42-06] //  他拿了一片煮熟的鱼吃了。
  - **What enabled the disciples to understand God’s word? **  什么使得门徒们能够明白神的话语？ 
    * //Jesus opened their minds. [42-07] //  耶稣打开了他们的思路。
  - **What was the message that Jesus’ disciples were to proclaim? **  耶稣的门徒们在传扬什么信息？ 
    * //They would proclaim that everyone should repent in order to receive forgiveness for their sins. [42-08] //  他们将传扬使罪得赦免， 每个人都应该悔改。​
  - **Where were Jesus’ disciples to proclaim this message? **  耶稣的门徒们在哪里传扬这个信息？ 
    * //They would proclaim it first in Jerusalem, and then go to all people groups everywhere. [42-08] //  他们先在耶路撒冷，后在全地（向所有人群）宣扬。
  - **How long did Jesus continue to appear to the disciples?**  耶稣持续向门徒们显现了多久？ 
    * //He appeared to them for 40 days. [42-09] //  他向他们显现了40天之久。
  - **How big was the largest group of disciples that saw Jesus during this time? **  在那 一次 ​里， 最多有多少门徒看到耶稣的显现？​      
    * //He appeared to more than 500 disciples at the same time. [42-09] //  他同时向500 个门徒显现。​
  - **What did Jesus command his disciples to go and do? **   耶稣命令他的门徒们去做什么？​      
    * //He said to them, “Make disciples of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.” [42-10] //  他对门徒们说“ 以圣父、圣子、圣灵的名义给所有民族的人施洗，使他们成为门徒。并教他们遵守一切我已命令过你们的事”。
  - **Why did Jesus tell the disciples to wait in Jerusalem? **  为什么耶稣要门徒们在耶路撒冷等候？ 
    * //They were to wait until the Father gave them power when the Holy Spirit came on them. [42-11] //  门徒们在等圣灵降临到他们的时候神赐给他们的能力。
  - **Where did Jesus go forty days after his resurrection? ** 在复活四十天之后，耶稣去了哪里？ 
   * //He went up to heaven. [42-11] //  t他上升到天堂去了。
  - **What is Jesus doing right now? **   耶稣现在在做什么？​
      * //He is seated at the right hand of God and ruling over all things. [42-11] //  他正坐在天父的右手边管理全地。
