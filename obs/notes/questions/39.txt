====== Story #39: Jesus Is Put on Trial 故事# 39    耶稣受审 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers 理解性问题和答案 =====

  - **What time of the day was it when the Jewish leaders began to question Jesus? **  犹太首领​们在那天什么时候开始审问耶稣的？
    * //It was the middle of the night. [39-01] //  是在半夜。
  - **Why could the Jewish leaders not prove Jesus was guilty of anything? **  为什么犹太首领​们无法证明耶稣是有罪的？
    * //The statements of the false witnesses did not agree with each other. [39-02] // 因为那些假证人的证词相互不一致。
  - **What question did the high priest finally ask Jesus? **  大祭司最后问了耶稣什么问题
    * //“Tell us, are you the Messiah, the Son of the living God?” [39-03] //“告诉我们，你是不是弥赛亚，永生神的儿子? “
  - **What answer did Jesus give to the high priest? **  耶稣给了大祭司什么答案
    * //“I am, and you will see me seated with God and coming from heaven.” [39-04] //  “我是，你会看到我是来自天堂并坐在上帝身边。 “
  - **What was the crime that the high priest said that Jesus had committed? **  大祭司说耶稣犯了什么罪？
    * //Jesus said he was the Son of God. [39-04] //  耶稣说他是神的儿子。
  - **Where was Peter during Jesus' trial? **  耶稣受审时彼得在哪里？
    * //Peter was waiting outside the house of the high priest. [39-06] //  彼得在大祭司屋外等着。
  - **Why did the people think that Peter had been with Jesus? **  < 为什么人们认为彼得一直与耶稣在一起？​
    * //Peter and Jesus were both from Galilee. [39-06] //  彼得和耶稣都是从加利利来的。
  - **What did Peter say when the people asked if he knew Jesus ? **  当人们问彼得是否认识耶稣时他说怎么说？
    * //Peter denied three times that he knew Jesus. [39-07] //  彼得三次否认他认识耶稣。
  - **What happened immediately after Peter denied Jesus the third time? **  当彼得第三次否认认识耶稣后立即发生了什    * //A rooster crowed, and Jesus turned and looked at Peter. [39-07] //  鸡就叫了，耶稣转过身来看彼得。
  - **What did Judas do when he saw that the Jewish leaders had condemned Jesus? **  当犹大看到犹太首领对耶稣定罪时他做了什么？
    * //Judas became full of sorrow and went away and killed himself. [39-08] //  犹大变得很懊悔， 离开并自杀了。
  - **Why did the Jewish leaders take Jesus to the Roman governor Pilate? **  为什么犹太人首领带耶稣去罗马总督彼拉多
    * //They hoped Pilate would condemn Jesus and sentence him to be killed. [39-09] //  他们希望彼拉多将耶稣定罪并宣判被杀。
  - **What was the first question that Pilate asked Jesus? **  彼拉多问耶稣的第一个问题是什么? 
    * //“Are you the king of the Jews?” [39-09] //“你是犹太人的王吗? “
  - **What did Jesus say was the reason he came to earth? **  耶稣说他来到地球的原因是什么？
    * //To tell the truth about God. [39-10] // 讲述真理。​
  - **How many times did Pilate say to the crowd that Jesus was not guilty? ** 彼拉多对人群说了多少次耶稣是无罪 
    * //Three times. [39-11] // 三次。
  - **Why then did Pilate allow Jesus to be crucified? ** 那么为什么彼拉多还允许耶稣被钉在十字架上？
    * //Pilate became afraid that the crowd would begin to riot. [39-12] // 彼拉多怕人群开始暴动。
  - **How did the Roman soldiers treat Jesus? **  罗马士兵是怎么对待耶稣?     
    * //They whipped Jesus, put a royal robe and a crown of thorns on him, and mocked him. [39-12] // 他们鞭打耶稣给他穿上皇袍和戴上荆棘王冠，并嘲笑他。
