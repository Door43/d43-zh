====== Bethlehem伯利恒 ======

===== Facts: =====

实例

Bethlehem was a small city in the land of Israel, near the city of Jerusalem. It still exists to the present day.

伯利恒是以色列地的一个小城，靠近耶路撒冷城。它至今仍然存在。

  * King David was born in Bethlehem; for this reason it has been called the "city of David."
  * 大卫王出生在伯利恒；为着这个原因，它也被称为“大卫之城”。
  * The prophet Micah said that the Messiah would come from Bethlehem.
  * 先知弥迦曾说，弥赛亚会从伯利恒来。
  * Bethlehem is the city where Jesus was born.
  * 伯利恒是耶稣出生的地方。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**]

[参见：大卫]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a shepherd from the town of **Bethlehem**.
  * 大卫曾是个从伯利恒城来的牧人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin. The prophet Micah said that he would be born in the town of **Bethlehem**.
  * 先知以赛亚预言道，弥赛亚将由一个处女而生。先知弥迦说，弥赛亚将出生在伯利恒。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to **Bethlehem**  because their ancestor was David whose hometown was **Bethlehem**.
  * 耶稣和玛利亚只好从他们居住的拿撒勒长途旅行到伯利恒，因为他们的先祖是大卫，其家乡就是伯利恒。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]**"The Messiah, the Master, has been born in **Bethlehem**!"
  * “弥赛亚，主，出生在伯利恒”！
