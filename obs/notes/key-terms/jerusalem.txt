====== Jerusalem耶路撒冷 ======

耶路撒冷

===== Facts: =====

實情：

Jerusalem was a city in the land of Israel. It is still there today.

耶路撒冷是一座位於以色列土地上的城市。直到今天它仍在那裡。

  * King David conquered Jerusalem from one of the Canaanite tribes and made it his capital.
  * 大偉王曾經從迦南部落中的一個部落的手中征服了耶路撒冷，並且把它立為他自己的首都。
  * The temple was built in Jerusalem by David's son, King Solomon.
  * 這聖殿就是由大偉的兒子，所羅門王，在耶路撒冷城裡所建造的。
  * God sent the Babylonians to destroy Jerusalem because of Israel's sin. However, he allowed them to return and rebuild Jerusalem 70 years later
  * 神因為以色列人的罪的緣故，曾經差遣迦勒底人去摧毀了耶路撒冷。可是經過了七十年後，他又允許他們回去重建耶路撒冷。
  * Jerusalem was the city where Jesus the Messiah was put on trial and sentenced to die on a cross.
  * 耶路撒冷就是那座救世主耶穌（彌賽亞）曾經被送上十字架上受審並被判死刑的那個城市。
  * Jerusalem was one of the where Jesus spent time with his disciples after he came back to life, and before returning to heaven.
  * 耶路撒冷就是耶穌復活後升天之前與他的門徒相處過的地方之一。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:solomon|Solomon]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]

【參見：大偉，所羅門，救世主（彌賽亞），耶穌】

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事裡的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** David conquered **Jerusalem**  and made it his capital city.
  * 【17－05】大偉不但征服了耶路撒冷而且把它立為他自己的首都。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-02|[18-02]]]** In **Jerusalem**, Solomon built the Temple for which his father David had planned and gathered materials.
  * 【18－02】聖殿是由所羅門在耶路撒冷城裡建造的，但是其建造所需要的計畫和材料卻是由其父親大偉所策劃和預備的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-07|[20-07]]]** They (Babylonians) captured the city of **Jerusalem**, destroyed the Temple, and took away all the treasures of the city and the Temple.
  * 【20－07】他們（迦勒底人）攻佔了耶路撒冷城之後，便把聖殿給摧毀了，同時還把所有在城裡的和在聖殿裡的財寶全都擄了去。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-12|[20-12]]]** So, after seventy years in exile, a small group of Jews returned to the city of **Jerusalem**  in Judah.
  * 被擄後經過七十年，有一小群猶太人又回到在猶大的耶路撒冷城裡。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** About three years after Jesus first began preaching and teaching publicly, Jesus told his disciples that he wanted to celebrate this Passover with them in **Jerusalem**, and that he would be killed there.
  * 【38－01】在耶穌第一次開始公開傳道和宣教之後，大約有三年之久，耶穌才告訴他的門徒，他要和他們一起在耶路撒冷過逾越節，他還會在那裡被殺。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** After Jesus and the disciples arrived in **Jerusalem**, Judas went to the Jewish leaders and offered to betray Jesus to them in exchange for money.
  * 【38－02】當耶穌和他的門徒來到耶路撒冷之後，猶大便去見猶太人的首領們，表示他願意出賣耶穌給他們來換取金錢的利益。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]**"It was also written in the scriptures that my disciples will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to receive forgiveness for their sins. They will do this starting in **Jerusalem**, and then go to all people groups everywhere. "
  * 【42－08】經上記著說：我的門徒會去傳講人人都當悔改使罪得赦的福音。他們要從耶路撒冷傳起，直傳到各地各族為止。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-11|[42-11]]]** Forty days after Jesus rose from the dead, he told his disciples, "Stay in **Jerusalem**  until you receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you."
  * 【42－11】耶穌從死裡復活後在地上又過了四十天，他吩咐他的門徒說：“留在耶路撒冷裡，直等到聖靈降在你們身上得著能力為止”。
