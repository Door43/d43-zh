====== sacrifice 祭 F ======

==== (sacrifice, offering) ====

（祭，奉献）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A sacrifice is an offering to God of something valuable as a form of worship.

祭是献给神一件贵重物品，作为一种敬拜的方式。

  * The Israelites made several different kinds of sacrifices. These included sacrifices to ask God to forgive sin and sacrifices to thank him for blessings.
  * 以色列人有许多不同的祭。这包括祈求神要饶恕他们的罪的祭和感谢神祝福的祭。
  * Most sacrifices involved killing an animal and offering it to God as a gift.
  * 大部分的祭都要宰杀一只动物，并把它作为礼物献给神。

Animal sacrifices could never truly make a person clean from his sin. Only the sacrifice of Jesus, God's perfect, sinless Son, can cleanse people from sin.

动物的祭从不会真正地使人的罪得洁净。只有神完美无罪的儿子耶稣，他作为的祭，方能使人从罪里得洁净。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>圣经故事中的</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>例子</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>：</font>

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-14|[03-14]]]** After Noah got off the boat, he built an altar and **sacrificed**  some of each kind of animal which could be used for a **sacrifice**. God was happy with the **sacrifice**  and blessed Noah and his family.
  * 挪亚下了船后，他筑了一个祭坛并把每一种可用来作为祭物的动物献上一些为祭。神喜悦这祭，赐福挪亚和他的家人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]**"Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a **sacrifice**  to me." Again Abraham obeyed God and prepared to **sacrifice**  his son.
  * “把以撒，你独生的儿子，杀了他作为祭献给我”。亚伯拉罕再次顺服神，并准备牺牲他的儿子作为祭物。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-09|[05-09]]]** God had provided the ram to be the **sacrifice**  instead of Isaac.
  * 神预备了公羊作祭物来代替以撒。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed God's law could bring an animal to the Tent of Meeting as a **sacrifice**  to God. A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the altar. The blood of the animal that was **sacrificed**  covered the person's sin and made that person clean in God's sight.
  * 任何一个违背了神律法的人都可以带一只动物到会幕作为祭物献给神。祭司会杀了动物，把它烧在祭坛上。被献为祭的动物的血遮盖了那人的罪，使那人在神的眼里看为洁净。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-06|[17-06]]]** David wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could worship God and offer him **sacrifices**.
  * 大卫想建一座圣殿，在那里所有的以色列人都可以敬拜神，并献祭给神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-06|[48-06]]]** Jesus is the Great High Priest. Unlike other priests, he offered himself as the only **sacrifice**  that could to take away the sin of all the people in the world.
  * 耶稣是至高的大祭司。不像其他的祭司，他把自己作为唯一的祭献上，来除去世上所有人的罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-08|[48-08]]]** But God provided Jesus, the Lamb of God, as a **sacrifice**  to die in our place.
  * 但是神预备了耶稣，神的羔羊，作为祭代替我们死。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-11|[49-11]]]** Because Jesus **sacrificed**  himself, God can forgive any sin, even terrible sins.
  * 因为耶稣已献上他自己为祭，神可以赦免任何的罪，即使是那些可怕的罪。
