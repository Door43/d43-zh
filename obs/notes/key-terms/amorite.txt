====== Amorite亚摩利人 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实（考古）依据：

The Amorites were a group of people living in Canaan in the time of Joshua.

亚摩利人是在约书亚时代居住在迦南地的一族人。

  * The Amorites lived in cities on both sides of the Jordan River. Each city was ruled by a king.
  * 亚摩利人居住在沿约旦河两侧的城镇中。每座城镇都由一个王统治着。
  * God had promised to give the Israelites the land of Canaan, including the land of the Amorites.
  * 神曾应许给以色列人的迦南地，也包括亚摩利人的地。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:15-07|[15-07]]]** Sometime later, the kings of another people group in Canaan, the **Amorites**, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked Gibeon.
  * [15-07]一段时间以后，在迦南的另一部落，就是亚摩利人的众王，听说基遍人和以色列人订立了一条和约，于是他们把军队合并成一支庞大的的军队，攻打基遍。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:15-08|[15-08]]]** In the early morning they surprised the **Amorite**  armies and attacked them.
  * [15-08]在清早，他们突袭了亚摩人的军队。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:15-09|[15-09]]]** God fought for Israel that day. He caused the **Amorites**  to be confused and he sent large hailstones that killed many of the **Amorites**.
  * [15-09]那天，神为以色列人而战。祂使亚摩人惊惶失措，祂降下大冰雹，砸死了很多亚摩利人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:15-10|[15-10]]]** God also caused the sun to stay in one place in the sky so that Israel would have enough time to completely defeat the **Amorites**.
  * [15-10]神也使太阳在空中停住，这样以色列人将有足够的时间去彻底打败亚摩利人。
