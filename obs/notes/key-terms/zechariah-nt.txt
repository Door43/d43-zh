====== Zechariah 撒迦利亚 F ======

===== Facts: 神迹 =====

Zechariah was a Jewish priest who became the father of John the Baptist.

撒迦利亚是一个犹太裔的祭祀，后来成为施洗约翰的父亲。

  * Zechariah loved God and obeyed him.
  * 撒迦利亚爱神并尊崇他。
  * For many years Zechariah and his wife, Elizabeth, prayed earnestly to have a child, but did not have one. Then when they were very old, God answered their prayers and gave them a son.
  * 很多年来，撒迦利亚和他的妻子伊丽莎白，极力祷告想要有一个孩子，但是却不能。后来他们老了，神应许了他们的祷告，赐给他们一个儿子。

  * Zechariah prophesied that his son John would be the prophet who would announce and prepare the way for the Messiah.
  * 撒迦利亚预言，他的儿子约翰将会是个先知，并将为弥赛亚宣布并准备了道路。

===== Examples from the Bible stories:、 =====

圣经故事里的例子

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-01|[22-01]]]** Suddenly an angel came with a message from God to an old priest named **Zechariah**. **Zechariah**  and his wife, Elizabeth, were godly people, but she had not been able to have any children.
  * 突然一个天使带着上帝的消息，来到一个名叫撒迦利亚的老祭祀面前。撒迦利亚和他的妻子伊丽莎白，都是虔诚的人，但她一直没能有任何孩子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-02|[22-02]]]** The angel said to **Zechariah**, "Your wife will have a son. You will name him John."
  * 天使对撒迦利亚说，“你的妻子将有一个儿子。你将给他起名叫约翰”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-03|[22-03]]]** Immediately, **Zechariah**  was unable to speak.
  * 立刻，撒迦利亚不能够说话了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-07|[22-07]]]** Then God allowed **Zechariah**  to speak again.
  * 于是神允许撒迦利亚再次能说话。
