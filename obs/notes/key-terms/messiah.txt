====== Messiah 弥赛亚 P ======

==== (Messiah, Holy One, Christ) ====

（弥赛亚，圣者，基督）

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

"Messiah" means, "Anointed One" or, "Chosen One" and refers to God's Son, whom God the Father appointed to rule over his people, and to save them from sin and death.

“弥赛亚”意思是“受膏者”，或 “被拣选者”，意指神的儿子，他被父神指派来统治他的子民，并拯救他们脱离罪和死亡。

  * Hundreds of years before the Messiah came, God's prophets told many things about the Messiah. These prophecies are written in the Bible.
  * 在弥赛亚到来的几百年前，神的先知们就预言了很多关于弥赛亚的事情。这些预言都被写在圣经里。
  * During Jesus' time living on earth, he fulfilled many of those prophecies; someday he will come back and fulfill the rest of the prophecies about the Messiah.
  * 在耶稣生活在地上的时候，他应验了很多这些预言；有一天，耶稣将再回来，应验先知们关于弥赛亚其余的预言。
  * Jesus also did many miraculous works to prove that he is the Messiah.
  * 耶稣也行了很多的神迹，来证明他就是弥赛亚。
  * The Messiah is a descendent of King David.
  * 弥赛亚是大卫王的后裔。
  * In the Greek language, the word for Messiah is "Christ."
  * 在希腊文，弥赛亚一词就是“基督”。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:god-son|Son of God]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]

[参见：神的儿子，大卫，耶稣]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]** The **Messiah**  was God's Chosen One who would save the people of the world from sin.
  * 弥赛亚是神拣选的那位，他将拯救这世上的人脱离罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-08|[17-08]]]** As it happened, the Israelites would have to wait a long time before the **Messiah**  came, almost 1,000 years.
  * 正如所发生的，在弥赛亚到来之前，以色列人将不得不等待很长一段时间，几乎是1000年。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-01|[21-01]]]** From the very beginning, God planned to send the **Messiah**.
  * 从一开始，神就计划派遣弥赛亚。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-04|[21-04]]]** God promised King David that the **Messiah**  would be one of David's own descendants.
  * 神应许大卫王，弥赛亚将是大卫的一个后裔。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** The **Messiah**  would start the New Covenant.
  * 弥赛亚将开启新约。
  * **[21-06] **  The prophets of God also said, "**Messiah**  would be a prophet, a priest, and a king."
  * 神的先知们也说：“弥赛亚将是一位先知，一位祭司和一位君王。”
  * The prophet Isaiah prophesied that the **Messiah**  would be born from a virgin.
  * 先知以赛亚曾预言，弥赛亚将由一个处女而生。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]**"But God raised him to life again to fulfill the prophecy which says, 'You will not let your **Holy One**  rot in the grave.'"
  * “但是神使他复活，去应验所说的预言：”你将不会让你的圣者腐烂在坟墓里（你必不叫你的圣者见朽坏 ）。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-09|[43-09]]]**"But know for certain that God has caused Jesus to become both Lord and **Messiah**!"
  * “但是，要知道，神已经使耶稣为主和为弥赛亚！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-11|[43-11]]]** Peter answered them, "Every one of you should repent and be baptized in the name of Jesus **Christ**  so that God will forgive your sins."
  * 彼得回答他们：”你们各人都应该悔改，都要奉耶稣基督的名受洗，这样，神将会赦免你们的罪。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-06|[46-06]]]** Saul reasoned with the Jews, proving that Jesus was the **Messiah**.
  * 扫罗和犹太人讲道理，证明耶稣就是弥赛亚。
