====== Messiah弥赛亚 ======

==== (Messiah, Holy One, Christ) ====

（弥赛亚，圣者，基督）

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

"Messiah" means, "Anointed One" or, "Chosen One" and refers to God's Son, whom God the Father appointed to rule over his people, and to save them from sin and death.

“弥赛亚”意思是，“受膏者”，或 ”被拣选者“，既意指神的儿子，他被神天父指派来统治他的子民，并拯救他们远离他们的罪和死亡。

  * Hundreds of years before the Messiah came, God's prophets told many things about the Messiah. These prophecies are written in the Bible.
  * 在弥赛亚到来的几百年前，神的先知们预言了很多关于弥赛亚的事情。这些预言都被写在圣经里。
  * During Jesus' time living on earth, he fulfilled many of those prophecies; someday he will come back and fulfill the rest of the prophecies about the Messiah.
  * 在耶稣生活在地上的时代，他应验了很多预言；某一天，他将会回来，应验先知们关于弥赛亚的其余的预言。
  * Jesus also did many miraculous works to prove that he is the Messiah.
  * 耶稣也行了很多的神迹，来证明他就是弥赛亚。
  * The Messiah is a descendent of King David.
  * 弥赛亚是大卫王的后裔。
  * In the Greek language, the word for Messiah is "Christ."
  * 在希腊语言里。弥赛亚一词就是“基督”。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:god-son|Son of God]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]

[参见：神的儿子，大卫，耶稣]
