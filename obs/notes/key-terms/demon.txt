====== demon 惡魔 ======

===== Definition: =====

定義

Demons are spirit beings that oppose God's will.

惡魔就是那些反對神旨意的邪靈

  * God created spirit beings to serve him. Some rebelled against him and are called "demons." Those who continue to serve him are called "angels."
  * 神造 了許多靈體來服事祂。 有 些靈體背叛 神， 就被稱為“ 惡魔"。 那些繼續服事神的靈體就被稱為“ 天使"
  * Demons and angels are more powerful than humans, but not as powerful as God.
  * 惡魔和 天使是比人類 更 有能力， 但 是他們的能力遠不及神
  * Sometimes demons attack people or try to control them. We call this, "demon-possession."
  * 有時候 惡魔攻擊人 或試圖 控制 他們 。 我們稱之為 “ 被鬼附身"
  * Demons sometimes use their power to make idols or false gods appear to be real.
  * 魔鬼 有時用自己的 力量讓 偶像 或 假神 看起來似乎是 真的
  * Jesus gave his apostles power over the demons.
  * 耶穌給 他的使徒 們有權力 可以勝過惡魔
  * When Jesus returns to earth, he will punish everyone who has rebelled against him, including the demons, Satan, and unbelievers.
  * 當耶穌 再來的時侯， 他將 懲罰 每一個背叛 他 的，包括 惡魔 ，撒旦和 不信 祂的人
  * "Demon" can also be translated as, "evil spirit."
  * 惡魔，也可以 翻譯為 ， 邪靈

The opposite of "demon" is an "angel."

“ 惡魔” 的反面是 “天使 ”

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事 中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-09|[26-09]]]** Many people who had **demons**  in them were brought to Jesus. When Jesus commanded them, the **demons**  came out of the people, and often shouted, "You are the Son of God!"
  * 很多 被鬼 附的人被帶到 了耶穌 面前。 當耶穌 吩咐 惡魔 出來，惡魔 就從這人裏出來 ， 並且常常 喊 叫， “你是 神的兒子 ！ ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-08|[32-08]]]** The **demons**  came out of the man and entered the pigs.
  * 惡魔 就從這人裏出來，進入豬 群裏去
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-05|[47-05]]]** Finally one day when the slave girl started yelling, Paul turned to her and said to the **demon**  that was in her, "In the name of Jesus, come out of her." Right away the **demon**  left her.
  * 有一天 ，當 這使女開始喊 叫， 保羅 轉過身去 ， 向在她 裏面的 惡魔說 ， “ 奉耶穌的名 ， 從她身上出來 。 ” 惡魔就馬上 離開了她。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-02|[49-02]]** He (Jesus) walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out **demons**, raised the dead to life, and turned five loaves of bread and two small fish into enough food for over 5,000 people.
  * 他 （ 耶穌） 在水上行走 ， 平靜 風浪 ， 治好了許多 生病的人 ， 趕 出了 惡魔 ， 使死人復活，並 用五個餅和 兩條小魚 變成足夠的糧食 餵飽超過 5000人
