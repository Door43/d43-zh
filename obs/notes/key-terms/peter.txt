====== Peter 彼得 L ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Peter was one of Jesus' disciples, and then became one of his twelve apostles.

彼得是耶稣门徒中的一个，后来成为他的12个使徒中的一个。

  * Peter was a fisherman, but left everything in order to follow Jesus.
  * 彼得曾是个渔夫，但放弃了一切去跟随耶稣。
  * Peter was very zealous for Jesus. However, when Jesus was arrested, Peter was afraid and denied that he knew Jesus. Peter later repented, and Jesus restored him as a leader in the church.
  * 彼得很热心为耶稣。但是，当耶稣被捕时，彼得害怕了，并否认他认识耶稣。彼得后来悔改了，而且耶稣也恢复了他作为教会的一个领袖。
  * After Jesus went to heaven, Peter became one of the most important leaders of the early church.
  * 耶稣升天后，彼得成了早期教会的最重要的领袖之一。
  * God enabled Peter to do miraculous things so that people would believe his teaching about Jesus.
  * 神使彼得行了很多神迹，这样人们就会相信他关于耶稣的教导。
  * Peter later wrote letters to fellow believers to encourage and teach them, and two of these letters are included in the Bible.
  * 彼得后来写信给众信徒，鼓励并教导他们，并且这些信中有两封信被放在圣经里。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-09|[28-09]]]****Peter**  said to Jesus, "We have left everything and followed you. What will be our reward?"
  * 彼得对耶稣说，“我们已经放弃了一切来跟随你。我们的奖赏是什么呢？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-01|[29-01]]]** One day **Peter**  asked Jesus, "Master, how many times should I forgive my brother when he sins against me? As many as seven times?"
  * 有一天，彼得问耶稣，“主啊，当我弟兄得罪我，我应该饶恕他几次呢？到7次可以吗？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:31-05|[31-05]]]** Then **Peter**  said to Jesus, "Master, if it is you, command me to come to you on the water." Jesus told **Peter**, "Come!"
  * 于是，彼得对耶稣说到，“主啊，如果是你，请叫我从水面上走到你那里。” 耶稣对彼得说，“来把！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:36-01|[36-01]]]** One day, Jesus took three of his disciples, **Peter**, James, and John with him.
  * 有一天，耶稣带着他的三个门徒：彼得、雅各和约翰与他同行。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-09|[38-09]]]****Peter**  replied, "Even if all the others abandon you, I will not!" Then Jesus said to **Peter**, "Satan wants to have all of you, but I have prayed for you, **Peter**, that your faith will not fail. Even so, tonight, before the rooster crows, you will deny that you even know me three times."
  * 彼得回答，“即使所有其他人都背弃了你，我不会！”于是耶稣对彼得说，“撒旦想要夺取你们所有的人，但是我已为你祈求，彼得，叫你不至于失了信心。尽管如此，今晚，在鸡叫之前，你将三次不认我。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-15|[38-15]]]** As the soldiers arrested Jesus, **Peter**  pulled out his sword and cut off the ear of the servant of the high priest.
  * 当士兵逮捕了耶稣时，彼得拔出了他的剑，割掉了大祭司的仆人的一只耳朵。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-11|[43-11]]]****Peter**  answered them, "Every one of you should repent and be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ so that God will forgive your sins."
  * 彼得回答他们，“你们每一个人都应该悔改，并奉耶稣基督的名受洗，这样，神将会赦免你们的罪。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]****Peter**  answered them, "This man stands before you healed by the power of Jesus the Messiah."
  * 彼得回答他们，“这个站在你们面前的人是被耶稣弥赛亚的大能医治好了。"
