====== Satan 撒但 F ======

==== (Satan, devil, evil one) ====

（撒但，魔鬼，那恶者）

===== Facts: =====

事实：

Satan is a spirit being that God created, but he rebelled against God and became God's enemy.

撒但是神造的一个灵，但是他背叛了神而成为神的仇敌。

  * Satan hates God and all that God created, because he wants to take the place of God and be worshiped as God.
  * 撒但恨神和神所造的一切，因为他想要取代神的位置，并象神一样被敬拜。
  * Satan tempts people to rebel against God so that he can become like a king over them.
  * 撒但诱惑人去悖逆神，这样他可以象王一样地去控制人。
  * But God sent the Son of God to rescue people from Satan's control and bring them into God's kingdom.
  * 但是神派神的儿子去拯救人脱离撒但的控制，并把人带进神的国度。
  * Satan is powerful, but God has all power, and one day the Son of God will completely destroy Satan.
  * 撒但能力强大，但神有全能，并且有一天，神的儿子将彻底毁灭撒但“。
  * Satan is also called, "the devil" and, "the evil one."
  * 撒但也被叫做”魔鬼“和”那恶者“。
  * The name "Satan" means "enemy." The word "devil" means "accuser."
  * ”撒但“一名意思是”仇敌“。”魔鬼“一词意思是”控告者”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>圣经故事中的</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>例子</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>：</font>

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-01|[21-01]]]** The snake who deceived Eve was **Satan**. The promise meant that the Messiah who would come would defeat **Satan**  completely.
  * 欺骗夏娃的蛇就是撒但。那应许是指弥赛亚将要来并要彻底打败撒但。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-06|[25-06]]]** Then **Satan**  showed Jesus all the kingdoms of the world and all their glory and said, "I will give you all this if you bow down and worship me."
  * 撒但把世上的万国和万国的荣华都<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>指给耶稣看</font>    ，并说：“你若俯伏拜我，我将赐给你所有的这些“。
  * Jesus did not give in **Satan**'s temptations, so **Satan**  left him
  * 耶稣没有屈服于撒但的试探，于是撒但离开了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]** So Jesus explained, "The seed is the word of God. The path is a person who hears God's word, but does not understand it, and the **devil**  takes the word from him."
  * 于是耶稣解释道，“那种子就是神的话。种子洒落在路边的就是听神的话的人，但是不明白神的话，于是魔鬼夺走了神的话。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-07|[38-07]]]** After Judas took the bread, **Satan**  entered into him.
  * 在犹大吃了饼后，撒但入了他的心。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-04|[48-04]]]** God promised that one of Eve's descendants would crush **Satan's**  head, and **Satan**  would wound his heel. This meant that **Satan**  would kill the Messiah, but God would raise him to life again, and then the Messiah will crush the power of **Satan**  forever.
  * 神应许夏娃的一个后裔将会打破撒但的头，而撒但将伤到他的脚跟。这意思是说，撒但将要杀害弥赛亚，但是神将使他复活，然后弥赛亚将永远毁灭撒但的势力。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-15|[49-15]]]** God has taken you out of **Satan's**  kingdom of darkness and put you into God's kingdom of light.
  * 神已经把你们从撒但的黑暗的国度带出来，迁入神的光明的国度。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-09|[50-09]]]**"The weeds represent the people who belong to the **evil one**. The enemy who planted the weeds represents the **devil**."
  * “那种子代表了属那恶者的人。撒种的仇敌代表魔鬼。“
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-10|[50-10]]]**"When the world ends, the angels will gather together all the people who belong to the **devil**  and throw them into a raging fire, where they will cry and grind their teeth in terrible suffering."
  * ”当世界末了时，天使把所有属魔鬼的人聚集起来，丢到熊熊的火里，在那里他们要在剧烈的痛苦中哀哭切齿。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-15|[50-15]]]** When Jesus returns, he will completely destroy **Satan**  and his kingdom. He will throw **Satan**  into hell where he will burn forever, along with everyone who chose to follow him rather than to obey God.
  * 当耶稣回来时，他将彻底摧毁撒但和他的国度。他将把撒但丢入地狱，连同每个选择跟从撒但不顺服神的人，在那里他们将永远被焚烧。
