====== heal 医治/治病/痊愈/愈合 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To heal is to cause a person's wounds, disease, or disability (such as blindness) to no longer exist. It also means to cause a sick or disabled person to be healthy again.

医治/治病/愈合，就是使人的伤口愈合、疾病或残疾（如失明）不再有。这也是说，使一个生病或残疾的人又恢复了健康。

  * A person who is healed has been made well.
  * 病愈之人，恢复良好。
  * Natural healing happens gradually.
  * 自然愈合，缓慢恢复。
  * Miraculous healing normally occur suddenly, as when Peter caused a crippled man to immediately be able to walk.
  * 奇迹般的愈合常常是突然发生的，正如彼得使一个跛脚的人立即能走路了。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-14|[19-14]]]** One of the miracles happened to Naaman, an enemy commander, who had a horrible skin disease. He had heard of Elisha so he went and asked Elisha to **heal**  him.
  * 这些神迹中有一个发生在乃缦身上，乃缦是敌军的一位元帅，他有着可怕的皮肤病。他听说了以利沙，于是跑去请求以利沙给他治病。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-10|[21-10]]]** He (Isaiah) also predicted that the Messiah would **heal**  sick people and those who could not hear, see, speak, or walk.
  * 他也（以赛亚）预言说，弥赛亚将会医治生病的人，以及那些不能听见、不能看见、不能说话或不能走路的那些人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-06|[26-06]]]** Jesus continued saying, "And during the time of the prophet Elisha, there were many people in Israel with skin diseases. But Elisha did not **heal**  any of them. He only **healed**  the skin disease of Naaman, a commander of Israel's enemies."
  * 耶稣继续说道，“在先知以利沙的时期，在以色列很多人有皮肤病。但是以利沙没有医治他们中的任何人。他只医治以色列的敌军元帅乃缦的皮肤病。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-08|[26-08]]]** They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus **healed**  them.
  * 他们带来了很多生病或残疾的人，包括那些不能看见，不能走路，不能听见，或不能说话的人。耶稣治好了他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-14|[32-14]]]** She had heard that Jesus had **healed**  many sick people and thought, "I'm sure that if I can just touch Jesus' clothes, then I will be **healed**, too!"
  * 她听说耶稣治好了很多病人，就想，“我肯定，如果我只要能摸一下耶稣的衣服，那么我也会得痊愈！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-03|[44-03]]]** Immediately, God **healed**  the lame man, and he began to walk and jump around, and to praise God.
  * 立刻，神治好了那个瘸腿的人，那人就开始走路和跳跃，然后赞美神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]** Peter answered them, "This man stands before you **healed**  by the power of Jesus the Messiah."
  * 彼得回答他们，“这站在你们面前的人藉着耶稣弥赛亚的大能得痊愈了。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-02|[49-02]]]**] Jesus did many miracles that prove he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, **healed**  many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and turned five loaves of bread and two small fish into enough food for over 5,000 people.
  * 耶稣行了很多神迹，证明他就是神。他行在水上，平息了风暴，治愈了很多生病的人，驱魔赶鬼，使死人复活，把五块饼两条鱼变成足够的食物供5000多人吃饱。
