====== believer 信徒/信仰者 ======

===== Definitions & Facts: =====

定义& 事实依据；

A "believer" is a person who trusts completely in a person or an idea.

“信徒”是指完全信奉一个人或一种理念的人。

  * In the Bible, "believer" specifically refers to someone who believes in Jesus.
  * 在圣经里，“信徒”特别是指相信耶稣的那些人。
  * The term "Christian" eventually came to be the main title for believers because it indicates that they believe in Christ.
  * “基督徒”一词，最终成为信徒的主要称谓，是因为这称谓表示他们信奉基督。
  * Other common ways to translate "believer" are, "follower of Jesus" or, "disciple."
  * 其它翻译“beliver（信徒）”的常用的词有，“耶稣的信徒”，或“门徒弟子”。

[参见：相信，基督徒，门徒]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-01|[43-01]]]** After Jesus returned to heaven, the disciples stayed in Jerusalem as Jesus had commanded them to do. The **believers**  there constantly gathered together to pray.
  * 在基督回归天国后，信徒们按照耶稣所命令他们的，就留在耶路撒冷。在那里的信徒们不断地聚集在一起祷告。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-03|[43-03]]]** While the **believers**  were all together, suddenly the house where they were was filled with a sound like a strong wind. Then something that looked like flames of fire appeared over the heads of all the **believers**.
  * 正当信徒们全都聚到一起时，突然他们所在的房屋里充满了一阵阵象强风一样的声音。于是，有如火焰般的东西出现在所有信徒的头上。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** Every day, more people became **believers**.
  * 每一天，越来越多的人成为了信徒。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-06|[45-06]]]** That day many people in Jerusalem started persecuting the followers of Jesus, so the **believers**  fled to other places. But in spite of this, they preached about Jesus everywhere they went.
  * 那天，在耶路撒冷的很多人开始迫害耶稣的信徒，于是信徒们便逃到了其他的地方。但是尽管如此，他们无论到哪里都坚持为耶稣传道。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-01|[46-01]]]** Saul was the young man who guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen. He did not believe in Jesus, so he persecuted the **believers**.
  * 扫罗那时是个年轻人，曾为那些人杀害司提反的人看管他们的长袍 。他不相信耶稣，所以他迫害信徒。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-09|[46-09]]]** Some **believers**  who fled from the persecution in Jerusalem went far away to the city of Antioch and preached about Jesus…It was at Antioch that **believers**  in Jesus were first called "Christians."
  * 从耶路撒冷的迫害中逃出来的一些信徒，去了很远的安提阿城，并为耶稣传道….就是在安提阿，信耶稣的信徒们第一次被称为“基督徒”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-14|[47-14]]]** They also wrote many letters to encourage and teach the **believers**  in the churches.
  * 他们也写了很多信，来鼓励和教导众教会里的信徒们。
