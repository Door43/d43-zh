====== Galilee 加利利 F ======

===== Facts: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>事实:</font>

Galilee is the most northern part of Israel, farther north than Samaria.

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>加利利是以色列最北的地方，比撒玛利亚更靠北。</font>

  * Galilee is bordered on the east by a large lake called "the Sea of Galilee."
  * 加利利<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>接壤于</font>    一个称为<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>“加利利海”的大湖泊的</font>    东面。
  * Jesus grew up and lived in the town of Nazareth in Galilee.
  * 耶稣住在加利利的小镇<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>拿撒勒，长大成人。</font>

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:samaria|Samaria]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:nazareth|Nazareth]]**]
