====== trust ======

信任

==== (trust, to trust) [信任（名词），信任（动词）] ====

===== Definition: =====

定义

To trust someone or something is to believe that they are reliable, honest, true, and dependable.

信任某人或某事就是相信他们是可靠的，诚实的，真实的和可依赖的。

  * Trust is closely related to faith. We trust someone because we have faith that they will do what they promised to do.
  * 信任和信仰有很近的关联。我们信任某人，因为我们有信仰，这个信仰就是使他们做他们承诺要做的。
  * Trusting in Jesus means believing that he is God, believing that his sacrificial death on the cross paid for our sins, and depending on him to save us.
  * 相信耶稣是说相信他是神，相信他在十字架上的牺牲付了我们的罪，并依靠他来拯救我们。
  * Often people foolishly trust in themselves, other people, or things to meet their needs, even though these cannot bring them the good that they desire.
  * 通常人民愚昧地相信自己，其他人，或满足他们需要的事物，尽管这些都不能带给他们所企望的好处。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:faith|faith]]**] [参见：信仰]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they **trusted**  in God and believed that Moses was a prophet of God.
  * 当 以色列人看见埃及人死了，他们信任神，相信摩西是神的先知。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-15|[14-15]]]** Joshua was a good leader because he **trusted**  and obeyed God.
  * 约书亚是一个好的领导者，因为他相信并服从神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a humble and righteous man who **trusted**  and obeyed God.
  * 大卫是个相信并服从神的谦卑的义人
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-06|[34-06]]]** Then Jesus told a story about people who **trusted**  in their own good deeds and despised other people.
  * 于是耶稣讲了一个关于人们凭着他们自己相信的善行和鄙视他人的故事。
