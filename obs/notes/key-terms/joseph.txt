====== Joseph(OT) 约瑟（旧约）P ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Joseph was the eleventh son of Jacob and the first son of his mother Rachel.

约瑟是雅各的第十一个儿子，也是他母亲拉结的第一个儿子。

  * Joseph was his father's favorite son so his brothers were jealous of him; they sold him into slavery.
  * 约瑟是他父亲最喜欢的儿子，所以他的兄弟们都嫉妒他；他们把他卖了去做奴隶。
  * Joseph went through difficult times, including being a slave and a prisoner in Egypt, yet he remained faithful to God.
  * 约瑟经历了很多苦难，包括在埃及做奴隶和囚犯，但他仍坚信神。
  * God brought him to the second highest place of power in Egypt and used him to save Egypt, and his father's family, from starving.
  * 神使他坐在埃及<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>权力</font> 第二高的地位，并使用他把埃及和他父亲家里的人从饥荒中拯救出来。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jacob|Jacob]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]**]

[参见：雅各，埃及]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]****Joseph's**  brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had dreamed that he would be their ruler.
  * 约瑟的兄弟们恨他，因为他们的父亲最爱他，还因为约瑟梦到他将是他兄弟们的统治者。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-04|[08-04]]]** The slave traders took **Joseph**  to Egypt.
  * 奴隶贩子们把约瑟带到了埃及。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-05|[08-05]]]** Even in prison, **Joseph**  remained faithful to God, and God blessed him.
  * 甚至在监狱里，约瑟仍坚信神，而神也祝福他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given **Joseph**  the ability to interpret dreams, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison.
  * 神给了约瑟解释梦的能力，所以法老让人把约瑟从监狱里带到他面前。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-09|[08-09]]]****Joseph**  told the people to store up large amounts of food during the seven years of good harvests.
  * 约瑟告诉人们，在七年丰收期间储存大量的粮食。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-02|[09-02]]]** The Egyptians no longer remembered **Joseph**  and all he had done to help them.
  * 埃及人不再记得约瑟，以及他曾给他们的一切帮助。
