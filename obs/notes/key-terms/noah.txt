====== Noah 挪亚 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事迹：

Noah, his wife, his sons, and their wives are the only people who were saved from the worldwide flood.

挪亚和他的妻子，儿子，儿媳妇是世界性大洪水中唯一得救的人。

  * Noah was a righteous man who obeyed God.
  * 挪亚是一个义人，他顺服神。
  * God told Noah to build a huge boat to protect his family and some of each of the animals from the flood.
  * 神告诉挪亚去建造一艘大船来保护他的家人和各种动物的一部分，来逃避洪水。
  * Noah and his family were saved to repopulate the earth.
  * 挪亚和他的家人蒙拯救后，他们又在地上繁衍后代。
  * Everyone born since the flood is a descendant of Noah's family.
  * 每个在洪水以后出生的人都是挪亚家的后代。
