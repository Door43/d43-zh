====== mercy 怜悯/怜恤/慈爱 L ======

==== (mercy, merciful) ====

（怜悯/怜恤/慈爱，怜悯的/怜恤的/慈爱的）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Mercy is coming to the aid of those in need, especially when they are in a lowly or humbled condition.

怜悯是指帮助那些需要帮助的人，特别是当他们处于低微或卑微状况时。

  * We show mercy when we help someone who is in great need.
  * 当我们帮助了那些十分需要帮助的人时，我们就表现出了怜悯。
  * We show mercy when we have the power to harm someone, but treat him kindly instead.
  * 当我们有能力去伤害某人，却相反仁慈地对待了他，我们就表现出了慈爱。
  * We show mercy when we forgive someone who has done us wrong.
  * 当我们原谅了恶待我们的人，我们就表现出了怜恤。
  * God is merciful to us, and he wants us to be merciful to others.
  * 神对我们是怜恤的，祂也要我们对他人有怜恤之心。

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:19-17|[19-17]]]** He (Jeremiah) sank down into the mud that was in the bottom of the well, but then the king had **mercy**  on him and ordered his servants to pull Jeremiah out of the well before he died.
  * 他（耶利米）沉到井底的泥里，但后来王怜悯他，命令他的仆人在耶利米快死之前，把他从井里拉了出来。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:20-12|[20-12]]]** The Persian Empire was strong but **merciful**  to the people it conquered.
  * 波斯帝国是强大的，但对它所征服的百姓却是慈爱的。

  * 于是，耶稣问那律法师，“你怎么看？这三个人中哪一个是遭抢劫和被殴打的那个人的邻舍呢？”他回答说“那位怜悯他的。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:32-11|[32-11]]]** But Jesus said to him, "No, I want you to go home and tell your friends and family about everything that God has done for you and how he has had **mercy**  on you."
  * 但是耶稣对他说，“不是，我要你回家去，告诉你的朋友和家人，神对你所做的一切，以及他是如何怜恤你的。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:34-09|[34-09]]]**"But the tax collector stood far away from the religious ruler, did not even look up to heaven. Instead, he pounded on his chest and prayed, 'God, please be **merciful**  to me because I am a sinner.”
  * “但是税吏离宗教首领站得很远的，甚至不敢抬头望天。反而，他捶着胸，祷告说，”神啊，请怜悯我吧，因为我是个罪人。“
