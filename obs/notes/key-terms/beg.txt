====== beg 乞求/央求/乞讨 ======

==== (to beg, beggar) （乞求/乞讨/央求，乞丐） ====

===== Definition: 定义： =====

"To beg" is to ask for something earnestly, urgently, and often humbly.

“乞求/央求”就是极力地，急切地，而且通常是卑微地索要某东西。

  * Often people beg when they think that the other person might not want to give them what they ask for.
  * 通常，当人们认为，他们索要某东西而他人不想给时，他们就乞求/央求。
  * "Beg" can also mean to ask for money; a beggar is someone who sits or stands in a public place to ask people for money.
  * “乞求/乞讨”也可以是指要钱；乞讨者（乞丐）是在公共地方坐着或站着向人索要钱的人。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证:

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:10-04|[10-04]]]** God sent frogs all over Egypt. Pharaoh **begged**  Moses to take away the frogs.
  * 神差遣青蛙遍布埃及各地。法老央求摩西把青蛙带走。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-08|[29-08]]]**"The king called the servant and said, 'You wicked servant! I forgave your debt because you **begged**  me."
  * 这王叫了奴仆（过来），说：“你这恶仆！我豁免了你的债，是因为你乞求了我。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-07|[32-07]]]** The demons **begged**  Jesus, "Please do not send us out of this region!" There was a herd of pigs feeding on a nearby hill. So, the demons **begged**  Jesus, "Please send us into the pigs instead!"
  * 恶魔们央求耶稣，“请不要把我们从这地赶走吧”！在附近的山上有一群猪正在吃食。于是，恶魔们央求耶稣，”请把我们赶到猪群里吧“。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-10|[32-10]]]** The man who used to have the demons **begged**  to go along with Jesus.
  * 这个曾经有恶魔（俯身）的人，乞求要去跟随耶稣。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:35-11|[35-11]]]** His father came out and **begged**  him to come and celebrate with them, but he refused."
  * 他的父亲出来，求他来和他们一起庆祝，但是他拒绝了。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-01|[44-01]]]** One day, Peter and John were going to the Temple. As they approached the Temple gate, they saw a crippled man who was **begging**  for money.
  * 一天，彼得和约翰去了神殿。当他们靠近神殿时，他们看见了一个跛脚的人，正在那里讨钱。
