====== God the Father 父神 P ======

==== (God the Father, the Father, heavenly Father) ====

( 父神，父，天父)

===== Facts: =====

事實：

God the Father, together with the Son and the Holy Spirit, is Yahweh, the one true God.

父神，与子和圣灵，合起来就是耶和华，唯一的真神。

  * God exists as the Father, the Holy Spirit, and the Son. Each one is fully God, and yet they are only one God. This is a mystery that mere humans cannot fully understand.
  * 神以圣父， 圣灵， 和圣子（的样式）存在。 每一位都是完全的神 ，但他们是同一位神 。这是一个人类无法完全理解的谜 。
  * The title, "God the Father," expresses the kind of personal relationship that he has to "God the Son." It is not a physical relationship. Rather, it expresses that the Son honors and serves the Father.
  * “ 父神 ”这个称呼表达了一种关乎神和“神的儿子”之间的一种个人关系 。 这不是一种肉体上的关系 ，相反， 它表达了圣子对天父的尊敬和服事。
  * God the Father sends the Holy Spirit to his people.
  * 父神将圣灵<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>赐</font>    给他的百姓。
  * Anyone who believes in the Son becomes a child of God the Father.
  * 所有信圣子的人，就成为父神的儿女。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-09|[24-09]]]** There is only one God. But John heard **God the Father**  speak, and saw Jesus the Son and the Holy Spirit when he baptized Jesus.
  * 世上只有一位神 。但约翰为耶稣受洗时，他听到了父神说话，并看见圣子耶稣和圣灵 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-09|[29-09]]]** Then Jesus said, "This is what my **heavenly Father**  will do to every one of you if you do not forgive your brother from your heart."
  * 耶稣说 ，“ 如果你不从你的内心饶恕你的兄弟，那么我的天父也会同样对待你们每一个人 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-09|[37-09]]]** Then Jesus looked up to heaven and said, "**Father**, thank you for hearing me."
  * 耶稣抬头望天，说：“父啊，我感谢你，因你听见我。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-07|[40-07]]]** Then Jesus cried out, "It is finished! **Father**, I give my spirit into your hands."
  * 耶稣喊道：“ 成了！父啊，我把我的灵魂交在你手里 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]**"So go, make disciples of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of **the Father**, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
  * “所以，去使万民作我的门徒，奉父、子、圣灵的名为他们施洗，凡我所吩咐你们的，都教训他们遵守 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-08|[43-08]]]**"Jesus is now exalted to the right hand of **God the Father**."
  * “ 耶稣现在升到父神的右边 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-10|[50-10]]]**"Then the righteous ones will shine like the sun in the kingdom of **God their Father**."
  * “ 到那时，义人在他们父神的国里将像太阳一样发出光来”。
