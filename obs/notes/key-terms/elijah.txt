====== Elijah 以利亚 f ======

======   ======

===== Facts:神迹实例 =====

Elijah was one of the most important prophets of Yahweh. Elijah prophesied when Ahab was the king of the northern kingdom of Israel

以利亚曾是耶和华的最重要的先知们中的一个。当亚哈是以色列北部王国的王前，以利亚就曾预言了。

  * Elijah confronted Ahab because Ahab encouraged the people to worship false gods.
  * 以利亚怒视亚哈，因为亚哈唆使人敬拜那些假神。
  * God provided food and shelter for Elijah in miraculous ways.
  * 神用各种方法为以利亚提供食物和庇护。
  * God did many miracles through Elijah and showed Israel that Yahweh is the only true God.
  * 神通过以利亚做了很多神迹，并且向以利亚表明耶和华是唯一的真神。
  * Elijah confronted the 450 prophets of Baal and demonstrated that Baal is a false god. As a result, the people in the kingdom of Israel
  * 以利亚怒视巴力的450个先知，并证明了巴力是一个假神。结果就是，以色列王国的人民
  * killed the prophets of Baal and temporarily worshiped only Yahweh.
  * 杀了巴力的这些先知，暂时信奉了唯一的耶和华。
  * Elijah did not die–he was taken up to heaven alive. Elijah and Moses then appeared and talked with Jesus hundreds of years later when Jesus was getting ready to die in Jerusalem.
  * 以利亚没有死 — 他被活着带到了天堂。在耶稣准备好了要死在耶路撒冷的几百年后，以利亚和摩西出现了，和耶稣说了话。
  *

===== Examples from the Bible stories: 来自圣经故事里的例子 =====

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  was a prophet when Ahab was king over the kingdom of Israel.
  * [19-02] 当亚哈是以色列王国的王时，以利亚就是一个先知了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  said to Ahab, "There will be no rain or dew in the kingdom of Israel until I say so."
  * [19-02] 以利亚对亚哈说，“直到我说有雨才会有，否则全以色列王国将不会有雨或雨露”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-03|[19-03]]]** God told **Elijah**  to go to a stream in the wilderness to hide from Ahab who wanted to kill him. Every morning and every evening, birds would bring him bread and meat.
  * [19-03] 神告诉以利亚到荒野之地的溪流里去，躲着想要杀他的亚哈。每天早上和晚上，鸟儿会带给他面包和肉。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-04|[19-04]]]** But they took care of **Elijah**, and God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty.
  * [19-04] 但是他们看顾以利亚，并且神给了他们如此丰富的提供，这样他们的面缸和油瓶就从不会空。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told **Elijah**  to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with Ahab because he was going to send rain again.
  * [19-05] 三年半后，神告诉以利亚去回到以色列王国，和亚哈通话，因为神要在那里再次赐雨。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-07|[19-07]]]** Then **Elijah**  said to the prophets of Baal, "Kill a bull and prepare it as a sacrifice, but do not light the fire."
  * [10-07] 于是以利亚对巴力的先知说，“杀一只公牛，准备用来作为祭祀，但是不要点火”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-12|[19-12]]]** Then **Elijah**  said, "Do not let any of the prophets of Baal escape!"
  * [19-12] 于是以利亚说，“不要让巴力的任何一个先知逃跑掉”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:36-03|[36-03]]]** Then Moses and the prophet **Elijah**  appeared. These men had lived hundreds of years before this. They talked with Jesus about his death that would soon happen in Jerusalem.
  * {36-03] 于是摩西和先知以利亚出现了。在此之前这些人已经活了几百年。他们和耶稣讲到了他不久将要在耶路撒冷之死。
