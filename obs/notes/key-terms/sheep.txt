====== sheep羊 ======

===== Definition: =====

A sheep is a medium-sized animal with four legs that has wool all over its body.

羊是中等尺寸的四肢动物，全身覆盖羊毛。

  * People eat meat from sheep and use their wool to make clothing and other things.
  * 人类吃羊身上的肉，用它们的羊毛做衣服或其它东西。
  * Sheep are very trusting, weak and timid. They are easily influenced to wander away. They need a shepherd to lead them in the right direction and to provide them with food, water and shelter.
  * 羊是非常可信赖的，软弱而胆怯。它们很容易受到惊扰而走失。他们需要一个牧羊人以正确的方向来带领它们，并提供他们食物，水和庇护。
  * In the Bible, people are compared to sheep who have God as their shepherd.
  * 在圣经里，人被比做羊，他们有神做他们的牧羊人。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-12|[09-12]]]** One day while Moses was taking care of his **sheep**, he saw a bush that was on fire.
  * 一天 正当摩西看顾他的羊时，他看见树丛着火了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a shepherd from the town of Bethlehem. At different times while he was watching his father's **sheep**, David had killed both a lion and a bear that had attacked the **sheep**.
  * 大卫曾是来自伯利恒城的一个牧羊人。在他还在不同的时间看顾他父亲的羊群时，大卫就已经杀死了曾攻击羊群的一只狮子和一只熊。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:30-03|[30-03]]]** To Jesus, these people were like **sheep**  without a shepherd.
  * 对耶稣来说，人就象没有牧羊人的羊。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-08|[38-08]]]** Jesus said, "All of you will all abandon me tonight. It is written, 'I will strike the shepherd and all the **sheep**  will be scattered.'"
  * 耶稣说，“你们所有人今晚都将背弃我。圣经记载，”我将击打牧羊人，所有的羊群都将四处离散”。
