====== peace和平/和睦/喜乐 ======

==== (peace, peaceful) ====

（和平/和睦，和平的/和睦的）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Peace is the absence of any conflict, fear, or intimidation.

和平是没有冲突，恐惧或恐吓的。

  * Peace can refer to freedom from war between groups of people.
  * 和平可以指族群部落之间没有战争的自由。
  * To make peace with a group of people is to stop fighting against them.
  * 向一群人求和，就是停止和他们争战。
  * Peace can also refer to a good relationship between people or between people and God.
  * 安乐/和睦也可以指人之间的良好的关系，或人和神之间的喜乐关系。
  * Personal peace refers to a calm state of mind with no anxiety or fear.
  * 个人的喜乐是指思想的平静状态，没有焦躁或恐惧。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-06|[15-06]]]** God had commanded the Israelites not to make a **peace**  treaty with any of the people groups in Canaan.
  * 神曾名命令 以色列人不要和迦南的族群签订和平条约。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-12|[15-12]]]** Then God gave Israel **peace**  along all its borders.
  * 于是，神使色列地界内的人和平相处。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a deliverer who rescued them from their enemies and brought **peace**  to the land.
  * 于是神派遣了一个士师，把他们从敌人那里救了出来，为那块土地带来了和平与安宁。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-13|[21-13]]]** He (Messiah) would die to receive the punishment for other people's sin. His punishment would bring **peace**  between God and people.
  * 他（弥赛亚 ）将用他的死，为着他的子民的罪而受惩罚。他所受的惩罚将为神和子民之间带来和平与安详。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-14|[48-14]]]** David was the king of Israel, but Jesus is the king of the entire universe! He will come again and rule his kingdom with justice and **peace**, forever.
  * 大卫是以色列的王，但耶稣是整个宇宙的王！耶稣将会在来，用正义与和平永远统治着他的国度。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** Jesus will rule his kingdom with **peace**  and justice, and he will be with his people forever.
  * 耶稣用和平与正义统治着他的国度，他将和他的子民永远在一起。
