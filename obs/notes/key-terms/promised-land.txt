====== Promised Land应许之地 ======

==== (Promised Land, Canaan) ====

（应许之地，迦南）

===== Facts: =====

实例：

This was the land that God had promised to give to Abraham and his descendants.

这是神应许给亚伯拉罕和他的后裔之地。

  * After Abraham's descendants had lived in Canaan for many years, there was a famine and they had to move to the country of Egypt. Over 400 years later, God brought them back to Canaan.
  * 亚伯拉罕的后裔们在迦南生活了很多年之后，发生了一次饥荒，他们不得不搬到埃及之地。400年后，神把他们带回迦南。
  * Canaan was also called the "Promised Land" by Abraham's descendants.
  * 迦南也被亚伯拉罕的后裔称作“应许之地”。
  * Because of Israel's disobedience, God took the people out of Canaan for seventy years during the Exile. Then, he brought them back to the Promised land again.
  * 因为以色列的背叛，在流亡七十年后神把百姓带出了迦南。于是，神再次把他们带回应许之地。

[参见：迦南]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-01|[12-01]]]** They (Israelites) were no longer slaves, and they were going to the **Promised Land**!
  * 他们（以色列人）不再是奴隶了，并且他们将前往应许之地！
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-01|[14-01]]]** After God had told the Israelites the laws he wanted them to obey as part of his covenant with them, God began leading them from Mount Sinai toward the **Promised Land**, which was also called Canaan.
  * 在神告诉以色列人他想要他们遵守的律法，以作为和他们立的约之后，神开始带领他们从西奈山前往应许之地，即也被称作迦南地。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** God had promised Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob that he would give the **Promised Land**  to their descendants, but now there were many people groups living there.
  * 神应许亚伯拉罕，以撒和雅各，他将把那块应许之地赐给他们的后裔，但是现在有很多人住在那里。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-14|[14-14]]]** Then God led the people to the edge of the **Promised Land**  again.
  * 于是神再次引导百姓前往那应许之地的边界。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-02|[15-02]]]** The Israelites had to cross the Jordan River to enter into the **Promised Land**.
  * 以色列人不得不跨过约旦河，进入那块应许之地。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-12|[15-12]]]** After this battle, God gave each tribe of Israel its own section of the **Promised Land**.
  * 这次战役之后，神把那块应许之地赐给以色列各个支派，各部落都拥有自己的部分。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** This period of time when God's people were forced to leave the **Promised Land**  is called the Exile.
  * 神的百姓被迫离开那应许之地的那段时期叫做流放。
