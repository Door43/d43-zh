====== promise承诺/应许 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A promise is a vow or commitment to do something.

承诺就是发誓或保证要去做某事。

  * When someone promises to do something, he says that he will definitely do it.
  * 当某人承诺去做某事时，他说他肯定会去做。
  * When someone promises that something will happen, he says that it will definitely happen.
  * 当某人承诺某些事将会发生时，他说那肯定将会发生。
  * Promises are important parts of formal agreements such as covenants.
  * 承诺是正式协议的重要部分，如合约。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:covenant|covenant]]**]

[参见：合约]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-15|[03-15]]]** God said, "I **promise**  I will never again curse the ground because of the evil things people do, or destroy the world by causing a flood, even though people are sinful from the time they are children."
  * 神说，“我承诺（应许）我将不再因为人们做恶事而诅咒土地；或引发洪水来摧毁世界，尽管人从他们的孩童时期就是有罪的”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-16|[03-16]]]** God then made the first rainbow as a sign of his **promise**. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what he **promised**  and so would his people.
  * 神于是造了第一道彩虹作为他的应许的一个标记。每次彩虹出现在空中时，神就会记住他所应许的，他的子民也将记住。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-08|[04-08]]]** God spoke to Abram and **promised**  again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God's **promise**.
  * 神和亚伯拉罕说话，并再次应许，亚伯拉翰将有一个儿子，并且他的子孙如天上的星星一样多。亚伯拉罕相信了神的应许。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]**"Your wife, Sarai, will have a son—he will be the son of **promise**."
  * “你的妻子萨莱，将有一个儿子——他将是应许之子”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-15|[08-15]]]** The covenant **promises**  that God gave to Abraham were passed on to Isaac, then to Jacob, and then to Jacob's twelve sons and their families.
  * 神赐给亚伯拉罕的应许被传给了以撒，然后传给了雅各，然后传给了雅各的十二儿子和他们的家人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-14|[17-14]]]** Though David had been unfaithful to God, God was still faithful to his **promises**.
  * 尽管大卫不忠于神，神还是信实的，<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) text-transform: none; text-indent: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; float: none; display: inline !important; white-space: normal; widows: 1; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;>恪守着</font> 他的应许。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-01|[50-01]]]** Jesus **promised**  he would return at the end of the world. Though he has not yet come back, he will keep his **promise**.
  * 耶稣应许他将在世界末日归来。尽管他尚未归来，但他将<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) text-transform: none; text-indent: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; float: none; display: inline !important; white-space: normal; widows: 1; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;>恪守着他的应许。</font>
