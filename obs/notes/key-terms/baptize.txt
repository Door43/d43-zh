====== baptize 施洗 ======

==== (to baptize, baptism) ====

[施洗，（动词），施洗，（名词）]

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To baptize someone is to ritually wash that person with water. This act is called, "baptism."

施洗某人，就是在仪式上用水洗浴那人。这个行为被叫做”施洗“。

  * Baptism is a way for a person to demonstrate that they repent of their former life, and associate themselves with a certain religious group, teacher, or way of life.
  * 施洗是为一个人声明他悔改了他原来的生命的一种方式，并把他自己和某种宗教族群，某个指导者，或某个生活方式联系在一起。。
  * John (the Baptist) baptized people as a sign of repentance from their sins.
  * 约翰（施洗者）施洗了人，作为人从他们的罪中悔改的一个标志。
  * Christian baptism is a representation of our hearts being cleansed from sin by faith in Jesus Christ, and of our entrance into the Church.
  * 基督徒的洗礼，表明我们的心籍着信服耶稣基督，从罪中被洁净，并表明我们进入了教会。
  * Christians vary in the way they do baptism. Some pour the water on the person, and others put the person completely under the water.
  * 基督徒在他们受洗的方式上有些变动。有些人是把水浇到人身上，其他人是把人完全浸到水里。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:john-baptist|John (the Baptist)]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:repent|repentance]]**]

[参见：约翰（施洗者），悔改]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-03|[24-03]]]** When people heard John's message, many of them repented from their sins, and John **baptized**  them. Many religious leaders also came to be **baptized**  by John, but they did not repent or confess their sins.
  * 当人们听到约翰的信息时，他们中的很多人悔改了他们的罪，而且约翰施洗了他们。很多宗教首领们也来找约翰施洗，但是他们并没有悔改或承认他们的罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-06|[24-06]]]** The next day, Jesus came to be **baptized**  by John.
  * 第二天，耶稣来了，约翰施洗了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-07|[24-07]]]** John said to Jesus, "I am not worthy to **baptize**  you. You should **baptize**  me instead."
  * 约翰对耶稣说，”我不配给你施洗。相反你应该给我施洗。“
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]** So go, make disciples of all people groups by **baptizing**  them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
  * 所以，快去使所有族群部落的人成为信徒，以圣父，圣子和圣灵的名义给他们施洗，并教导他们服从我命令你的一切。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-11|[43-11]]]** Peter answered them, "Every one of you should repent and be **baptized**  in the name of Jesus Christ so that God will forgive your sins."
  * 彼得回答他们：”你们中的每一个人应该以耶稣基督的名义悔改并受洗，这样神会原谅你们的罪“。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-12|[43-12]]]** About 3,000 people believed what Peter said and became disciples of Jesus. They were **baptized**  and became part of the church at Jerusalem.
  * 大约3000千人相信了彼得所说的，并成为了耶稣的信徒。”他们受了洗并成为耶路撒冷教会的一部分。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-11|[45-11]]]** As Philip and the Ethiopian traveled, they came to some water. The Ethiopian said, "Look! There is some water! May I be **baptized**?"
  * 在菲利比和埃塞俄比亚人，他们来到了水边。埃塞俄比亚人说，“看呐！这里有水！我可以受洗吗？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-05|[46-05]]]** Saul immediately was able to see again, and Ananias **baptized**  him.
  * 扫罗立刻有能看见了，亚拿尼亚施洗了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-14|[49-14]]]** Jesus invites you to believe in him and be **baptized**.
  * 耶稣请你们相信他，并受洗。
