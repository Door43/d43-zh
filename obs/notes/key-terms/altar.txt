====== altar 祭坛 P ======

===== Definition: =====

===== 定义： =====

An altar is a raised structure on which sacrifices were burned as gifts, or offerings, to God.

祭坛是一个叠高的结构用来在上面烧祭牲给神作为礼物或祭物。

  * Altars were often made by packing together a mound of earth or by carefully placing stones into a large pile.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>祭坛通常是把泥土堆成一堆或把石头小心的堆成一堆作成的。</font>
  * Some special altars were made of metal, such as gold or bronze.
  * 有些特别的祭坛是用金属造的，例如是金或铜作成的。
  * God told his people to build altars on which to make sacrifices to him, and the nations around Israel also built altars to make sacrifices to their gods.
  * 神叫祂的百姓筑祭坛在上面献祭给祂。在以色列四周的列国也筑祭坛在上面献祭给他们的神。

[参见：祭牲]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-14|[03-14]]]** After Noah got off the boat, he built an **altar**  and sacrificed some of each kind of animal which could be used for a sacrifice.
  * [03-14]挪亚下船后，他筑了一个祭坛，从可以被用于献祭的各种动物中，选了一些献给神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]** When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an **altar**.
  * [05-08]当他们到了献祭的地方，亚伯拉罕就捆绑他的儿子以撒，把他放在一个祭坛上。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the **altar**.
  * [13-09]祭司把动物杀了，并把它烧在祭坛上。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]** He (Gideon) built a new altar dedicated to God near where the **altar**  to the idol used to be and made a sacrifice to God on it.
  * [16-06]他（基甸）在曾经是偶像祭坛的附近，为神筑了一个新的祭坛，并且在上面向神献祭。
