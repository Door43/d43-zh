====== Rahab 喇合 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Rahab was a woman who lived in Jericho when Israel attacked the city. She was a prostitute.

当以色列人进攻耶利哥城时， 喇合是住在耶利哥城的一个女人。她曾是个妓女。

  * Rahab hid the two Israelites who came to spy on Jericho before the Israelites attacked it. She helped the spies escape back to the Israelite camp.
  * 就在以色列人进攻耶利哥城前，喇合把两个前来耶利哥城打探的两个以色列探子藏了起来。她帮助这两个探子逃回到以色列人的阵营中。
  * Rahab became a believer in Yahweh and she and her family were saved when Jericho was destroyed. They came to live with the Israelites.
  * 喇合成了耶和华的信徒。当耶利哥城被摧毁时， 她和她的全家都得救了。她们来到以色列人阵营，和他们住在了一起，

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-01|[15-01]]]** In that city there lived a prostitute named **Rahab**  who hid the spies and later helped them to escape. She did this because she believed God. They promised to protect **Rahab**  and her family when the Israelites would destroy Jericho.
  * 在那城里住着一个妓女，名叫喇哈，藏了（以色列人的）探子，后来帮助他们逃走了。她这么做，是因为她信奉神。他们（两个探子）承诺喇哈，在以色列人即将攻打耶利哥城时，他们会保护她和她的家人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-05|[15-05]]]** The Israelites destroyed everything in the city as God had commanded. **Rahab**  and her family were the only people in the city that they did not kill. They became part of the Israelites.
  * 以色列人按照神所命令的，摧毁了城里的一切。喇哈和她的家人是这城里唯一没有被杀的人。她们成为了以色列人的一部分（百姓）。
