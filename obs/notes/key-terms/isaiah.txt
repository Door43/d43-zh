====== Isaiah以赛亚 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Isaiah was a prophet of God.

以赛亚是神的一位先知。

  * Isaiah wrote many prophecies about Israel that came true while he was still living.
  * 以赛亚活着的时候，写了很多关于以色列的预言，都应验了。
  * Isaiah is especially known for the prophecies he wrote about the Messiah that came true 700 years later when Jesus was living on earth.
  * 以赛亚特别以他写的关于700年后耶稣回到世上的预言而著名。
  * Jesus and his disciples quoted Isaiah's prophecies to teach people about the Messiah.
  * 耶稣和他的信徒们引用以赛亚的预言，来向人们传关于弥赛亚的道。
  * The Book of Isaiah is one of the major books of the Bible.
  * 以赛亚书是圣经里众多书中的一部。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  * 先知以赛亚曾预言，弥赛亚将由一个处女而生。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-10|[21-10]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  said the Messiah would live in Galilee, comfort broken-hearted people, and proclaim freedom to captives and release to prisoners.
  * 先知以赛亚说，弥赛亚将住在加利利，在那里他使受伤的心得安慰，使俘虏得自由，使囚犯得释放。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-11|[21-11]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  also prophesied that the Messiah would be hated without reason and rejected.
  * 先知以赛亚也曾预言，弥赛亚将毫无原因地被人恨，被排斥。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-12|[21-12]]]****Isaiah**  prophesied that people would spit on, mock, and beat the Messiah.
  * 以赛亚预言，人们将会向弥赛亚吐口水，取笑，还打他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** They handed him (Jesus) the scroll of the prophet **Isaiah**  so that he would read from it. Jesus opened up the scroll and read part of it to the people.
  * 他们递给他（耶稣）先知以赛亚的名册，让他读。耶稣打开名册，向人们读了其中的部分。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-08|[45-08]]]** When Philip approached the chariot, he heard the Ethiopian reading from what the prophet **Isaiah**  wrote.
  * 当腓力靠近战车时，他听到了埃塞俄比亚人正读着先知以赛亚所写的书。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip explained to the Ethiopian that **Isaiah**  was writing about Jesus.
  * 腓力向埃塞俄比亚人解释说，以赛亚正在写关于耶稣的书。
