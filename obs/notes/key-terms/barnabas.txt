====== Barnabas 巴拿巴 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Barnabas was one of the early Christians who lived during the time of the apostles.

巴拿巴是早期基督徒中的一位，曾生活在使徒时代。

  * When Saul (Paul) became a Christian, Barnabas encouraged the other believers to accept him as a fellow believer.
  * 当扫罗（保罗）成为基督徒后，巴拿巴鼓励其他的信奉者接受扫罗作为一个团契信徒。
  * Barnabas and Paul traveled together to preach the good news about Jesus in different cities.
  * 巴拿巴和保罗一同赶路，以便在各个城市传关于耶稣的福音。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:paul|Saul (Paul)]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:christian|Christian]]**]

[参见：扫罗（保罗），基督徒]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-08|[46-08]]]** Then a believer named **Barnabas**  took Saul to the apostles and told them how Saul had preached boldly in Damascus.
  * 于是，一个名叫巴拿巴的信徒把扫罗带到众信徒中，告诉他们扫罗是怎样在大马士革大胆传道的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-09|[46-09]]]****Barnabas**  and Saul went there to teach these new believers more about Jesus and to strengthen the church.
  * 巴拿巴和扫罗去了那里，教导这些新的信奉者更多关于耶稣的信息，以稳固教会。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-10|[46-10]]]** One day, while the Christians at Antioch were fasting and praying, the Holy Spirit said to them, "Set apart for me **Barnabas**  and Saul to do the work I have called them to do." So the church in Antioch prayed for **Barnabas**  and Saul and placed their hands on them.
  * 有一天，正当在安提阿的基督徒们正在禁食和祷告时，圣灵对他们说，“为我分派巴拿巴和扫罗，去做我让他们要做的事吧。” 于是，在安提阿的教会为巴拿巴和扫罗祷告，他们把手放在他们的头上（祷告着）。
