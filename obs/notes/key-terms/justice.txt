====== justice 公平 F ======

==== (justice, justly) ====

（公平，公平地）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Justice is right or reasonable behavior or treatment.

公平是正确的或合理的行为或待遇。

  * Laws, actions, and decisions that are just are morally right and fair.
  * 公平的法律，行动和决定，在道德上都是正确的和公平的。

  * To rule justly is to make decisions that are morally right and to treat fairly those people over whom one rules.
  * 公平地统治，就是要做出道德上正确的决策，并要公平地对待被统治的人民。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]** David ruled with **justice**  and faithfulness for many years, and God blessed him.
  * 大卫用公平和信实统治了很多年，神也赐福给他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** Some of these kings (of Judah) were good men who ruled **justly**  and worshiped God.
  * 犹大国的王中有一些是善的，他们公平地统治，并敬拜神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-16|[19-16]]]** They (the prophets) all told the people to stop worshiping idols and to start showing **justice**  and mercy to others.
  * 他们（先知们）都告诉人们，不要再拜偶像，并要向人显出公平和怜悯。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** Jesus will rule his kingdom with peace and **justice**, and he will be with his people forever.
  * 耶稣将用平安和公平统治他的国度，并且他将和他的子民永远在一起。
