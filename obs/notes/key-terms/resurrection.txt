====== resurrection复活 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Resurrection is the act of becoming alive again after having died.

复活是已经死亡以后又活过来的行为。

  * To resurrect someone is to bring that person back to life again. Only God has the power to do this.
  * 使人复活，就是把那人又带回到生命中来。只有神有能力做到。
  * The word "resurrection" often refers to Jesus' coming back to life after he died.
  * “复活” 一词通常是指耶稣死后，又回到生命中来。
  * When Jesus said, "I am the Resurrection and the Life" he meant that he is the source of resurrection, and the one who causes people to come back to life.
  * 当耶稣说，“我就是复活和生命”，他意思是指，他是复活之源，而且他就是那使人得着生命的圣者。
  * Resurrection can be translated as, "coming back to life" or, "becoming alive again after being dead."
  * “复活” 也可以被翻译为，：回到生命中：或者，：“死了之后又活过来了”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-14|[21-14]]]** Through the Messiah's death and **resurrection**, God would accomplish his plan to save sinners and start the New Covenant.
  * 通过弥赛亚的死和复活，神将会完成他的计划，来救赎罪人，开始新约。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-05|[37-05]]]** Jesus replied, "I am the **Resurrection**  and the Life. Whoever believes in me will live, even though he dies.
  * 耶稣回答说，“我就是复活和生命。无论何人，信我者活，尽管他死了。
