====== humble 谦卑 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A person who is humble does not think of himself as better than others.

一个谦卑的人就是不会认为自己会比别人做的更好。

  * Being humble before God means understanding our weakness and imperfection in comparison with his greatness, wisdom and perfection.
  * 在神面前谦卑，就是明了我们自己和神相比之下的软弱和缺陷，神是伟大智慧而完美的神。
  * A truly humble person will obey God.
  * 真正谦卑的人是顺服神。
  * To be humbled means that a person is put in a position of lower status or regard.
  * 一个人要做到谦卑，必须要放低自己的位置和心态。
  * If a person humbles himself, he puts himself in a position of lower importance, out of esteem and respect for others.
  * 一个谦卑的人，

The opposite of being "humble" is to be "proud" or "arrogant."

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a **humble**  and righteous man who trusted and obeyed God.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-10|[34-10]]]**"God will **humble**  everyone who is proud, and he will lift up whoever **humbles**  himself."
