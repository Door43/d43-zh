====== God's law 神的律法 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义

God's law is all of the commands and instructions that he gave to his people Israel.

神的律法是他给他的百姓以色列人一切 的命令和指示。

  * God gave his people many different commands and instructions when he spoke with Moses on Mount Sinai.
  * 当神在西奈山对摩西说话时，他给他的百姓 许多不同的 命令和指令 。
  * The Ten Commandments were part of God's law. They were a concise statement of some of the most important responsibilities of Israel towards God and each other.
  * 十诫 是神的律法的一部分 。它们是 一些 以色列人对神 和对 彼此最 重要的责任 的简要陈述 。
  * These commands deal with everyday things like worship, food, health, family relationships, and war.
  * 这些命令是关于像崇拜， 食品， 健康， 家庭关系 ， 以及战争的日常事务 。
  * The laws that God gave to Israel were an expression of the moral nature of God. They teach us about the holy character of God, as well as the consequences of disobeying him.
  * 神赐给 以色列人的律法是神的道德本性的表达 。他们教我们关于神的神圣 的性格， 以及不服从他 的后果 。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:ten-commandments|Ten Commandments]]**]

【同时看： 十诫 】

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other **laws**  and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these **laws**, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would punish them.
  * 神也给了其他许多法律和规则让人遵行 。 如果人们遵守这些 法律， 神应许他会祝福并保佑他们。 如果他们不服从这些法律，神会惩罚他们 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed **God's law**  could bring an animal to the altar in front of the Tent of Meeting as a sacrifice to God.
  * 人若违背了神的律法，可以带一隻动物来到会幕前面的祭坛， 把动物当作祭物献给神 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai. The people promised to remain faithful to God and follow **his laws**.
  * 约书亚提醒百姓他们有义务服从神在西奈山与以色列人所立的约 。百姓承诺会继续忠于神 ，并遵行他的律法。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-01|[16-01]]]** After Joshua died, the Israelites disobeyed God and did not drive out the rest of the Canaanites or obey **God's laws**.
  * 约书亚去世后， 以色列人 违背了神， 并没有赶出剩下的迦南人或遵守神的律法 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write **his law**  on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins.
  * 在 约 ，神把他的律法写在人心，人们会亲自知道神，他们将是他的人，神会赦免他们的罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** Jesus answered, "What is written in **God's law**?"
  * 耶稣回答说：“ 神的律法是怎么写的 ？ ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** Jesus said to him, "Why do you call me 'good?' There is only one who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have eternal life, obey **God's laws**."
  * 耶稣对他说， “你为什么称呼我‘良善的’ ？ ” 只有一位是良善的的，那 就是神。 但是，如果你想得到永生 ，就遵守神 的律法 。 “
