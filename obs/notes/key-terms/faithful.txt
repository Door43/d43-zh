====== faithful 忠心的 批 ======

==== (faithful, faithfulness) ====

（<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>忠心的，忠心</font>    ）

===== Definition: =====

Being faithful (faithfulness) means being loyal to a person or living according to a set of beliefs.

定义：忠心是忠诚于某人或遵照某一套信仰去生活。

  * A faithful person can be depended on to always keep his promises.
  * 一个忠心的人是可以信赖他总会守住自己的承诺。
  * To be faithful to someone means to be loyal to that person.
  * 对某人有忠心意思是他会忠于那个人。
  * A faithful person remains dedicated to a task, even when it is long and difficult.
  * 尽管是漫长而艰难的，一个忠心的人会始终致力于那项任务。
  * Faithfulness is the consistent practice of doing what we say we will do, or what we believe God wants us to do.
  * 忠心是坚持去做所说过要做的事，或是做我们相信是神要我们做的事。
  * God is faithful to us by keeping His promises to us.
  * 神借着信守他给我们的应许显明他是信实的。
  * We are faithful to God when we persevere in obeying Him.
  * 当我们坚持顺服神的时候，我们对神是忠心的。

The opposite of "faithful" is "unfaithful."

“忠心”的反义词是“不忠”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-05|[08-05]]]** Even in prison, Joseph remained **faithful**  to God, and God blessed him.
  * 即使是在监狱里，约瑟仍然忠心于神，神也祝福他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-12|[14-12]]]** Even so, God was still **faithful**  to His promises to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.
  * 即便如此，神仍然信实的持守他向着亚伯拉罕，以撒和雅各的应许。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** The people promised to remain **faithful**  to God and follow his laws.
  * 百姓承诺一直忠心于神，并遵守他的法律。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]** David ruled with justice and **faithfulness**  for many years, and God blessed him. However, toward the end of his life he sinned terribly against God.
  * 大卫公正信实地统治多年，神也祝福他。然而在晚年的时候，他犯严重的罪得罪神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-04|[18-04]]]** God was angry with Solomon and, as a punishment for Solomon's **unfaithfulness**, he promised to divide the nation of Israel into two kingdoms after Solomon's death.
  * 神向所罗门发怒，作为对所罗门不忠的惩罚，他定意在所罗门死后把以色列国分裂为两个国家。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:35-12|[35-12]]]**"The older son said to his father, 'All these years I have worked **faithfully**  for you!"
  * “大儿子对他的父亲说：‘这些年来我忠心地服侍你！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-17|[49-17]]]** But God is **faithful**  and says that if you confess your sins, he will forgive you.
  * 但神是信实的，并说如果你承认自己的罪，他要赦免你。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-04|[50-04]]]** If you remain **faithful**  to me to the end, then God will save you."
  * 你若一直忠心于我，那么神会拯救你。
