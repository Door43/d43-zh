====== pray祷告 W ======

==== (pray, prayer) ====

（祷告（动词），祷告（名词））

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To pray is to talk to God.

祷告就是向神说话。

  * People often pray to God to ask him to help them or other people.
  * 人经常向神祷告，请求神帮助他们或其他人。
  * People also thank and praise God in their prayers.
  * 人们也在祷告中，感谢、赞美神
  * Praying can also mean confessing our sins to God and asking him to forgive us.
  * 祷告也可指向神承认自己的罪，并请求神赦免我们。
  * When people try to talk to their false god, this is also called "praying.
  * 当人们试着跟他们的假神说话时，这也称作“祷告”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:06-05|[06-05]]]** Isaac **prayed**  for Rebekah, and God allowed her to get pregnant with twins.
  * 以撒为利百加祷告，神就使她怀了双胞胎。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-12|[13-12]]]** But Moses **prayed**  for them, and God listened to his **prayer**  and did not destroy them.
  * 但是摩西为他们祷告，神听了他的祷告，没有毁灭他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-08|[19-08]]]** Then the prophets of Baal **prayed**  to Baal, "Hear us, O Baal!"
  * 于是，巴力的众先知向巴力祷告：“哦，巴力，请听我们说！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-07|[21-07]]]** Priests also **prayed**  to God for the people.
  * 祭司也为百姓向神祷告。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Jesus told his disciples to **pray**  that they would not enter into temptation.
  * 耶稣告诉他的门徒们要祷告，使他们不落入试探当中。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** The disciples continually listened to the teaching of the apostles, spent time together, ate together, and **prayed**  with each other.
  * 门徒们继续听从使徒的教导，彼此交接，一起用饭并祷告。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to **pray**, to study his word, to worship him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
  * 神告诉你们要祷告，要学习他的话语，要和其他的基督徒一起敬拜他，并告诉其他人神为你所成就的一切。
