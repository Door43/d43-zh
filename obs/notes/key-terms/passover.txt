====== Passover 逾越节 L ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

This was a celebration that God wanted the Israelites to do each year in order to remember how God "passed over" the houses of the Israelites and did not kill their sons when he killed the firstborn sons of every Egyptian family.

这是个神让以色列人每年举办一次的
庆祝活动 ，为的是让他们记住，当神杀了每个埃及人的家里的头生子时，神是如何“越”过以色列人的房屋而不杀他们的儿子的。

  * The Passover Feast, or celebration, includes a special meal that reminds people of the meal they ate that night. The meal included a lamb without any defect that they killed, roasted, and shared, as well as bread made without yeast.
  * 逾越节的宴会，或
庆祝活动 ，包括特殊的一顿饭，以此来提醒人们那天晚上他们吃的那一
餐 。那一
餐 包括一只瑕疵的羔羊，他们把它宰杀了，烤熟，并分享，同时还有无酵饼。
  * God told the Israelites to eat this meal every year in order to remember and celebrate how God "passed over" their houses and how he set them free from slavery in Egypt.
  * 神告诉以色列人每年都要吃这顿饭，为的是记住并庆祝神如何“越”过他们的房屋，以及是如何把他们从埃及的奴役中拯救出来的。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-14|[12-14]]]** God commanded the Israelites to remember his victory over the Egyptians and their deliverance from slavery by celebrating the **Passover**  every year.
  * 神吩咐以色列人通过每年庆祝逾越节，来记住神勝过埃及人，以及来纪念以色列人从奴役中被释放。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** Every year, the Jews celebrated the **Passover**. This was a celebration of how God had saved their ancestors from slavery in Egypt many centuries earlier.
  * 犹太人每年庆祝逾越节。就是庆祝神是怎样在很多世纪以前，把他们的祖先从埃及的奴役中拯救出来的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-04|[38-04]]]** Jesus celebrated the **Passover**  with his disciples.
  * 耶稣和他的门徒们庆祝逾越节。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-09|[48-09]]]** When God saw the blood, he passed over their houses and did not kill their firstborn sons. This event is called the **Passover**.
  * 当神看见了血，就越过以色列人的房屋，也没有杀他们的长子。这事件被称作逾越节。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-10|[48-10]]]** Jesus is our **Passover**  Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the **Passover**  celebration.
  * 耶稣是我们的逾越节的羔羊。他是完美无罪的，却在庆祝逾越节的时候被杀害。
