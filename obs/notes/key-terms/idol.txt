====== idol偶像 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

An idol is an object that people make so they can worship it.

偶像是人制作的一个物件，人们可以用来供奉它。

  * People make idols to represent the gods that they worship.
  * 人制作偶像，是为了(用它）代表他们膜拜的神。
  * These false gods do not exist–there is no God besides Yahweh. Therefore the idols have no power.
  * 这些假神并不存在 —— 除了耶和华之外，并无他神。所以偶像是没有权能/权柄的。
  * Sometimes things happen by coincidence that make idols seem to have power. Sometimes demons work through idols in order to deceive people.
  * 有时，发生了巧合，使得偶像看上去似乎有权能。有时妖魔鬼怪通过偶像做法，为的是欺骗人。
  * Idols are often made of precious materials like gold or silver.
  * 偶像通常是用类似金或银的贵重材料做成的。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:god-false|god]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:worship|worship]]**]

[参见：神，敬拜]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-05|[13-05]]]**"Do not make **idols**  or worship them, because I, Yahweh, am a jealous God."
  * “不要制作偶像，不要膜拜它们，因为我，耶和华，是一个嫉(妒的)神。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-12|[13-12]]]** Aaron made a golden **idol**  in the shape of a calf. The people began to wildly worship the **idol**  and make sacrifices to it!
  * 亚伦做了一个牛形的金制偶像。人们开始疯狂地拜这偶像，并且向它献祭。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-03|[14-03]]]**"You must completely destroy all of their **idols**. If you do not obey me, you will worship their **idols**  instead of me."
  * “你必须彻底销毁他们所有的偶像。如果你不服从我，那你将会拜他们的偶像，而不是（拜）我，”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-12|[18-12]]]** All of the kings and most of the people of the kingdom of Israel worshiped **idols**. This kind of worship often included sexual immorality and sometimes even child sacrifice.
  * 以色列国所有的众王和大多数人都拜偶像。这种膜拜通常包括淫乱，甚至有时献童祭。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-16|[19-16]]]** They (the prophets) all told the people to stop worshiping **idols**  and to start showing justice and mercy to others.
  * 他们（先知们）都告诉人们，停止膜拜偶像，（转而）开始向他人传扬正义和怜悯。
