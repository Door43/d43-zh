====== lamb ======

==== (lamb, Lamb of God) ====

羔羊,神的羔羊

===== Definition: =====

定義 :

In the Bible, a lamb is a young sheep or goat. A sheep is a four-legged animal with a thick woolly hair. People raise them for their wool or as a source of food. Sheep are defenseless and timid. Goats are similar animals, and are used mainly for food.
