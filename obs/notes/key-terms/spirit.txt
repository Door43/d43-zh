====== spirit灵/灵魂 ======

==== (spirit, spiritual) ====

（灵，属灵的）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

The spirit is the non-physical part of people which enables them to relate to other people and to relate to God.

灵是人的非物质的那部分，使他们能够和其他人相关联，和神相关联。

  * A person's spirit is the part of him that can believe in God.
  * 一个人的灵（灵魂）是他能够相信神的那部分。
  * Something is "spiritual" if it pertains to this inner nature of a person or if it describes something about God and our relationship to him.
  * 如果某些事是与一个人的内在本质有关，或这事说明了关于神以及我们与神的关系的，这就是“属灵的”。
  * When a person dies, their spirit leaves their body.
  * 当一个人死了，他们的灵魂就离开了他们的身体。
  * God is spirit.
  * 神就是灵。
  * God created other spirit beings including angels, Satan, and demons.
  * 神创造了其它的灵体，包括天使，撒旦和恶魔。
  * Some languages may have different words for the different meanings of the word, "spirit."
  * 一些语言对“灵”这个字可以有不同的字眼对应不同的意思。

The opposite of "spiritual" is "physical."

“属灵的”的反义词是“物质的”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-03|[13-03]]]** Three days later, after the people had prepared themselves **spiritually**, God came down on top of Mount Sinai with thunder, lightning, smoke, and a loud trumpet blast.
  * 三 天后，在人们在属灵上准备好了自己后，伴随着电闪雷鸣，烟火和响亮的号角声，神降临到西奈山山顶，。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-07|[40-07]]]** Then Jesus cried out, "It is finished! Father, I give my **spirit**  into your hands." Then he bowed his head and gave up his **spirit**.
  * 于是耶稣大声喊到，“成了！天父，我把我的灵交到你的手上。”于是他低下了他的头，交出了他的灵魂。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-05|[45-05]]]** As Stephen was dying, he cried out, "Jesus, receive my **spirit**."
  * 当史蒂芬弥留之际，他大声喊到，“耶稣，请接受我的灵魂。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-07|[48-07]]]** All the people groups are blessed through him, because everyone who believes in Jesus is saved from sin, and becomes a **spiritual**  descendant of Abraham.
  * 所有的族裔支派通过他都得了赐福，因为每个相信耶稣的人都从罪中被拯救。
