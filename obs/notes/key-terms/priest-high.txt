====== high priest 大祭司 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义

The high priest was the leader over all of Israel's priests.

大祭司是全以色列祭司们的领袖。

  * The Israelites had many priests, but only one high priest at a time.
  * 以色列曾有很多祭司，但每次只有一位大祭司。
  * The high priest had special responsibilities. He was the only one who was permitted to go into the most holy part of the Temple to offer a special sacrifice once a year.
  * 大祭司有特别的职责。他是唯一的一位被允许进入至圣所的祭司，一年一次献特别的祭。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:priest|priest]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:temple|Temple]]**]

[参见：祭司，圣殿 ]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-08|[13-08]]]** No one could enter the room behind the curtain except the **high priest**, because God lived there.
  * 除了大祭司没有人可以进入幔子后面的房间，因为神在里面。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-07|[21-07]]]** The Messiah who would come would be the perfect **high priest**  who would offer himself as a perfect sacrifice to God.
  * 将要来的弥赛亚将是一位完美的大祭司，他把自己作为完美的祭物奉献给神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-03|[38-03]]]** The Jewish leaders, led by the **high priest**, paid Judas thirty silver coins to betray Jesus.
  * 那些犹太首领们，在大祭司的带领下，给了犹大30块银币，让他（犹大）去背叛耶稣。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-01|[39-01]]]** The soldiers led Jesus to the house of the **high priest**  in order for the **high priest**  to question him.
  * 士兵们把主耶稣带到大祭司的房子，以便于让大祭司审问耶稣。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-03|[39-03]]]** Finally, the **high priest**  looked directly at Jesus and said, "Tell us, are you the Messiah, the Son of the living God?"
  * 最后，大祭司直接看着耶稣说：“告诉我们，你是弥赛亚吗？是那永生神的儿子吗？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-07|[44-07]]]** The next day, the Jewish leaders brought Peter and John to the **high priest**  and the other religious leaders.
  * 第二天，犹太领袖们把彼得和约翰带到大祭司和其他宗教首领那里。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-02|[45-02]]]** So the religious leaders arrested Stephen and brought him to the **high priest**  and the other leaders of the Jews, where more false witnesses lied about Stephen.
  * 所以宗教首领们逮捕了司提反，把他带到大祭司和其他犹太首领那里，在那里很多人做假见证控告司提反。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-01|[46-01]]]** The **high priest**  gave Saul permission to go to the city of Damascus to arrest Christians there and bring them back to Jerusalem.
  * 大祭司给了扫罗许可，让他到大马士革逮捕基督徒，并把他们带回耶路撒冷。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-06|[48-06]]]** Jesus is the Great **High Priest**. Unlike other priests, he offered himself as the only sacrifice that could to take away the sin of all the people in the world. Jesus was the perfect **high priest**  because he took the punishment for every sin that anyone has ever committed。
  * 耶稣是伟大的大祭司，不像其他的祭司，他把自己当作祭物来除去世上所有人的罪。耶稣是完美的大祭司，因为他为人所犯所有的罪接受了惩罚。
