====== Temple ======

神殿

===== Facts: 神迹 =====

The Temple was a building where the Israelites offered sacrifices to God.

神殿是以色列人供奉祭祀神的一所建筑。

  * God referred to the Temple as his dwelling place where the people could come to him.
  * 神把神殿作为他居住的地方，在那里人们可以来找他。
  * Sometimes the term "Temple" refers to the whole Temple complex, including the courtyards that surrounded the main building.
  * 有时术语“神殿”是指整个神殿综合建筑群，包括环绕主建筑的庭院。
  * People came to the Temple building and its courtyards to pray, worship God and bring animals to sacrifice to God.
  * 人们来到神殿建筑和庭院，祷告敬拜神，带动物来祭祀神。
  * The inner room of the Temple building, which had a thick curtain separating it from the rest of the Temple, could not be entered by anyone except the high priest.
  *

神殿建筑的内室，有一个厚的帘子，把内室和神殿其余部分隔开，除了大祭祀，任何人不得入内。

  * The original temple that Solomon built was destroyed by the Babylonians, but was rebuilt after the Exile.
  * 所罗门建造的原来的神殿，被巴比伦人毁坏，在流放后被重新建造。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:17-06|[17-06]]]** David wanted to build a **temple**  where all the Israelites could worship God and offer him sacrifices.
  * 大卫想要建造一座神殿，在那里所有的以色列人都可以敬拜神，供奉祭祀。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:18-02|[18-02]]]** In Jerusalem, Solomon built the **Temple**  for which his father David had planned and gathered materials. Instead of at the Tent of Meeting, people now worshiped God and offered sacrifices to him at the **Temple**. God came and was present in the **Temple**, and he lived there with his people.
  * 在耶路撒冷，所罗门建造了神殿，因为他的父亲大卫曾计划并且已经聚集了材料。取代会幕的是，现在人们在神殿敬拜神，并供奉祭祀，神来到神殿，在那里显现，他和他的人民住在那里。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:20-07|[20-07]]]** They (Babylonians) captured the city of Jerusalem, destroyed the **Temple**, and took away all the treasures.
  * 他们（巴比伦人）占领了耶路撒冷城，毁坏了神殿，拿走了所有的金银财宝。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:20-13|[20-13]]]** When the people arrived in Jerusalem, they rebuilt the **Temple**  and the wall around the city of the city and the **Temple**.
  * 当人们到达耶路撒冷时，他们重建了神殿院墙和城墙。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:25-04|[25-04]]]** Then Satan took Jesus to the highest point on the **Temple**  and said, "If you are the Son of God, throw yourself down, for it is written, 'God will command his angels to carry you so your foot does not hit a stone.'"
  * 于是撒旦把耶稣带到神殿的制高点，说，“如果你是神的儿子，跳下去，因为经上写着，“神将命令他的天使接着你，你的脚不会碰到石头”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:40-07|[40-07]]]** When he died, there was an earthquake and the large curtain that separated the people from the presence of God in the **Temple**  was torn in two, from the top to the bottom.
  * 在他死时，有大地震，神殿中那巨大的把人和神在显现时分隔开的帐幕从上到下裂成两半。
