====== virgin 处女 P ======

===== Definition: 定义： =====

A virgin is a woman who has not had sexual relations.

处女就是一个尚未与人发生性关系的女人。

  * The prophet Isaiah said that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  * 先知以赛亚说，弥赛亚将会从一个处女诞生。
  * Mary was a virgin when she was pregnant with Jesus. He did not have a human father.
  * 当马利亚怀着耶稣时，她还是个处女。耶稣没有一个人类的父亲。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:isaiah|Isaiah]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:mary|Mary]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]

[参考：以赛亚，弥赛亚，马利亚，耶稣]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a **virgin**.
  * 先知以赛亚曾预言说，弥赛亚将会从一个处女诞生。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-04|[22-04]]]** She (Mary) was a **virgin**  and was engaged to be married to a man named Joseph.
  * 她（马利亚）是个处女，已经和一个叫约瑟的男人订了婚。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]** Mary replied, "How can this be, since I am a **virgin**?"
  * 马利亚回答说：“怎么有这样的事？因为我还是个处女。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-01|[49-01]]]** An angel told a **virgin**  named Mary that she would give birth to God's Son. So while she was still a **virgin**, she gave birth to a son and named him Jesus.
  * 一位天使告诉一个名叫马利亚的处女，她将要生的是神的儿子。所以在她还是个处女时，她生了一个儿子，给他取名叫耶稣。
