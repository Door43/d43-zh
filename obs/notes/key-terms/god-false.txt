====== god 神 ======

==== (god, gods) ====

（神，众神）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A god is something that people worship because they believe it has great power to affect their lives.

神是人们崇拜的某种东西 ，因为他们相信它有很大的能力，会影响他们的生活 。

  * When god is written with a small 'g,' it refers to a false god.
  * 神这个字在英文中若用小写的 “g”， 它指的是一个假神。
  * When God is written with a capital 'G,' it refers to the one and only true God.
  * 神这个字在英文中若用大写的 “G ”， 它指的是唯一的真神 。
  * These "gods" do not exist. Yahweh is the only God, and he has no rival.
  * 这些“众神”是不存在的 。耶和华是唯一的神，他没有对手（没有其他的神） 。
  * People sometimes make idols as symbols of their false gods.
  * 人们有时会造偶像作为他们的假神的代表。
  * Demons often deceive people into believing that these false gods and their idols have power.
  * 魔鬼往往欺骗人去相信这些假神和偶像有能力 。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:idol|idol]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:demon|demon]]**]

【同时看：神， 偶像 ，魔鬼 】

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:10-02|[10-02]]]** Through these plagues, God showed Pharaoh that he is more powerful than Pharaoh and all of Egypt's **gods**.
  * 通过这些灾害， 神向法老显明，他比法老和所有埃及的神明更强大 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-04|[13-04]]]** Then God gave them the covenant and said, "I am Yahweh, your God, who saved you from slavery in Egypt. Do not worship other **gods**."
  * 然后，神与他们立约，说：“ 我是耶和华，你们的神，将你们从埃及作奴隶中拯救出来。不要拜别的神 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** They (Canaanites) worshiped false **gods**  and did many evil things.
  * 他们（ 迦南人 ） 崇拜假神 ，并做了许多恶事 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-01|[16-01]]]** The Israelites began to worship the Canaanite **gods**  instead of Yahweh, the true God.
  * 以色列人开始崇拜迦南人的众神，而不是拜耶和华，这位真神 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** But most of Judah's kings were evil, corrupt, and they worshiped idols. Some of the kings even sacrificed their children to false **gods**.
  * 但大部分犹大的王是恶的、腐败的，他们拜偶像 。有些王甚至献自己的孩子给假神 。
