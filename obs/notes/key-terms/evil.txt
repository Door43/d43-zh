====== evil 邪恶 P ======

==== (evil, wicked, bad) （邪恶的，罪恶的，堕落的/腐败的） ====

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Evil is any action, thought or character that is opposed to God's holy character and will.

邪恶是和神的圣洁的性格和意志背道而驰的任一行为、思想或性格。

  * Evil people and evil behaviors can also be described as bad, wicked or immoral. The words "evil" and "wicked" often have a very similar meaning.
  * 邪恶的人和行为也可以被描述为腐败的、罪恶的或不道德的。 “邪恶的”和“罪恶的” 这两个词，通常有着非常近似的意思。
  * The results of evil are clearly shown in how people mistreat others by killing, stealing, slandering or being cruel and unkind.
  * 邪恶所招致的后果，从人们是如何杀害、偷盗、诽谤或冷酷无情和待人刻薄等恶待他人的行为中就显而易见。
  * An evil spirit is a being that rebels against God.
  * 邪灵是悖逆神的一个载体（载物）。

"Evil" is the opposite of "good," and "wicked" is the opposite of "righteous."

"邪恶的”是“良善的”的反义词，“罪恶的”是“正义的”的反义词。

[参见：违背，罪，良善的，正义的/公义的]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:02-04|[02-04]]]**"God just knows that as soon as you eat it, you will be like God and will understand good and **evil**  like he does."
  * 神知道，一旦你吃了（善恶树上的）果子，你就会像他一样能分辨善与恶。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-01|[03-01]]]** After a long time, many people were living in the world. They had become very **wicked**  and violent.
  * 很长时间以后，很多人生活在这世上。他们变得非常邪恶和残暴。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:03-02|[03-02]]]** But Noah found favor with God. He was a righteous man living among **wicked**  people.
  * 但是诺亚蒙了神的恩。他是一个生活在恶人当中的义人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-02|[04-02]]]** God saw that if they all kept working together to do **evil**, they could do many more sinful things.
  * 神看到，如果他们一直都作恶多端，那他们就会做很多更加罪恶的事。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-12|[08-12]]]**"You tried to do **evil**  when you sold me as a slave, but God used the evil for good! "
  * “你们虽然做了一件恶事，把我当奴隶卖掉了，但是神使坏事变成好事！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** They (Canaanites) worshiped false gods and did many **evil**  things.
  * 他们（迦南人）拜假神，且做了很多恶事。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-01|[17-01]]]** But then he (Saul) became a **wicked**  man who did not obey God, so God chose a different man who would one day be king in his place.
  * 但是后来他（扫罗）变成了一个不遵从神的恶人，所以神拣选了另外一个人，有一天那人将会代替扫罗做王。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-11|[18-11]]]** In the new kingdom of Israel, all the kings were **evil**.
  * 在以色列的新王国（北国），所有的国王都是邪恶的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-08|[29-08]]]** The king was so angry that he threw the **wicked**  servant into prison until he could pay back all of his debt.
  * 国王非常震怒，就把那个邪恶的仆人送进了牢狱，直到他能够还清所有欠下的债。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-02|[45-02]]]** They said, "We heard him (Stephen) speak **evil**  things about Moses and God!"
  * 他们说，”我们听到他（司提反）说亵渎诽谤摩西和神的话！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** He (Jesus) will wipe away every tear and there will be no more suffering, sadness, crying, **evil**, pain, or death.
  * 他（耶稣）将擦干每一滴泪水，也不再有痛苦、悲伤、哭泣、邪恶、疼痛或死亡。
