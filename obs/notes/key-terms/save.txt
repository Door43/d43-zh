====== save拯救/解救 ======

==== (save, salvation, saved) ====

（拯救/解救，救恩，被拯救/解救）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To save someone or something is to rescue that person or thing from something bad.

拯救/解救某人或某事，就是从不好的坏事中营救/搭救某人或挽救某事。

  * In a physical sense, people can be saved from harm, danger, or death.
  * 在实际意义上，人可以从伤害，危险或死亡中被抢救。
  * In a spiritual sense, if a person has been "saved," it means that through Jesus' death on the cross, God has forgiven him and rescued him from being punished for his sin.
  * 在属灵意义上，如果一个人已经“被救赎”，这意思是说，通过耶稣的十字架上的死，神原谅了他，把他因着他的罪从被惩罚中拯救出来。
  * Only God can save people from being punished for their sin.
  * 只有神可以因着人的罪，拯救人不受惩罚。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:punish|punish]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:cross|cross]]**]

[参见：罪，惩罚，十字架]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-08|[09-08]]]** Moses tried to **save**  his fellow Israelite.
  * 摩西试图拯救他的同胞以色列人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to **save**  the firstborn son of anyone who believed in him.
  * 神给任何一个相信他的人一个办法，来拯救他们头生的儿子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** Moses told the Israelites, "Stop being afraid! God will fight for you today and **save**  you."
  * 摩西告诉以色列人，“不要害怕！神今天会为你而战，并拯救你。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-13|[12-13]]]** The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to praise God because he **saved**  them from the Egyptian army.
  * 以色列人唱了很多歌，来庆祝他们的新的自由，并赞美神，因为他拯救了他们远离以色列军队。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** This pattern repeated many times: the Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to **save**  them.
  * 这个形式重复了很多次：以色列人会犯罪，神将会惩罚他们，他们也会忏悔，并且神会派遣一个拯救者去拯救他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]**"You crucified Jesus, but God raised him to life again! You rejected him, but there is no other way to be **saved**  except through the power of Jesus!"
  * 你钉死了耶稣，但是神使他升天复活！你拒绝了他，但是除了耶稣的大能以外，别无拯救”！
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-11|[47-11]]]**] The jailer trembled as he came to Paul and Silas and asked, "What must I do to be **saved**?" Paul answered, "Believe in Jesus, the Master, and you and your family will be **saved**."
  * 狱卒战战兢兢地来到保罗和西拉的面前，问到，“我必须怎么做，方能得拯救？”保罗回答，“相信耶稣，相信主，你和你的家人将被拯救”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-12|[49-12]]]** Good works cannot **save**  you.
  * 善行不能救你。/ 没有人因为善行而得救。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-13|[49-13]]]** God will **save**  everyone who believes in Jesus and receives him as their Master. But he will not **save**  anyone who does not believe in him.
  * 神将拯救每一个信耶稣并接受他做他的主人的人。但是他不会拯救任何一个不信他的人。
