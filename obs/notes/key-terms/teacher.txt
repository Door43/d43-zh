====== teacher 老师/教师/教导者/指导者 ======

==== (teacher, Teacher) ====

==== （老师/教师，教导者/指导者） ====

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A teacher is a person who instructs other people. Teachers help others to obtain and use both knowledge and skills.

老师是指导其他人的人。老师帮助人们获得并能使用知识和技能。。

  * The word "teacher" is used in a special sense in the Bible for someone who teaches about God, rather than for a school teacher for children.
  * “Teacher（教导者/指导者）”，这个字眼以特殊的意义用在圣经中，是指教导关于神的人，并不是教孩子上课的学校老师。
  * The people that learn from a teacher are called "students." People who learn spiritual truth from a teacher are also called "disciples."
  * 向老师学习的人被称作“学生”。向老师学习灵的道的人被称作“门徒”。
  * In these stories, the word "teacher" is capitalized as, "Teacher" when it is used as a title for Jesus.
  * 在这些（圣经）故事中，字眼“teacher”要大写成 “Teacher"，用作对耶稣的称谓。。
  * People called Jesus, "Teacher" as a title of respect because he taught them about God.
  * 人们称呼耶稣“老师”，作为尊称，因为他教导关于神的道。
  * Some cultures have other words that they use to show respect to those who teach about God, such as, "Sir" or, "Rabbi" or, "Preacher."
  * 某些文化有其他的字用来表示对那些教导关于神的人尊敬，如“先生”，或“拉比”或“传道者”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** One day, an expert in the Jewish law came to Jesus to test him, saying, "**Teacher**, what must I do to inherit eternal life?"
  * 一天，一个犹太律法的博士来到耶稣面前测试耶稣，说，“老师（夫子），我怎样做才能得到永生”？
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** One day a rich young ruler came up to Jesus and asked him, "Good **Teacher**, what must I do to have eternal life?"
  * 一天，一个富有的年轻的统治者来到耶稣面前，问他，“良善的老师（父子），我必须怎样做才能得到永生“？
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-02|[37-02]]]** After the two days had passed, Jesus said to his disciples, "Let's go back to Judea." "But **Teacher**," the disciples answered, "Just a short time ago the people there wanted to kill you!"
  * 过了两天，耶稣告诉他的门徒，"让我们回到犹太地”。"，老师“，门徒回答说，”就在不久前，那儿的人们还想杀了你”！
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-14|[38-14]]]** Judas came to Jesus and said, "Greetings, **Teacher**," and kissed him.
  * 犹大来到耶稣跟前，说，“你好， 老师，”并亲吻了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-03|[49-03]]]** Jesus was also a great **teacher**, and he spoke with authority because he is the Son of God.
  * 耶稣也是个伟大的老师，并且和有关当局说话，因为他是神的儿子。
