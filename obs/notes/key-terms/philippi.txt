====== Philippi腓力比 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Philippi was a city located in the northern part of ancient Greece.

腓力比是个坐落在古老的希腊北部的一个城市。

  * Paul and Silas traveled to Philippi to preach about Jesus to the people there.
  * 保罗和西拉来到腓力比。在那儿向人们传关于耶稣的道。
  * Paul and Silas were arrested in Philippi, but God miraculously freed them.
  * 保罗和西拉在腓力比被逮捕，但是神奇迹般地释放了他们。
  * Paul wrote the letter to the Philippians in the Bible to the Christians in Philippi.
  * 保罗写了信给圣经中的腓力比人和腓力比的基督徒们。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:paul|Paul]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:silas|Silas]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:christian|Christian]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** One day, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of **Philippi**  to proclaim the good news about Jesus.
  * 一天，保罗和他的朋友西拉去了腓力比城，传播关于耶稣的福音。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-13|[47-13]]]** The next day the leaders of the city released Paul and Silas from prison and asked them to leave **Philippi**.
  * 第二天，城里的首领们从监牢里释放了保罗和西拉，并请他们离开腓力比。
