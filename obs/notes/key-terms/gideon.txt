====== Gideon 基甸 F ======

===== Facts: =====

事实：

Gideon was an Israelite man whom God raised up to deliver Israel from their enemies.

基甸是<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>神兴起的一个</font>    以色列人，来<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>拯救以色列人脱离</font>    他们的敌人。

  * Gideon lived during the time, many years after the Israelites moved into Canaan, when a group of people called the Midianites kept attacking them.
  * 基甸活在以色列人迁入迦南地很多年后，那时被称为米甸人的一群人， 不断攻打他们（以色列人） 。
  * Gideon was very fearful of the Midianites, but God used him to lead the Israelites to fight against the Midianites and defeat them.
  * 基甸很害怕米甸人， 但神用他带领以色列人与米甸人争战，并击败他们。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子 :

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-05|[16-05]]]** The angel of Yahweh came to **Gideon**  and said, "God is with you, mighty warrior. Go and save Israel from the Midianites."
  * 耶和华的使者对他说“神与你同在，大能的勇士。去拯救以色列人脱离米甸人。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]****Gideon's**  father had an altar dedicated to an idol. God told **Gideon**  to tear down that altar.
  * 基甸的父亲筑了一个拜偶像的祭坛。神告诉基甸拆毁那个祭坛 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]** There were so many of them (Midianites) that they could not be counted. **Gideon**  called the Israelites together to fight them.
  * 有很多米甸人，他们多到数不清 。 基甸号召以色列人一起去攻打他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]****Gideon**  called the Israelites together to fight them. **Gideon**  asked God for two signs so he could be sure that God would use him to save Israel.
  * 基甸号召以色列人一起去攻打他们（米甸人）。 基甸向神祈求两个印证，这样他可以确信是神用他去拯救以色列（人） 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-10|[16-10]]]** 32,000 Israelite soldiers came to **Gideon**, but God told him this was too many.
  * 32000名以色列士兵来到基甸那里 ，但神告诉他，这太多人了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-12|[16-12]]]** Then **Gideon**  returned to his soldiers and gave each of them a horn, a clay pot, and a torch.
  * 于是，基甸回到了他的士兵那里，给他们每个人一只号角 ， 一个陶壶和一把火炬 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-15|[16-15]]]** The people wanted to make **Gideon**  their king.
  * 人们想立基甸作他们的王 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-16|[16-16]]]** Then **Gideon**  used the gold to make a special garment like the high priest used to wear. But the people started worshiping it as if it were an idol.
  * 于是，基甸用金子做了一件特殊的服装，像大祭司所穿的那样，但是人们开始把它当做一个偶像来拜 。
