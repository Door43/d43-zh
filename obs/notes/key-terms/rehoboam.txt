====== Rehoboam 罗波安 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Rehoboam was one of the sons of King Solomon, and became the king of the nation of Israel after Solomon died.

罗波安是所罗门王众多儿子们中的一个，在所罗门死后，他成为了以色列国的王。

  * When Rehoboam spoke too severely to the people of Israel, ten of the tribes rebelled against him. Because of this, the nation of Israel became two kingdoms: the kingdom of Israel in the north and the kingdom of Judah in the south.
  * 当罗波安和以色列人言词激烈地说话时，当地的部落中的十个人反叛了他。因此，以色列国分裂成了两个国：北部的以色列国和南部的犹大国。
  * Rehoboam continued to be the king of the kingdom of Judah, which consisted of the two tribes who remained loyal to him.
  * 罗波安继续做犹大国的王，这犹大国由两个部落组成，仍然效忠于罗波安。
  * Rehoboam was a wicked king who did not obey Yahweh, but served false gods.
  * 罗波安是个昏君，他不遵从耶和华，而信奉假神。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-05|[18-05]]]** After Solomon died, his son, **Rehoboam**, became king. **Rehoboam**  was a foolish man.
  * 所罗门死后，他的儿子罗波安继承王位。罗波安是个愚蠢的人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-06|[18-06]]]****Rehoboam**  answered foolishly and told them, "You thought my father Solomon made you work hard, but I will make you work harder than he did, and I will punish you more harshly than he did."
  * 罗波安愚蠢地答应了，并告诉他们，“你们认为我父所罗门使你负重轭（工作辛苦），而我将使你负更重的轭（工作更辛苦），比他更严厉地惩罚你们"。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-07|[18-07]]]** Ten of the tribes of the nation of Israel rebelled against **Rehoboam**. Only two tribes remained faithful to him.
  * 以色列国的十个部落起来反抗罗波安。只有两个部落仍忠实于他。
