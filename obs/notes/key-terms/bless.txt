====== bless赐福/祝福 ======

==== (bless, blessed, blessing) ====

赐福，被保佑，祝福）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Tsomeone or something is to state a desire for positive and beneficial things to happen to them.o "bless"

“保守”某人或某事，就是说出一个愿望，希望积极和有益的事情发生在这些人身上。

  * To cause beneficial things to happen to someone or something.
  * 使有益的事情发生在某人或某事上。
  * A father would often pronounce a formal blessing on his children.
  * 一个父亲会经常宣布一个正式的祝福给他的孩子们。
  * When God blesses something he causes it to be fruitful and to have a positive effect on others.
  * 当神保守某事时，他使这保守富有成效，并对其他人具有积极的影响。

The opposite of "bless" is "curse."

“”赐福“的反义词是”诅咒“。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:curse|curse]]**]

[参见：诅咒]
