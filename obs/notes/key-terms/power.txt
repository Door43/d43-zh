====== power能力/力量/权势/权柄 ======

===== Definition: =====

===== 定义： =====

The word "power" can refer to the ability to do things or make things happen, physical strength, or authority to rule.

“Power‘ （能力/力量/权势/权柄）一词，可以指做事情的能力，或使事情发生的权力，身体强壮的力量，或统治的权柄。

  * The "power of God" refers to God's ability to do things that are not possible for people to do.
  * “神的权能”指神做工的大能，而非人之所能为。
  * God is the source of all power–his power is never limited.
  * 神是所有力量的源泉 —— 他的力量永远不受限制的。
  * God is the highest authority, and has complete power over everything that he has created.
  * 神是至高权柄，对他所造的万物拥有完全的权能。
  * God gives his people power to do what he wants, so that when they heal people or do other miracles, they do this by the power of God.
  * 神赐力量给他的子民去做神想要（他们）做的的事，这样，当他们给人治病或做其它的神迹时，他们是藉着神的权能做到的。
  * Because Jesus and the Holy Spirit are also God, they have and use this same power.
  * 因为耶稣和圣灵也都是神，所以他们拥有并使用这相同的权能。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:miracle|miracle]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]**]

[参见：神迹，耶稣，圣灵]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]** The angel explained, "The Holy Spirit will come to you, and the **power**  of God will overshadow you. So the baby will be holy, the Son of God."
  * 天使解释说，“圣灵将降临到你身上，而且神的大能将荫庇你。所以这婴孩将是圣洁的神的儿子。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-01|[26-01]]]** After overcoming Satan's temptations, Jesus returned in the **power**  of the Holy Spirit to the region of Galilee where he lived.
  * 胜过撒旦的试探之后，满有圣灵的能力，耶稣回到了他曾居住的地方加利利。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-15|[32-15]]]** Immediately Jesus realized that **power**  had gone out from him.
  * 耶稣马上意识到，有能力从他身体里出去了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-11|[42-11]]]** Forty days after Jesus rose from the dead, he told his disciples, "Stay in Jerusalem until my Father gives you **power**  when the Holy Spirit comes on you."
  * 耶稣死里复活四十天后，他告诉他的信徒们，“呆在耶路撒冷，直到我的天父赐你能力，圣灵来到你面前。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-06|[43-06]]]**"Men of Israel, Jesus was a man who did many mighty signs and wonders by the **power**  of God, as you have seen and already know."
  * “以色列的人呐，耶稣就是那用神的大能行了很多异能、奇事和神迹的人啊，正如你们所看到的也知道的。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]** Peter answered them, "This man stands before you healed by the **power**  of Jesus the Messiah."
  * 彼得回答他们，“站在你前面的人，是籍着耶稣弥赛亚的大能被医治好的。”
