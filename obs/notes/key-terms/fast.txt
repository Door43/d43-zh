====== fast 禁食 F ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To fast is to stop eating food, or certain foods and drinks, for a period of time, such as for a day or more.

禁食是指在一段时间里（例如一天或更长）不吃食物，或者是某种食品和饮品。

  * Fasting can help people to focus on God and pray without being distracted by food.
  * 禁食能帮助人专注于神和祷告上，不为食物分心。
  * Jesus condemned the Jewish religious leaders for fasting for the wrong reasons. They fasted so that others would think they were very religious people.
  * 耶稣责备犹太宗教领袖们，因他们为错误的原因禁食。他们禁食是为了让人觉得他们很虔诚。
  * Sometimes people fast in order to show that they are very sad or grieved about something.
  * 有时候人禁食是为了表明他们为着某事非常难过或悲伤。

[参见 ： 犹太领袖]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Immediately after Jesus was baptized, the Holy Spirit led him out into the wilderness, where he **fasted**  for forty days and forty nights.
  * 耶稣受洗后，圣灵立即带他到旷野，他在那里禁食四十昼夜 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-08|[34-08]]]**"'For example, I **fast**  two times every week and I give you ten percent of all the money and goods that I receive.'"
  * “例如说我每周禁食两次，所得的财物我都奉献十分之一给你。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-10|[46-10]]]** One day, while the Christians at Antioch were **fasting**  and praying, the Holy Spirit said to them, "Set apart for me Barnabas and Saul to do the work I have called them to do."
  * 有一天，安提阿的基督徒禁食祷告时，圣灵对他们说， “要为我分派巴拿巴和扫罗，去做我召他们所做的工。”
