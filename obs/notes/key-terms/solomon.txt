====== Solomon 所罗门 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Solomon was the son of King David; his mother was Bathsheba.

所罗门是大卫王的儿子；他的母亲是拔示巴。

  * When Solomon became king, God told him to ask for anything he wanted. So Solomon asked for wisdom to rule the people justly and well. God was pleased with Solomon's request and gave him both wisdom and much wealth.
  * 当所罗门成为王时，神告诉他去求他想要的任何东西。所以所罗门求智慧，使他可以公正妥善地统治管理百姓。神喜悦所罗门所求的，并将智慧和许多财富赐给他。
  * Solomon built the Temple for God in Jerusalem that his father, David, had planned to build.
  * 所罗门为神在耶路撒冷建造了圣殿，乃是他的父亲大卫计划要建造的。
  * Although Solomon ruled wisely in the beginning years of his reign, later on he foolishly married many foreign women and started worshiping their gods.
  * 尽管所罗门在他在位的初期是个明智的统治者，但后来他愚蠢地娶了很多外邦女子，并开始拜她们的神。
  * Because of Solomon's unfaithfulness, after his death, God divided the Israelites into two kingdoms that often fought against each other.
  * 因为所罗门的不忠，他死后，神把以色列人分裂成为两个国。这两个国之间经常有战争。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-14|[17-14]]]** Later, David and Bathsheba had another son, and they named him **Solomon**.
  * 后来，大卫和拔示巴又生了一个儿子，他们给他取名叫所罗门。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-01|[18-01]]]** After many years, David died, and his son **Solomon**  began to rule. God spoke to **Solomon**  and asked him what he wanted most. When **Solomon**  asked for wisdom, God was pleased and made him the wisest man in the world. **Solomon**  learned many things and was a very wise judge. God also made him very wealthy.
  * 很多年以后，大卫死了，他的儿子所罗门接续作王治理。神向所罗门说话，问他最想要什么。当所罗门求要智慧时，神很喜悦，让他成了世上最有智慧的人。所罗门学到了很多东西，并是一个很有智慧的审判官。神也使他很富足。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-02|[18-02]]]** In Jerusalem, **Solomon**  built the Temple for which his father David had planned and gathered material.
  * 在耶路撒冷，所罗门建造了圣殿，为了这圣殿他的父亲大卫曾筹划并收集了材料。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-03|[18-03]]]** But **Solomon**  loved women from other countries. … When **Solomon**  was old, he also worshiped their gods.
  * 但是所罗门喜欢来自其它不同国家的女人…..在所罗门晚年时，他也拜她们的神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-04|[18-04]]]** God was angry with **Solomon**  and, as a punishment for **Solomon's**  unfaithfulness, he promised to divide the nation of Israel into two kingdoms after **Solomon's**  death.
  * 神向所罗门发怒，作为对所罗门不忠的惩罚，神应许在所罗门死后把以色列国分裂成两个国。
