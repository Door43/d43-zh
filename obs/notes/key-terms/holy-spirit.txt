====== Holy Spirit 圣灵 F ======

==== (Holy Spirit, God's Spirit, Spirit of God, My Spirit) ====

（圣灵 ， 神的灵，神的灵，我的灵）

===== Facts: =====

事实：

The Holy Spirit is God. The one true God exists eternally as the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. All three are equal in every way.

圣灵就是神。唯一真神是以圣父，圣子，圣灵三位一体的方式永远存在。 这三位在各方面都是同等的。

  * Because the Holy Spirit is God, the Holy Spirit is absolutely holy, infinitely pure, and morally perfect in all his nature and in everything he does.
  * 因为圣灵就是神，所以圣灵的本性和他一切的作为都是绝对圣洁，全然纯洁和道德完美的。
  * The Holy Spirit was active, along with the Father and the Son, in creating the world.
  * 圣灵与圣父、圣子一同创造世界。
  * In the past the Holy Spirit sometimes came to people briefly to give them a special message or to enable them to do great things.
  * 从前，圣灵有时会短暂的去带给人一个特殊的信息，或者给人能力使他们能做大事。
  * When God's Son, Jesus, returned to heaven, God sent the Holy Spirit to his people to lead them, teach them, comfort them and give them the ability to do God's will.
  * 神的儿子耶稣返回天堂以后，神就派遣圣灵临到他的百姓来带领，教导，安慰他们，并赐给他们能力来行神的旨意。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:01-01|[01-01]]]** But **God's Spirit**  was there over the water.
  * 但神的灵在水面上运行。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-08|[24-08]]]** When Jesus came up out of the water after being baptized, **the Spirit of God**  appeared in the form of a dove and came down and rested on him.
  * 当耶稣受洗后从水里出来，神的灵像鸽子一样显现并降临在他身上。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-01|[26-01]]]** After overcoming Satan's temptations, Jesus returned in the power of **the Holy Spirit**  to the region of Galilee where he lived.
  * 胜过撒旦的试探后，耶稣带着圣灵的能力回到他所居住的加利利地区。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-03|[26-03]]]** Jesus read, "God has given me **his Spirit**  so that I can proclaim good news to the poor, freedom to captives, recovery of sight for the blind, and release to the oppressed."
  * 耶稣读说：“神的灵在我身上，使我传福音给贫穷的人，宣告被虏的得释放，瞎眼的可以看见，受压制的可以得自由。"
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]**"So go, make disciples of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and **the Holy Spirit**  and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
  * “所以去，使万民作我的门徒，奉父，子，圣灵的名给他们施洗，我吩咐你们的一切，都要教导他们遵守。"
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-03|[43-03]]]** They were all filled with the **Holy Spirit**  and they began to speak in other languages.
  * 他们都被圣灵充满，开始说另一种语言。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-08|[43-08]]]**"And Jesus has sent the **Holy Spirit**  just as he promised he would do. The **Holy Spirit**  is causing the things that you are are now seeing and hearing."
  * “耶稣已照祂所应许的差遣了圣灵来。你们现在所见所闻的事都是这位圣灵所作成的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:43-11|[43-11]]]** Peter answered them, "Every one of you should repent and be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ so that God will forgive your sins. Then he will also give you the gift of the **Holy Spirit**.“
  * 彼得回答他们说：“你们每一个人都要悔改，并奉耶稣基督的名义受洗，这样神就会赦免你们的罪，也会赐圣灵给你们。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-01|[45-01]]]** He (Stephen) had a good reputation and was full of the **Holy Spirit**  and of wisdom.
  * 他（司提反）有好名声，并且满有圣灵和智慧。
