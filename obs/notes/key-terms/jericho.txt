====== Jericho 耶利哥P ======

===== Facts: =====

实情 ：

Jericho was a powerful city in the Promised Land of Canaan.

耶利哥是一座位于迦南应许之地的坚固城。

  * This was the first city in the land of Canaan that God told the Israelites to conquer.
  * 这是神吩咐以色列人去征服的第一座位于迦南地的城市。
  * Like all the Canaanites, the people of Jericho worshiped false idols.
  * 像所有的迦南人一样，耶利哥的人也拜许多的偶像。
  * When Joshua led the Israelites against Jericho, God did a great miracle to help them defeat the city.
  * 当约书亚率领以色列人去攻打耶利哥的时候，神行了一个极大的神迹来帮助他们攻取这座城市。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子 ：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-01|[15-01]]]** Joshua sent two spies to the Canaanite city of **Jericho**.
  * 【15－01】约书亚派了两个探子去迦南地的耶利哥城。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-03|[15-03]]]** After the people crossed the Jordan River, God told Joshua how to attack the powerful city of **Jericho**.
  * 【15－03】当百姓过了约旦河后，神告诉约书亚如何攻取坚固的耶利哥城。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:15-05|[15-05]]]** Then the walls around **Jericho**  fell down! The Israelites destroyed everything in the city as God had commanded.
  * 【15－05】然后围绕耶利哥的城墙全都倒下了！ 以色列人就照着神的命令把这座城里所有的东西都全部摧毁了。
