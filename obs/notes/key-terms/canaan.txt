====== Canaan 迦南 ======

==== (Canaan, Promised Land) ====

（迦南，应许之地）

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

This was the name of the land in the Middle East from the valley of the Jordan River to the Mediterranean Sea.

这是个中东的一个地名，位于约旦河河谷靠着地中海的地方，

  * God promised to give this land to Abraham and his descendants, the Israelites.
  * 神应许把这块地。赐给亚伯拉罕和他的后裔们—–以色列人。
  * Today the countries and territories in that land are Israel, Lebanon, part of Jordan, part of Syria, and Palestine.
  * 今天在那块应许之地的国家和领土有，以色列，黎巴嫩，部分约旦，部分叙利亚，和巴勒斯坦。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-05|[04-05]]]** He (Abram) took his wife, Sarai, together with all his servants and everything he owned and went to the land God showed him, the land of **Canaan**.

  * 当亚伯兰到了迦南，神说，“你举目看看周围。我将赐给你和你的后代所有你能看到的土地，作为你们（继承）的产业。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-09|[04-09]]]**"I give the land of **Canaan**  to your descendants."
  * “我把迦南的土地赐给你的后代。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-03|[05-03]]]**"I will give you and your descendants the land of **Canaan**  as their possession and I will be their God forever."
  * “我将赐给你和你的后代迦南地，作为他们的属地，我将永远是他们的神。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in **Canaan**, Jacob returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  * 在他们阔别在迦南的家乡二十年后，雅各带领他的家人，奴仆，及所有他的牲畜，一起回到了那里。
