====== Judas 犹大 L ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Judas was one of Jesus' twelve apostles. However, he betrayed Jesus to the Jewish leaders.

犹大是耶稣十二位使徒中的一个。然而，他把耶稣出卖给了犹太首领。

  * Judas managed the apostles' money, but he stole some of it for himself.
  * 犹大管着使徒们的钱，但是他会偷一些钱据为己有。
  * Judas betrayed Jesus by telling the religious leaders where Jesus was so they could arrest him.
  * 犹大告诉宗教首领耶稣所在的地方，出卖了耶稣，以便他们能抓到耶稣。
  * When the religious leaders condemned Jesus to die, Judas regretted that he had betrayed Jesus, so he killed himself.
  * 当宗教首领们判处耶稣死刑时，犹大后悔他背叛了耶稣，于是自杀了。

===== Examples from the Bible stories =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** One of Jesus' disciples was a man named **Judas**. … After Jesus and the disciples arrived in Jerusalem, **Judas**  went to the Jewish leaders and offered to betray Jesus to them in exchange for money.
  * 耶稣的门徒中有一个人叫犹大, …….在耶稣和门徒们到了耶路撒冷后，犹大去到犹太人首领那里，提议出卖耶稣给他们，以换取金钱。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-03|[38-03]]]** The Jewish leaders, led by the high priest, paid **Judas**  thirty silver coins to betray Jesus.
  * 犹太首领们由大祭司带领着，付给犹大三十块银币去出卖耶稣。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-14|[38-14]]]****Judas**  came with the Jewish leaders, soldiers, and a large crowd. They were all carrying swords and clubs. **Judas**  came to Jesus and said, "Greetings, teacher," and kissed him.
  * 犹大和犹太首领，士兵以及一大群人一起来了。他们全都手持剑和棍杖。犹大来到耶稣跟前，说，“你好，夫子，”并亲吻了祂。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-08|[39-08]]]** Meanwhile, **Judas**, the betrayer, saw that the Jewish leaders had condemned Jesus to die. He became full of sorrow and went away and killed himself.
  * 与此同时，叛徒犹大看见犹太首领判处了耶稣死刑。他满心忧伤地离开，并自杀了。
