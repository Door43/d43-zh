====== King of the Jews 犹太人的王 L ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

"King of the Jews" is a title for Jesus, the Messiah.

“犹太人的王”是耶稣、弥赛亚的头衔。

  * An unusual star indicated that Jesus was the "King of the Jews" on the day that he was born.
  * 在耶稣出生那天，一颗非同寻常的星星的出现，显示耶稣就是“犹太人的王”。
  * Jesus has the right to be the "King of the Jews" because he is a descendent of David, and he is the Messiah.
  * 耶稣有权利做“犹太人的王”，因为他是大卫的后裔，并且是弥赛亚。
  * The Roman soldiers mocked Jesus by calling him the "King of the Jews." They did not realize that he truly had the authority to be the king.
  * 罗马士兵
戏弄 耶稣，叫他“犹太人的王”。他们不知道耶稣确实有权柄做王。
  * The title, "King of the Jews" was written on a piece of wood and placed at the top of Jesus' cross when he was crucified.
  * 在耶稣被钉在十字架上时，“犹太人的王” 这一头衔被写在一块木牌上，并放在他的十字架的顶端。
  * Jesus will return to earth and rule over everyone, including the Jews, as the king of the kingdom of God.
  * 耶稣将以神国的王回到地上，统治所有的人包括犹太人。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-09|[23-09]]]** Some time later, wise men from countries far to the east saw an unusual star in the sky. They realized it meant a new **king of the Jews**  was born.
  * 一段时间以后，从遥远的东方国来的博士们看见了天空中一颗非同寻常的星星。他们知道那意味着一个新犹太人的王诞生了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-09|[39-09]]]** Pilate asked Jesus, "Are you the **King of the Jews**?"
  * 彼拉多问耶稣，“你是犹太人的王吗？”

  * 罗马士兵鞭打了耶稣，并给他披上一个王袍，带上荆棘做的王冠。然后，他们
戏弄 他，说，“看呐，犹太人的王！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-02|[40-02]]]** Pilate commanded that they write, "**King of the Jews**" on a sign and put it on the cross above Jesus' head.
  * 彼拉多命令他们在一个标识牌上写着，“犹太人的王”，把它放到耶稣的头上的十字架上。
