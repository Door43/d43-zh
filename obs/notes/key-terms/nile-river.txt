====== Nile River ======

尼罗河

===== Facts: =====

事迹：

The Nile is a very big river in northeastern Africa.

尼罗河是非洲东北新一条很大的河流。

  * The Nile River flows north through Egypt and empties into the Mediterranean Sea.
  * 尼罗河流经埃及北部，然后注入地中海。
  * Crops grow well in its fertile valley.
  * 在肥沃的尼罗河谷地，庄稼长得非常好。
  * Most Egyptians live near the Nile River since it is an important source of water and food crops.
  * 大部分的埃及都沿尼罗河居住，因为那是很重要的水和农作物来源。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-04|[08-04]]]** Egypt was a large, powerful country located along the **Nile River**.
  * 位于尼罗河畔的埃及是一个辽阔强盛的国家。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-04|[09-04]]]** Pharaoh saw that the Israelites were having many babies, so he ordered his people to kill all Israelite baby boys by throwing them into the **Nile River**.
  * 法老看到以色列人要生许多的婴儿，于是他就命令他的百姓去杀所有以色列的男婴，把他们扔到尼罗河里。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-06|[09-06]]]** When the boy's parents could no longer hide him, they put him in a floating basket among the reeds along the edge of the **Nile River**  in order to save him from being killed.
  * 当男孩的父母不能再隐藏他的时候，他们就把他放在一个漂浮的篮子里，把篮子放在尼罗河的芦苇当中，希望借此保护男孩不会被杀。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:10-03|[10-03]]]** God turned the **Nile River**  into blood, but Pharaoh still would not let the Israelites go.
  * 神把尼罗河的水变成血，但是法老仍然不认以色列人离开。
