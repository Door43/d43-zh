====== Moses 摩西 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Moses was a prophet and leader of the Israelite people for over 40 years.

摩西是一位先知，并做以色列人的首领长达40多年。

  * God chose Moses to free the Israelites from slavery in Egypt and lead them to the Promised Land.
  * 神拣选了摩西把以色列人从埃及的奴役中拯救出来，并带领他们到应许之地。
  * God gave Moses the stone tablets with his commandments for the Israelite people.
  * 神把<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>石板给了摩西，上面写</font> 着神给以色列人的诫命。
  * Near the end of his life, Moses disobeyed God so he didn't get to live in the Promised Land of Canaan.
  * 在摩西晚年，他没有顺服神，所以他不能住在迦南就是应许之地。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:promised-land|Promised Land]]**]

[参见：埃及，应许之地]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-12|[09-12]]]** One day while **Moses**  was taking care of his sheep, he saw a bush that was on fire.
  * 有一天，正当摩西在牧养着他的羊群时，他看见荆棘被火烧着。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]****Moses**  told the Israelites, "Stop being afraid! God will fight for you today and save you."
  * 摩西告诉以色列人：“不要害怕！神今天将为你们争战，并拯救你们。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-07|[12-07]]]** God told **Moses**  to raise his hand over the sea and divide the waters.
  * 神吩咐摩西向海举手，把水分开。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they trusted in God and believed that Moses was a prophet of God.
  * 当以色列人看到埃及人都死了时，他们就相信神，并相信摩西就是神的先知。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** Then God wrote these Ten Commandments on two stone tablets and gave them to **Moses**.
  * 于是，神在两块石板上写下了十诫，并把石板给了摩西。
