====== disciple 門徒 ======

===== Definition: =====

定義 :

A disciple is a student who follows and learns from a teacher.

門徒 就是 一個學生, 他跟隨老師 也向老師學習。

  * The people who listened to the teaching of Jesus and obeyed it were called disciples of Jesus.
  * 一個人聽了 耶穌的教導 並且服從 ， 他就被稱為 耶穌 的門徒。
  * Hundreds of disciples followed Jesus at different times during his ministry on earth.
  * 耶穌 在地上 服事時,在不同的時間 有數百名門徒跟隨耶穌
  * The twelve apostles were chosen from among the disciples of Jesus. They continued to be called "disciples," as well as "apostles."
  * 十二使徒 是被 從 耶穌的門徒 之 中選出來的。 他們繼續 被稱為“ 門徒 ”和 “ 使徒 ”。
  * Just before Jesus went up to heaven, he commanded his disciples to teach others so that they could become his disciples, too.
  * 就在 耶穌 升天 之前， 他 命令他的 門徒去教 導別人 ，使他們 也都能夠 成為他的 門徒。
  * Anyone, even in modern times, who follows the way and teachings of Jesus in obedience and faith is also a disciple of Jesus.
  * 任何人，即使是在 現在， 誰 在順服和信心 裏跟隨耶穌的 道路和教導 的, 也都被稱為耶穌的門徒。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:apostle|apostle]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:believer|believer]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**]

另請參見： 使徒 ， 信徒 ， 耶穌

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事 中的例子

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:30-08|[30-08]]]** He (Jesus) gave the pieces to his **disciples**  to give to the people. The **disciples**  kept passing out the food, and it never ran out!
  * 他 （ 耶穌） 把擘開的食物給他的 門徒去給 其他的人。 門徒們不斷的 把食物 傳遞出 去，食物永遠不會 用盡！
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** About three years after Jesus first began preaching and teaching publicly, Jesus told his **disciples**  that he wanted to
  * celebrate this Passover with them in Jerusalem, and that he would be killed there.
  * 大約 在耶穌首次公開 傳道和教 導的三年 後 ， 耶穌告訴 他的 門徒，他想與他們 在耶路撒冷 慶祝 逾越節 ， 並且他將 在那裡 被殺害。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Then Jesus went with his **disciples**  to a place called Gethsemane. Jesus told his **disciples**  to pray that they would not enter into temptation.
  * 於是 耶穌 與他的 門徒到一個叫 客西馬尼 的地方。 耶穌 告訴他的 門徒們 要禱告 ， 這樣他們 就不會 進入 誘惑 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]** Jesus said to his **disciples**, "All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. So go, make **disciples**  of all people groups by baptizing them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
  * 耶穌 對 他的門徒說 ：“天上 地下所有的 權柄都 賜給我了。 所以 你們要去使萬民作我的門徒, 奉父，子 ， 聖靈 的名 替他們施洗，一切我 所吩咐你們的 都要教導他們 遵守。 “
