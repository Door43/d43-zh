====== Nebuchadnezzar 尼布甲尼撒 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事迹：

Nebuchadnezzar was a king of the Babylonian Empire. This was a powerful empire during the time of the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah.

尼布甲尼撒是巴比伦帝国的王。在以色列国和犹大国时期，这是一个很强大的帝国。

  * Nebuchadnezzar had a powerful army that conquered many other people groups
  * 尼布甲尼撒有一支强大的军队，这支军队征服了许多的民族。
  * Under Nebuchadnezzar's leadership, the Babylonian army attacked and conquered the kingdom of Judah, and took most of the people of Judah to Babylon as captives. This "Babylonian Exile" lasted 70 years.
  * 在尼布甲尼撒的带领下，巴比伦军队攻击并征服了犹大国，而且把许多犹大百姓俘虏到巴比伦去了。这次“被掳到巴比伦”持续了70年。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** About 100 years after the Assyrians destroyed the kingdom of Israel, God sent **Nebuchadnezzar**, king of the Babylonians, to attack the kingdom of Judah.
  * 在亚述毁灭以色列国大约100年后，神差遣巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒去攻击犹大国。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** The king of Judah agreed to be **Nebuchadnezzar's**  servant and pay him a lot of money every year.
  * 犹大王答应做尼布甲尼撒的仆人，并且每年进贡许多的钱财。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-08|[20-08]]]** To punish the king of Judah for rebelling, **Nebuchadnezzar's**  soldiers killed the king's sons in front of him and then made him blind.
  * 作为犹大王背叛的惩罚，尼布甲尼撒的士兵在犹大王面前杀了他的众子，并且弄瞎了他的眼睛。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]****Nebuchadnezzar**  and his army took almost all of the people of the kingdom of Judah to Babylon, leaving only the poorest people behind to plant the fields.
  * 尼布甲尼撒和他的军队把差不多所有的犹大国百姓带到巴比伦去，只把最贫穷的百姓留下耕种田地。
