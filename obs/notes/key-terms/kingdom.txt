====== kingdom王国/国度 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A kingdom is a group of people ruled by a king.

王国是被一个王统治着的一族群

  * A kingdom can be of any geographical size; a king might govern a nation or country or only one city.
  * 一个王国可以是任何地域大小的：一个国王可以统治一个国家，或本邦本土，或只是一个城市。
  * "Kingdom" also refers to the ruling or governing power that a king exercises over a group of people.
  * “王国”也指一个国王操纵族群的统治或执政能力。
  * God's kingdom refers to his rule over everything.
  * 神的国度是指神统领一切。
  * Satan also temporarily rules over many things. This is "Satan's kingdom" which will be destroyed by God.
  * 撒旦也暂时统领了很多事情。这就是将被神摧毁的“撒旦的国度”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-02|[13-02]]]** God said to Moses and the people of Israel, "If you will obey me and keep my covenant, you will be my prized possession, a **kingdom**  of priests, and a holy nation."
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-04|[18-04]]]** God was angry with Solomon and, as a punishment for Solomon's unfaithfulness, he promised to divide the nation of Israel in two **kingdoms**  after Solomon's death.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-07|[18-07]]]** Ten of the tribes of the nation of Israel rebelled against Rehoboam. Only two tribes remained faithful to him. These two tribes became the **kingdom**  of Judah.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-08|[18-08]]]** The other ten tribes of the nation of Israel that rebelled against Rehoboam appointed a man named Jeroboam to be their king. They set up their **kingdom**  in the northern part of the land and were called the **kingdom**  of Israel.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-08|[21-08]]]** A king is someone who rules over a **kingdom**  and judges the people.
