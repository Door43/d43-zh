====== servant仆人/奴仆W ======

==== (servant, slave, slavery) ====

（仆人，奴仆，奴役）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A servant is a person who works for another person.

仆人/奴仆，是为另外一个人做工的人。

  * Some servants are an important part of their master's household and are treated almost like members of the family.
  * 一些仆人是他们主人家庭中重要的部分，几乎<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>被看待成</font> 象家庭成员一样。
  * People who follow God are often called his "servants."
  * 跟随神的人经常被称作神的“仆人”。
  * A slave is a kind of servant who is the property of the person he works for. Just as people buy clothing and animals, in some parts of the world, people buy people. The person who buys a slave is called his "owner" or "master." Some masters treat their slaves very cruelly while other masters treat their slaves very well.
  * 奴仆是仆人的一种，他们是那被服侍的人的财产。正如人们买衣服和动物，在这世上的一些地方，人买卖人。买奴仆的人叫做“业主”，或“主人”。一些主人对待他们的奴仆非常残忍，而另一些主人却非常善待他们的奴仆。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** When Abraham was very old and his son, Isaac, had grown to be a man, Abraham sent one of his **servants**  back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.
  * 当亚伯拉罕年迈，他的儿子以撒已成年时，亚伯拉罕派他一个仆人回到他的本族居住之地，去为他的儿子以撒娶个妻子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-04|[08-04]]]** The **slave**  traders sold Joseph as a **slave**  to a wealthy government official.
  * 奴隶贩子把约瑟作为一个奴仆，卖给一个富有的政府官员。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-13|[09-13]]]**"I (God) will send you (Moses) to Pharaoh so that you can bring the Israelites out of their **slavery**  in Egypt."
  * “我（神）将差遣你（摩西）到法老那里，这样你可以把以色列人带出埃及这个为奴之地。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-10|[19-10]]]** Then Elijah prayed, "O Yahweh, God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, show us today that you are the God of Israel and that I am your **servant**."
  * 于是以利亚祷告说，“哦，耶和华，亚伯拉罕，以撒和雅各的神啊，今天向我们表明你就是以色列的神，而我是你的仆人”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-03|[29-03]]]**"Since the **servant**  could not pay the debt, the king said, 'Sell this man and his family as **slaves**  to make payment on his debt.'"
  * “因为这仆人无力偿还所欠的债，那王就说，”把这人和他的家人卖了做奴仆，偿还他的债务”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:35-06|[35-06]]]**"All my father's **servants**  have plenty to eat, and yet here I am starving."
  * “我父亲的所有的仆人们都吃得饱足，而我却在这儿忍饥挨饿。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-04|[47-04]]]** The **slave**  girl kept yelling as they walked, "These men are servants of the Most High God.
  * 正当他们走过时，女仆继续高喊，“这些人是至高神的仆人。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-04|[50-04]]]** Jesus also said, "A **servant**  is not greater than his master."
  * 耶稣也说，“仆人不能高过主人。”
