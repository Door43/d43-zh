====== innocent无辜的/无罪的 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To be innocent means to **not** be guilty of something.

无辜的，意思指是无罪的。

  * In the case of a crime, a person is innocent of the crime if he has **not**  committed that crime.
  * 在一个刑事案中，如果一个人没有犯那罪，那么他是无辜的。
  * Jesus is the only human being who was completely innocent, because he never did anything wrong. He was sinless.
  * 耶稣是唯一的完全无罪的人，因为他从没有做错任何事。他是无罪的。

The opposite of "innocent" is "guilty."

“无辜的”的反义词是“有罪的”

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:guilt|guilt]]**]

[参见：有罪]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** After two years, Joseph was still in prison, even though he was **innocent**.
  * 两年后，尽管他是无辜的，约瑟仍被关在监狱里。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-04|[40-04]]]** One of them mocked Jesus, but the other said, "Do you have no fear of God? We are guilty, but this man is **innocent**."
  * 他们中的一个（犯人）讥笑耶稣，但是另一个说道，“你不怕神吗？我们是有罪的，但是这人是无罪的。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-08|[40-08]]]** When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that happened, he said, "Certainly, this man was **innocent**. He was the Son of God."
  * 当看守耶稣的士兵看见了所发生的一切时，他说：“毫无疑问，这个人是无罪的。他确实是神的儿子。”
