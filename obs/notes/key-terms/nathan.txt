====== Nathan ======

拿单

===== Facts: =====

事迹：

Nathan was a faithful prophet of God who lived during the time when David was king over Israel.

拿单是这神一个忠心的先知，他生活在大卫作王治理以色列的时期。

  * God sent Nathan to confront David after David sinned grievously against Uriah.
  * 在大卫在乌利亚的事上严重的犯罪之后，神差遣拿单去见他。
  * Nathan rebuked David, in spite of the fact that David was the king.
  * 拿单责备大卫，尽管大卫当时是王。
  * David repented of his sin after Nathan spoke to him.
  * 拿单对大卫说话以后，大卫悔改了他的罪。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:uriah|Uriah]]**]

另请参见：大卫，乌利亚

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]** God sent the prophet **Nathan**  to David with this message, "Because you are a man of war, you will not build this Temple for me."
  * 神差遣先知拿单传信息给大卫说：“因为你是一个战士，所以你不可以为我建殿。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the prophet **Nathan**  to tell David how evil his sin was.
  * 因为大卫所行的，神向大卫发怒，所以神差遣先知拿单告诉大卫他所犯的罪是何等的恶。
