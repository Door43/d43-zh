====== tempt ======

引诱

==== (to tempt, temptation) ====

（引诱，诱惑）

===== Definition: =====

定义

To tempt someone is to try to get that person to do something wrong.

诱惑某人，就是尝试去使人做错误的事情。

  * A temptation is something that causes a person to want to do something wrong.
  * 诱惑是导致一个人想去做错误的事情的行为。
  * People are tempted by their own sinful nature and by other people.
  * 人们都会受到他们自己的罪恶的天性以及其他人的诱惑。
  * Satan also tempts people to disobey God and to sin against God by doing wrong things.
  * 撒旦也引诱人去违背神，通过做错误的事情来犯罪悖逆神。
  * Jesus resisted all of Satan's temptations. Jesus never sinned.
  * 耶稣抵制所有撒旦的诱惑。耶稣从不犯罪。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:disobey|disobey]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:satan|Satan]]**] 参见：罪行，违背，撒旦]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Then Satan came to Jesus and **tempted**  him to sin.
  * 于是撒旦来到耶稣跟前，引诱他去犯罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-08|[25-08]]]** Jesus did not give in to Satan's **temptations**, so Satan left him.
  * 耶稣没有屈服于撒旦的诱惑，所以撒旦离开了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Jesus told his disciples to pray that they would not enter into **temptation**.
  * 耶稣告诉他的信徒们要祷告，祷告他们将不会陷入诱惑。
