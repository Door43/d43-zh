====== repent忏悔/悔改/懊悔 ======

==== (repent, repentance) ====

[忏悔（动词），忏悔（名词）]

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To repent is to regret or be sorrowful for something and to decide to stop doing it.

忏悔就是为某事后悔或难过，并决定要停止不做了。

  * To repent from sin means to admit to God that it is wrong, to stop doing that sin, and to begin to obey God.
  * 从罪中忏悔，意思是说，向神承认那是错的，要停止行那罪，并开始信服神。
  * Repentance is the act of repenting.
  * 悔改是忏悔的行动。
  * When people truly repent of their sins, God forgives them.
  * 当人真正悔改自己的罪时，神就原谅他们。
  * The term, "repent" can be translated as, "turn from your sins and be sorry for them."
  * 术语，“忏悔”可以被翻译成，“从罪中反悔，并为此遗憾。”

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-02|[16-02]]]** After many years of disobeying God and being oppressed by their enemies, the Israelites **repented**  and asked God to rescue them.
  * 在背叛神并受敌人压迫很多年后，以色列人反悔了，祈求神来拯救他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** David **repented**  of his sin and God forgave him.
  * 大卫忏悔了他的罪，并且神原谅了他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:19-18|[19-18]]]** They (prophets) warned people that God would destroy them if they did not **repent**.
  * 他们（先知们）警告人们，如果他们不忏悔，神将摧毁他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-02|[24-02]]]** Many people came out to the wilderness to listen to John. He preached to them, saying, "**Repent**, for the kingdom of God is near!"
  * 很多人来到旷野来听约翰（讲道）。他向人们传道，说，“忏悔吧，因为神的国近了！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]**""It was also written in the scriptures that my disciples will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to **receive**  forgiveness for their sins. "
  * “经文里也记载着，我的信徒们将宣告，每个人都应该忏悔，以便为着他们的罪得着饶恕。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-05|[44-05]]]**"So now, **repent**  and turn to God so that your sins will be washed away."
  * “所以，现在，忏悔并归向耶稣吧，这样你们的罪将被洗刷干净。”
