====== son儿子 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A son is someone's direct male offspring. This is a relationship term and does not depend on the person's age.

儿子是某个人的直接的男性后代。这是一个关系术语，而且不依赖人的年龄。

  * "Son" usually refers to a boy or man in relation to his parents.
  * “儿子”通常是指一个男孩或男人和他的父母的关系。
  * Sometimes the word "son" refers to a boy or man in relation to someone who cares for him as if he were his or her offspring. This can happen when someone adopts a boy to be his or her son.
  * 有时字眼“儿子”是指一个男孩或男人和某个照顾他的人的关系，就好像他是他的或她的后代。这可以发生在某个人领养了一个男孩做他的或她的儿子的情况下。

[参见：神圣子]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-08|[04-08]]]** God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a **son**  and as many descendants as the stars in the sky.
  * 神对亚伯拉翰说了话，并再次应许他，他将会有一个儿子，并有很多子子孙孙的后代，象天上的星星一样多。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:04-09|[04-09]]]** God said, ""I will give you a **son**  from your own body."
  * 神说到，“我将从你自己的身体里给你一个儿子。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-05|[05-05]]]** About a year later, when Abraham was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's **son**.
  * 大约一年后，当亚伯拉翰100岁，撒拉90岁时，撒拉生了亚伯拉翰的儿子”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]** When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his **son**  Isaac and laid him on an altar. He was about to kill his **son**  when God said, "Stop! Do not hurt the boy! Now I know that you fear me because you did not keep your only **son**  from me."
  * 当他们到了祭祀的地方时，亚伯拉翰把他的儿子绑起来，把他放到一个祭坛上。就在他正要杀死他的儿子时，耶稣说到。“停下！不要伤害这个孩子！现在我知道你敬畏我了。因为你因着我都不留着你唯一的儿子。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-07|[09-07]]]** When she saw the baby, she took him as her own **son**.
  * 当她看见那个孩子时，她把他当做自己的儿子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:11-06|[11-06]]]** God killed every one of the Egyptians' firstborn **sons**.
  * 神杀了每一个埃及人头生的儿子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-01|[18-01]]]** After many years, David died, and his **son**  Solomon began to rule.
  * 很多年以后，大卫死了，他的儿子所罗门开始统治（国家）。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-04|[26-04]]]**"Is this the **son**  of Joseph?‚" they said.
  * “这是约瑟的儿子吗？”他们问道。
