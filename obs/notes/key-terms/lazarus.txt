====== Lazarus 拉撒路 P ======

==== Facts: ====

事实依据：

Lazarus was a friend of Jesus whom he raised from the dead.

拉撒路是耶稣的一个朋友 , 耶稣使他从死里复活。

  * Lazarus had two sisters, named Mary and Martha, who also believed in Jesus.
  * 拉撒路有两个姐妹，她们的名字是马利亚和马大，她们也信耶稣。
  * After Jesus raised Lazarus from the dead, the Jewish leaders were angry and tried to find a way to kill both of them.
  * 耶稣使拉撒路从死里复活之后，犹太领袖们感到愤怒，并试图找方法来杀死他们两个。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]** One day, Jesus received a message that **Lazarus**  was very sick. **Lazarus**  and his two sisters, Mary and Martha, were close friends of Jesus.
  * 有一天，耶稣收到一个信息说拉撒路病得很重。 拉撒路和他的两个姐妹，马利亚和马大，都是耶稣亲密的朋友。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-02|[37-02]]]** Jesus said, "Our friend **Lazarus**  has fallen asleep, and I must wake him."
  * 耶稣说：“我们的朋友拉撒路睡了，我必须叫醒他。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-03|[37-03]]]** Jesus' disciples replied, "Master, if **Lazarus**  is sleeping, then he will get better." Then Jesus told them plainly, "**Lazarus**  is dead."
  * 耶稣的门徒说：“主啊，拉撒路若是睡了，那么他就会好了。” 耶稣就明明的告訴他们说：“拉撒路死了。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-04|[37-04]]]** When Jesus arrived at **Lazarus'**  hometown, **Lazarus**  had already been dead for four days.
  * 当耶稣来到拉撒路的家乡，拉撒路已经死了四天。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-06|[37-06]]]** Jesus asked them, "Where have you put **Lazarus**?"
  * 耶稣问他们：“你们把拉撒路放在那里？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-09|[37-09]]]** Then Jesus shouted, "**Lazarus**, come out!"
  * 耶稣大声喊道：“ 拉撒路，出来！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** So **Lazarus**  came out! He was still wrapped in grave clothes.
  * 拉撒路就出来了！他还是包着裹尸布。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-11|[37-11]]]** But the religious leaders of the Jews were jealous, so they gathered together to plan how they could kill Jesus and **Lazarus**.
  * 但犹太人的宗教领袖们都很嫉妒，所以他们聚集在一起商议怎么才能杀死耶稣和拉撒路。
