====== wise 智慧 ======

=== (wise, wisdom, wisely) （智慧的，智慧，明智地） ===

===== Definition: 定义 =====

A person who is "wise" is one who exercises "wisdom." To be wise is to discern or judge a matter rightly.

一个“智慧的”的人就是实践了“智慧”的那人。拥有了智慧，就拥有了正确分辨或判断事物的能力。

  * To have "wisdom" is to possess important knowledge or experience.
  * 要想拥有”智慧“，就要拥有具备影响力的知识或阅历。
  * A "wise" person is able to make good choices.
  * 一个”智慧“的人是能够做出正确的选择的。
  * To act "wisely" is to know what is right and to do it.
  * “举止（行为）”明智，就是知道什么是正确的，怎么去做。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:02-05|[02-05]]]** She also wanted to be **wise**, so she picked some of the fruit and ate it.
  * 她也想要有智慧，所以她摘了一些果子并吃了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-01|[18-01]]]** When Solomon asked for **wisdom**, God was pleased and made him the **wisest**  man in the world.
  * 当所罗门祈求智慧时，神很高兴，并使他成为世上最有智慧的人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-09|[23-09]]]** Some time later, **wise**  men from countries far to the east saw an unusual star in the sky.
  * 一段时间以后，来自遥远东方国家的博士看见了空中一颗不寻常的星星。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-01|[45-01]]]** He (Stephen) had a good reputation and was full of the Holy Spirit and of **wisdom**.
  * 他（司提反）曾享有良好声誉，并且充满了圣灵和智慧。
