====== hell 地狱 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Hell is a place of unending pain and suffering that God prepared for punishing Satan and the evil spirits that follow him.

地狱是遭受无穷尽痛苦和折磨的地方，是神为惩罚撒旦和跟随撒旦的邪灵所预备的。

  * Because people have sinned and rebelled against God, they deserve eternal punishment in hell.
  * 因为人犯了罪，悖逆了神，他们就应该在地狱里接受永远的惩罚。

  * Hell is described as a place of fire and anguish.
  * 地狱被描述成火湖和倍受痛苦（煎熬）的地方。
  * God does not want anyone to go to hell, so he provided a way to be saved from it.
  * 神不愿意任何人下地狱，所以他预备了拯救人的方法。
  * God sent Jesus to be a sacrifice for people's sins so that if they believe in Jesus they will not go to hell.
  * 为了人的罪，神差遣耶稣成为祭牲，这样如果人们相信耶稣，就必得救，而不会下地狱。
  * If people reject salvation through Jesus, they will suffer in hell for forever。 \\ 如果人拒绝主耶稣的拯救，那他们将永远在地狱中遭受痛苦。
  * Hell could be translated as, "place of darkness and anguish."
  * 地狱也可以被翻译成“黑暗和痛苦的地方。”

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-14|[50-14]]]** He (God) will throw them into **hell**, where they will weep and grind their teeth in anguish forever. A fire that never goes out will continually burn them, and worms will never stop eating them.
  * 他（神）将把他们扔到地狱里，在那里他们将永远地在痛苦中哀哭切齿。永不熄灭的火将不停地焚烧他们，并且虫也不停地咬嗜他们。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-15|[50-15]]]** He will throw Satan into **hell**  where he will burn forever, along with everyone who chose to follow him rather than to obey God.
  * 神将把撒旦扔到地狱里，它（撒旦）和那些选择跟随它而不顺从神的人一起，将永远被火焚烧。
