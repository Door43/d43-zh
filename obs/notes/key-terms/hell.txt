====== hell 地狱 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

Hell is a place of unending pain and suffering that God prepared for punishing Satan and the evil spirits that follow him.

地狱是遭受无穷尽痛苦和折磨的地方，是神为惩罚撒旦和跟随他的邪灵所准备的。

  * Because people have sinned and rebelled against God, they deserve eternal punishment in hell.
  * 因为人犯了罪，背叛了神，他们就要在地狱里接受无尽的惩罚。

  * Hell is described as a place of fire and anguish.
  * 地狱被描述成火海和倍受煎熬的地方。
  * God does not want anyone to go to hell, so he provided a way to be saved from it.
  * 神不想让任何人下地狱，所以他提供了拯救人的方法。
  * God sent Jesus to be a sacrifice for people's sins so that if they believe in Jesus they will not go to hell.
  * 神派耶稣作为祭品来替代人的罪，这样如果人们相信主耶稣，就必得救不下地狱。
  * If people reject salvation through Jesus, they will suffer in hell for forever。 \\ 如果人拒绝通过主耶稣来拯救自己，那他将永远在地狱中遭受无尽痛苦。
  * Hell could be translated as, "place of darkness and anguish."
  * 地狱也可以翻译成“无尽黑暗和极端痛苦的地方。”
