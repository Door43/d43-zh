====== Eve 夏娃 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

This was the name of the first woman. Her name means "life" or "living."

这是（神造的）第一个女人的名字。这名字的意思是“生命”，或“活着“。

  * God personally formed Eve from a rib that he took out of Adam.
  * 神亲自从亚当身体里取出了肋骨，做成了夏娃。
  * Eve was created to be Adam's "helper." She came alongside Adam to assist him in the work that God gave him to do.
  * 夏娃被造成亚当的“帮手”。她来到亚当旁边，协助亚当做神给他要做的工作。
  * Eve was tempted by the snake (Satan) and was the first to sin by eating the fruit that God said not to eat.
  * 夏娃受到蛇（撒旦）的引诱，吃了神说的不能吃的果子，成了第一个犯下罪行的人。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:adam|Adam]]**] [参见：亚当]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:01-13|[01-13]]]** Then God took one of Adam's ribs and made it into a woman and brought her to him.
  * 于是神取出亚当的一根肋骨，用它造了一个女人，领她到亚当跟前。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:02-02|[02-02]]]** But there was a crafty snake in the garden. He asked the woman, "Did God really tell you not to eat the fruit from any of the trees in the garden?"
  * 但是伊甸园里有一个狡猾的蛇。他问这个女人，“神真的告诉你不要吃园中树上的任何的果子吗？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:02-11|[02-11]]]** The man named his wife **Eve**, which means "life-giver," because she would become the mother of all people.
  * 男人亚当给他的妻子取名夏娃，意思是“给生命的人”，因为她将会成为所有人的母亲。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:21-01|[21-01]]]** God promised that a descendant of **Eve**  would be born who would crush the snake's head.
  * 神应许夏娃生的后裔将会打碎这个蛇的头。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-02|[48-02]]]** Satan spoke through the snake in the garden in order to deceive **Eve**.
  * 撒旦借着园中的蛇的口说话，为的是欺骗诱惑夏娃。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-08|[49-08]]]** When Adam and **Eve**  sinned, it affected all of their descendants.
  * 当亚当和夏娃犯了罪后，这影响并波及到了他们所有的后代。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-16|[50-16]]]** Because Adam and **Eve**  disobeyed God and brought sin into this world, God cursed it and decided to destroy it.
  * 因为亚当和夏娃违背了神，并且把罪带到了这个世界上。神就诅咒了这一切，并决定要摧毁它。
