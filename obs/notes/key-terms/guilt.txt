====== guilt ======

有罪

==== (guilt, guilty) ====

( 有罪，有罪的）

===== Definition: =====

定義：

Guilt is the fact of having sinned or committed a crime.

有罪是犯了罪 ，或作了一件有罪的事。

  * To be guilty means, "to have done something morally wrong," that is, "to have disobeyed God."
  * 有罪是 ， “ 做了一些不道德的事 ”，即 ， “ 违背了神 。 ”
  * People who are guilty deserve to be punished.
  * 有罪的人应该受到惩罚 。
  * Some languages might translate "guilt" as, "the weight of one's sins" or, "the counting of one's sins."
  * 有些语言可能翻译 “有罪 ”成， “ 一个人的罪过的重量 ”，或者 “计算 一个人的罪恶 。 ”

The opposite of "guilty" is "innocent."

“有罪” 的反面是 “ 无辜的 ”。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:innocent|innocent]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:punish|punish]]**]

【同时看： 无辜 ， 罪恶 ， 惩罚 】

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-02|[39-02]]]** They brought many witnesses who lied about him (Jesus). However, their statements did not agree with each other, so the Jewish leaders could not prove he was **guilty**  of anything.
  * 他们带来了许多证人作加见证告他（ 耶稣） 。 但他们的证词彼此不合，所以犹太领袖无法证明他是有任何的罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:39-11|[39-11]]]** After speaking with Jesus, Pilate went out to the crowd and said, "I find no **guilt**  in this man." But the Jewish leaders and the crowd shouted, "Crucify him!" Pilate replied, "He is not **guilty**." But they shouted even louder. Then Pilate said a third time, "He is not **guilty**!"
  * 与耶稣说完话后 ， 彼拉多出去对人群 说：“ 我查出这人是无罪的 。 ” 但犹太领袖和人群喊道：“ 把他钉十字架！ ” 彼拉多回答说 ， “他 是无罪的 。 ” 但是，他们喊的更大声。彼拉多第三次说 ， “他是 无罪的 ！ ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-04|[40-04]]]** Jesus was crucified between two robbers. One of them mocked Jesus, but the other said, "Don't you fear God? We are **guilty**, but this man is innocent.
  * 耶稣被钉在两个强盗之间 。 其中一个人嘲弄耶稣 ， 但另一个人说 ， “你不敬畏神吗？我们是有罪的， 但是这个人是无罪的 。“
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-10|[49-10]]]** Because of your sin, you are **guilty**  and deserve to die.
  * 因为你的罪 ， 你是有罪的并是该死的 。
