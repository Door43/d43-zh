====== deliverer ======

拯救者

===== Definition: =====

A deliverer is someone who rescues people or sets people free.

一個拯救者就是一個人他將人們解救出來, 使人得著自由

  * The word "deliverer" can also be translated as, "rescuer."
  * 拯救者也可以翻譯成解救者
  * The deliverers in Israel were leaders who rescued the Israelite people from their enemies by leading them in battle against their enemies.
  * In many cases, the deliverer functioned as a military leader.
  * 在許多地方, 拯救者就是軍事上的領導者

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a **deliverer**  who rescued them from their enemies and brought peace to the land.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-16|[16-16]]]** They (Israel) finally asked God for help again, and God sent them another **deliverer**.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** Over many years, God sent many **deliverers**  who saved the Israelites from their enemies.
