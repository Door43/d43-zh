====== good news好消息 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

The phrase, "good news" means a message or announcement that tells people something that benefits them and makes them glad.

“ 好消息 ”， 这个词是指一个讯息或宣告，告诉人们对他们有益、使他们高兴的诸如之类的事 。

  * In the Bible, the phrase "the good news" often refers specifically to the message that God sent his Son to earth to save people from their sins: God forgives all people who trust in his Son, and he gives them eternal life.
  * 在圣经中，“ 好消息 ”一词 往往特指神派他的儿子到地上，拯救众人脱离他们的罪恶的信息：神赦免所有相信他儿子的人 ，并赐给他们永生。
  * This could also be translated as, "good message" or, "good announcement" or, "glad word."
  * 这也可以翻译成， “好信息”， “ 好宣告 ” 或 “ 好言语。 ”
  * The "good news" is also called the "good news of Jesus," the "good news about Jesus," and the "good news of the kingdom."
  * “好消息 ”也被称为“耶稣的好消息，” “ 关于耶稣的好消息， ”和“ 天国的好消息。 ”
  * In many Christian contexts, the good news is called "the gospel."
  * 在许多基督教的语境中，好消息被称为“ 福音”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

一些圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]** The angel said, "Do not be afraid, because I have some **good news**  for you. The Messiah, the Master, has been born in Bethlehem!"
  * 天使说： “ 不要害怕，因为我有好消息给你。弥赛亚，主，已经诞生在伯利恒！ ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-03|[26-03]]]** Jesus read, "God has given me his Spirit so that I can proclaim **good news**  to the poor, freedom to captives, recovery of sight for the blind, and release to the oppressed. This is the year of the Lord's favor."
  * 耶稣念到， “神<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>赐</font>    给我他的灵 ，使我能够宣布好消息给穷人，被虏的得自由，瞎眼的得看见 ， 并释放被压迫的，这是主悦纳人的禧年。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip also used other Scriptures to tell him the **good news of Jesus**.
  * 腓利还用其它的经文，告诉他关于耶稣的好消息。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-10|[46-10]]]** Then they sent them off to preach the **good news about Jesus**  in many other places.
  * 他们就差他们去其它许多地方传讲有关耶稣的好消息 。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** One day, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of Philippi to proclaim the **good news about Jesus**.
  * 有一天， 保罗和他的朋友西拉来到腓立比传讲有关耶稣的好消息。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-13|[47-13]]]** The **good news about Jesus**  kept spreading, and the Church kept growing.
  * 关于耶稣的好消息不断被传开， 教会不断增长。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-01|[50-01]]]** For almost 2,000 years, more and more people around the world have been hearing the **good news about Jesus**  the Messiah.
  * 近2000年来，世界各地越来越多的人听到了有关耶稣弥赛亚的好消息。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-02|[50-02]]]** When Jesus was living on earth he said, "My disciples will preach the **good news**  about the kingdom of God to people everywhere in the world, and then the end will come."
  * 当耶稣还活在地上时，他说， “ 我的门徒会把关于神国的好消息传到世界各地，然后末期才来到 。 ”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-03|[50-03]]]** Before he returned to heaven, Jesus told Christians to proclaim the **good news**  to people who have never heard it.
  * 他回到天堂之前， 耶稣告诉基督徒去把好消息传给那些从来没有听过的人 。
