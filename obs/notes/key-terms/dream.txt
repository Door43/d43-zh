====== dream 夢 F ======

==== (to dream, dream) ====

(作夢、夢)

===== Definition: =====

定義 :

Dreams are things that people see, hear, and experience in their minds while they are sleeping.

夢就是當人們在睡覺的時候，在他們的心思裏所看見、所聽到和所經歷的事。

  * Most of the time, the things that people experience in dreams are not really happening, although while the person is dreaming, it seems that they are.
  * 大多數時候，<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>雖然</font>    人在夢中所經歷的事好像是真實的，但事實上事情並沒有真正的發生。
  * Sometimes God causes people to dream about something so they can learn from it. Sometimes God may speak directly to people in their dreams.
  * 有時候， 神使人們在夢中見到一些事物，使他們能夠從中吸取教訓。有時候，神可以直接在人的夢中向人說話。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事中的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]** Joseph's brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had **dreamed**  that he would be their ruler.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]** 約瑟的哥哥們恨他，因為他們的父親最愛他，同時因為約瑟夢見自己將會是他們的統治者。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** One night, the Pharaoh, which is what the Egyptians called their kings, had two **dreams**  that disturbed him greatly. None of his advisors could tell him the meaning of the **dreams**.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** 一天晚上，法老，就是埃及人對他們的王的稱呼，作了兩個夢，這些夢使他大大的不安。沒有任何一個<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>他的</font>    謀士，可以告訴他夢的意思。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given Joseph the ability to interpret **dreams**, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison. Joseph interpreted the **dreams**  for him and said, "God is going to send seven years of plentiful harvests followed by seven years of famine."
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** 神給了約瑟解夢的能力，所以法老把約瑟從監獄裏帶到他面前。約瑟替法老解夢，他說：“神會降下七年豐富的收成，接著會有七個荒年。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-11|[16-11]]]** So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about something he had **dreamed**. The man's friend said, "This **dream**  means that Gideon's army will defeat the Midianite army!"
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:16-11|[16-11]]]** 於是當晚，基甸就下到營地，並聽見了一個米甸士兵告訴他的朋友關於他曾夢見的事。這人的朋友說：“這個夢的意思是基甸的軍隊將擊敗米甸的軍隊！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-01|[23-01]]]** He (Joseph) did not want to shame her (Mary), so he planned to quietly divorce her. Before he could do that, an angel came and spoke to him in a **dream**.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-01|[23-01]]]** 他（約瑟）不想羞辱她（瑪利亞），於是他打算暗暗地把她休了。在他要做這事之前，一個天使前來，在夢中向他說話。
