====== persecute 逼迫/ 迫害 P ======

==== (to persecute, persecution) ====

[逼迫/迫害（动词），逼迫/迫害（名词）]

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To persecute someone is to repeatedly attack him or to treat him cruelly.

逼迫某人，就是重复地攻击他，或残酷地对待他。

  * It is possible to persecute a single person, a small group of people, or even a large number of people.
  * 逼迫一个人、一小群人、或甚至一大群人，都是可能的。
  * The Jewish religious leaders persecuted Jesus because they did not like what he was teaching.
  * 犹太宗教首领迫害耶稣，因为他们不喜欢耶稣所教导的。
  * After Jesus went back to heaven, the Jewish religious leaders and the Roman government persecuted the Christians. However, the believers refused to stop preaching and teaching about Jesus, and the Church continued to grow.
  * 耶稣回到天上后，犹太宗教首领和罗马政府迫害基督徒。然而，信徒们拒绝停止传扬和教导关于耶稣的事，而且教会继续增长。
  * Other words for "persecution" are "mistreatment" or, "abuse" or, "oppression" or, "hostility."
  * 对应“迫害”的其它的词有，“恶待”，或“虐待”，或“压迫”，或“敌视”。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:jewish-leaders|Jewish leaders]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:rome|Rome]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:christian|Christians]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:church|Church]]**]

[参见：犹太首领，罗马，基督徒，教会]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:33-07|[33-07]]]**"The rocky ground is a person who hears God's word and accepts it with joy. But when he experiences hardship or **persecution**, he falls away."
  * “那石头地就像是人听了神的道，高兴地领受了。但是当他遇到了患难或逼迫，他就跌倒了。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-06|[45-06]]]** That day many people in Jerusalem started **persecuting**  the followers of Jesus, so the believers fled to other places.
  * 那天，在耶路撒冷的很多人开始逼迫跟随耶稣的人，所以信徒们就逃到其它地方去了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-02|[46-02]]]** Saul heard someone say, "Saul! Saul! Why do you **persecute**  me?" Saul asked, "Who are you, Master?" Jesus replied to him, "I am Jesus. You are **persecuting**  me!"
  * 扫罗听到有人说：“扫罗！扫罗！你为什么逼迫我？”扫罗问道：“主啊，你是谁？”耶稣回答说：“我是耶稣。你正在逼迫我！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-04|[46-04]]]** But Ananias said, "Master, I have heard how this man has **persecuted**  the believers."
  * 但是亚拿尼亚说：“主啊，我听说过这个人是怎样迫害众信徒的。”
