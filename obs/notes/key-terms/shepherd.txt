====== shepherd 牧人 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

A shepherd is a person who takes care of sheep.

牧人是看护羊群的人。

  * Sometimes a shepherd is the owner of the sheep, and sometimes he works for the owner.
  * 有时一个牧人是羊的主人，有时牧羊人是为羊的主人工作。
  * Shepherds lead sheep to grass so they can eat and to safe water so they can drink.
  * 牧人领着羊到草地去吃草，到安全的水边去喝水。
  * They guide sheep so they don't get lost or wander into danger.
  * 他们引导着羊，这样羊群不会走丢或步入危险之地。
  * They protect sheep from wild animals that try to attack them.
  * 他们保护着羊群不受野兽的攻击。
  * It is also used as a metaphor about leaders and those who follow them.
  * 这也用来比喻领袖和跟随领袖的人。

[参见：羊]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-11|[09-11]]]** Moses became a **shepherd**  in the wilderness far away from Egypt.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>在远离埃及的旷野之地，</font>    摩西成为一个牧人。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a **shepherd**  from the town of Bethlehem. At different times while he was watching his father's sheep, David had killed both a lion and a bear that had attacked the sheep.
  * 大卫曾是来自伯利恒城的一个牧人。在看管他父亲的羊群时，大卫曾在不同时候杀死了攻击羊群的狮子和熊。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]** That night, there were some **shepherds**  in a nearby field guarding their flocks.
  * 那天晚上，在附近的野地有一些牧人在看护着羊群。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-08|[23-08]]]** The **shepherds**  soon arrived at the place where Jesus was and they found him lying in a feeding trough, just as the angel had told them.
  * 牧人不久到了耶稣在的地方，他们发现他正躺在一个餵食槽里，正如天使告诉他们的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:30-03|[30-03]]]** To Jesus, these people were like sheep without a **shepherd**.
  * 对于耶稣来说，这些人就象是没有牧人的羊。
