====== eternal life 永生 P ======

===== Definition: 定义： =====

"Eternal" refers to something that never ends. "Eternal life" is used in the Bible to refer to living forever with God.

“永恒”指某些永无止境的事物。“永生”用在圣经中，用来指和神永远生活在一起。

  * When a person repents of his sin and trusts Jesus to forgive him, that person receives "eternal life." Nothing will ever separate that person from God–not even death. He will enjoy life in God's presence forever.
  * 当一个人认罪悔改并相信耶稣会赦免他时，那人就得着“永生”。没有什么东西，即使是死亡，能把人与神分开。他将享受永远活在神的面前。
  * In the Bible, to receive "eternal life" is to be "saved" from sin and death.
  * 在圣经里，要想得着“永生”，那就要从罪和死亡中得着救赎。
  * Sometimes this is translated as, "everlasting life" or, "living forever."
  * 有时这又被翻译成，“永恒的生命” 或 “永远活着”。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证；

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** One day, an expert in the Jewish law came to Jesus to test him, saying, "Teacher, what must I do to inherit **eternal life**?"
  * 一天，一个犹太的律法师来到耶稣面前试探他，说：“老师，我必须怎样做才能得到永生呢？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** One day, a rich young ruler came up to Jesus and asked him, "Good Teacher, what must I do to have **eternal life**?" Jesus said to him, "Why do you ask me about what is good? There is only One who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have **eternal life**, obey God's laws."
  * 一天，一个富有的年轻官员来到耶稣面前，问他，“良善的老师，我必须要怎样做才能得到永生？” 耶稣对他说：“你为什么问我什么是良善的呢？除了神以外，再没有良善的。如果你想承受永生，就当遵守神的诫命“。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-10|[28-10]]]** Jesus answered, "Everyone who has left houses, brothers, sisters, father, mother, children, or property for my name's sake, will receive 100 times more and will also receive **eternal life**."
  * 耶稣回答说，“每个为我名而舍弃房屋、兄弟、姐妹、父亲、母亲、孩子、或财产的人，将会获得100倍的（报偿），也将获得永生。”
