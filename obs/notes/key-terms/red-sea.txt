====== Red Sea 红海 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

The Red Sea is a body of water that separates northern Africa from the Arabian Peninsula.

红海是分隔北非和阿拉伯半岛的水域。

  * The Red Sea forms much of the eastern border of Egypt and is to the southwest of the Promised Land.
  * 红海形成了埃及东部边界的大部分，也位于应许之地的西南。
  * The Red Sea is long and narrow. It is larger than a lake or river, but smaller than an ocean.
  * 红海形状狭长。它比湖泊或河大些，但是小于海洋。
  * The Israelites had to cross the Red Sea when they were fleeing from Egypt.
  * 以色列人在逃出埃及时，他们必须越过红海。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:promised-land|Promised Land]]**]

[参见：埃及，应许之地]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-04|[12-04]]]** When the Israelites saw the Egyptian army coming, they realized they were trapped between Pharaoh's army and the **Red Sea**.
  * 当以色列人看到埃及军队来了，他们发现他们陷在法老的军队和红海当中。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** Then God told Moses, "Tell the people to move toward the **Red Sea**."
  * 于是神对摩西说：“吩咐百姓往红海去。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-01|[13-01]]]** After God led the Israelites through the **Red Sea**, he led them through the wilderness to a mountain called Sinai.
  * 神领着以色列人经过红海后，他又带领他们经过旷野之地，来到一座山叫西乃。
