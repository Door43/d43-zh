====== rebel反叛/悖逆 W ======

==== (to rebel, rebellious) ====

（反叛，叛逆的）

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To rebel is to refuse to submit to someone's authority. That is, to refuse to obey one's leader.

反叛，就是拒绝顺服某人的权柄。也就是，拒绝顺从某人的领导。

  * Passive rebellion happens when people refuse to do what the authorities over them have commanded.
  * 当人们拒绝去做他们的管理者命令的事情时，就成了消极反叛。
  * Active rebellion happens when people directly oppose or disobey their authorities.
  * 当人们直接地对抗或不顺服他们的管理者时，就成了积极的反叛。
  * To rebel can also refer to people going to war against a government or leader that is ruling over them. People may do this when they believe that the government or leader is ruling unjustly.
  * 反叛，也可以是指与统治他们的政府或领导起战争的人。当人认为政府或领导者的统治不公正时，他们可能会这么做。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-14|[14-14]]]** After the Israelites had wandered in the wilderness for forty years, all of them who had **rebelled**  against God were dead.
  * 以色列人在旷野漂流40年之后，他们中所有悖逆神的人都死了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-07|[18-07]]]** Ten of the tribes of the nation of Israel **rebelled**  against Rehoboam.
  * 以色列国的十个部落起来反叛罗波安。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-09|[18-09]]]** Jeroboam **rebelled**  against God and caused the people to sin.
  * 耶罗波安悖逆了神，也使他的子民犯罪。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** Most of the people of Judah also **rebelled**  against God and worshiped other gods.
  * 犹大国的大多数人也悖逆神，敬拜其它的神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-07|[20-07]]]** But after a few years, the king of Judah **rebelled**  against Babylon.
  * 但是几年以后，犹大王背叛了巴比伦。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-03|[45-03]]]** Then he (Stephen) said, "You stubborn and **rebellious**  people always reject the Holy Spirit, just as your ancestors always rejected God and killed his prophets.”
  * 于是，他（司提反)说：“你们这些顽固不化的悖逆之人，总是抗拒圣灵，正如你们的先祖总是抗拒神并杀害神的先知一样。”
