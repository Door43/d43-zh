====== David ======

大衛

===== Facts: =====

事蹟 :

David was the second king of Israel and he served God faithfully.

大衛是以色列的第二個王, 他忠心的事奉神

  * God chose David to become king when he was still a young boy caring for his family's sheep.
  * 大衛還是年青的孩子牧放家裏的羊的時侯, 神就揀選他作王
  * David became a great fighter and led the Israelite army in battles against their enemies.
  * 大衛成為一個偉大的戰士，帶領以色列軍隊與他們的敵人爭戰
  * Saul tried to kill David, but God protected him, and made him king after Saul's death.
  * 掃羅想殺大衛，但神保護他，掃羅死後神就立他作王
  * David sinned against God, but he repented and God forgave him.
  * 大衛犯罪得罪了神，但是他悔改了, 神就赦免了他

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** God chose a young Israelite named **David**  to be king after Saul. **David**  was a shepherd from the town of Bethlehem. … **David**  was a humble and righteous man who trusted and obeyed God.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-03|[17-03]]]****David**  was also a great soldier and leader. When **David**  was still a young man, he fought against a giant named Goliath.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]** Saul became jealous of the people's love for **David**. Saul tried many times to kill him, so **David**  hid from Saul.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** God blessed **David**  and made him successful. **David**  fought many battles and God helped him defeat Israel's enemies.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-06|[17-06]]]****David**  wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could worship God and offer him sacrifices.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]****David**  ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years, and God blessed him. However, toward the end of his life he sinned terribly against God.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what **David**  had done, so he sent the prophet Nathan to tell **David**  how evil his sin was. **David**  repented of his sin and God forgave him. For the rest of his life, **David**  followed and obeyed God, even in difficult times.
