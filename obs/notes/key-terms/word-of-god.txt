====== word of God 神的道 ======

==== (word of God, God's word, scripture, Word of God) ====

（神之道，神之话，圣经经文。神的话语）

===== Definition: 定义： =====

The "word of God" refers to all that God has said to people. Usually it refers to his written message, also known as the "scriptures."

神的话语是指神对人说的所有一切的话。通常它指的是他的字面信息，也就是人们所熟知的“经文”。

  * God used prophets, apostles, and other people to communicate the "word of God" to us.
  * 神用先知，使徒和其他人，来向我们沟通“神的话”。
  * In modern times, the "word of God" refers to the Bible.
  * 在现今年代，“神的话”指圣经。
  * Some languages will need to translate "God's word" as, "God's message" or, "God's book" or, "God's scroll."
  * 有些语言需要把“神的话语”翻译作“神的信息”，或“神的书”，或“神之经卷”。
  * Jesus is also called, "the Word" and, "the Word of God." These titles mean that he fully reveals who God is. He can do this because he is God himself.
  * 耶稣也被称作“”道“，和”神之话“。这些称谓的意思是，他完全揭示神是谁。他可以做到这些是因为他自己本身就是神。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:prophet|prophet]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:apostle|apostle]]**] [参见：先知，使徒]

===== Examples from the Bible stories =====

圣经故事里的例子

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-07|[25-07]]]** In **God's word**  he commands his people, 'Worship only the Lord your God and only serve him.'"
  * 用神的话，他命令他的子民。”只敬拜主你们的神，并只服侍他“。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]** So Jesus explained, "The seed is the **word of God**.
  * 所以 耶稣解释说，”种子就是神的话“。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-03|[42-03]]]** Then Jesus explained to them what **God's word**  says about the Messiah.
  * 于是，耶稣向他们解释，神之道说的有是关弥赛亚的话。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-07|[42-07]]]** Jesus said, "I told you that everything written about me in **God's word**  must be fulfilled." Then he opened their minds so they could understand **God's word**.
  * 耶稣说，”我告诉你，所有用神的话写的关于我的事情，都必须要遵从”。然后他打开了他们的心灵，这样他们都明白了神的话语。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip also used other **scriptures**  to tell him the good news of Jesus.
  * 腓力也使用其它的经文，来告诉他有关耶稣的福音。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:48-12|[48-12]]]** But Jesus is the greatest prophet of all. He is the **Word of God**.
  * 耶稣是所有先知里最伟大的。他就是神的话。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to pray, to study his **word**, to worship him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
  * 神告诉你要祷告，要学习他的话，要和其他的基督徒一起敬拜他， 并告诉其他人神为你所做的一切。
