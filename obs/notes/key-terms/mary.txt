====== Mary 马利亚 P ======

===== Facts: =====

事实：

Mary was the mother of Jesus and the wife of Joseph.

马利亚是耶稣的母亲，约瑟的妻子。

  * The Holy Spirit miraculously caused Mary to become pregnant while she was a virgin. The baby in her was the Son of God.
  * 圣灵在马利亚还是个处女时，使她奇迹般地怀孕。她怀的婴孩就是神的儿子。
  * Joseph took Mary as his wife, but Mary remained a virgin until after the baby was born.
  * 约瑟娶了马利亚为妻，但是直到生婴孩（耶稣）时，马利亚仍是个处女。
  * When the baby was born, Mary and Joseph named him Jesus.
  * 当婴孩出生时，马利亚和约瑟给他取名耶稣。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

<font 14px line-height font-weight: 19.6px;;  color: inherit;  background-color: inherit /inherit;;inherit;;inherit  background-color: inherit >圣经故事中的例子</font> ：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-04|[22-04]]]** When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel appeared to Elizabeth's relative, whose name was **Mary**. She was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named Joseph. The angel said, "You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him Jesus and he will be the Messiah."
  * 当伊利沙伯怀孕六个月时，同一位天使向伊利沙伯的的一位亲戚显现，这亲戚名叫马利亚。她是个处女，已经和一个名叫约瑟的男人订了婚。天使说：“你要怀孕生子。你要给这孩子取名叫耶稣，他将是那位弥赛亚。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]** The angel explained, "The Holy Spirit will come to you, and the power of God will overshadow you. So the baby will be holy, the Son of God." **Mary**  believed and accepted what the angel said.
  * 天使解释说：“圣灵要降临到你身上，神的大能将荫庇你。所以你的孩子将是圣洁的，是神的儿子。”马利亚相信并接受了天使所说的。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-06|[22-06]]]** Soon after the angel spoke to **Mary**, she went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard **Mary's**  greeting, Elizabeth's baby jumped inside her.
  * 天使对马利亚说了后不久，马利亚去拜访伊利沙伯。伊利沙伯一听到马利亚的问安，她的孩子就在她身体里面跳动。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-02|[23-02]]]** The angel said, "Joseph, do not be afraid to take **Mary**  as your wife. The baby in her body is from the Holy Spirit."
  * 天使说：“约瑟，不要怕，娶马利亚为你的妻。她身体里的孩子是从圣灵来的。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and **Mary**  had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.
  * 约瑟和马利亚从他们居住的拿撒勒，长途跋涉来到了伯利恒，因为<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>伯利恒是</font>    他们的祖先大卫的家乡。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-01|[49-01]]]** An angel told a virgin named **Mary**  that she would give birth to God's Son. So while she was still a virgin, she gave birth to a son and named him Jesus.
  * 一位天使告诉一个名叫马利亚的处女，她将生下神的儿子。所以在她还是个处女时，生了一个儿子，取名耶稣。
