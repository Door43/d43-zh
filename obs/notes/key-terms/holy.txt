====== holy 圣洁的 P ======

===== Definition: =====

定义

To be holy is to be separated from all sin and evil. Only God is absolutely holy. But he can make things and people holy.

圣洁是与所有罪和邪恶相隔绝的。只有神是绝对圣洁的。但神也能让人和事变为圣洁的。

  * A holy person or thing is morally perfect, completely without sin.
  * 圣洁的人或事是在道德上完美的，完全没有罪。
  * People and things that God sets apart for a special purpose are called holy, such as a priest who must be ceremonially cleansed of sin in order to serve God, or a place where God reveals himself, such as, “holy ground” or his temple.
  * 神为了特殊的目的而分别出来的人和事<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>被称为</font>   <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"圣洁“</font>   ，例如祭司，他必须用一种仪式来洁净自己的罪以便能服事神；或者是一个神启示他自己的地方，例如“圣地”或神的圣殿。

Make sure that the translation of the word "holy" does not mean something that is taboo or forbidden.

确保“圣洁”一词的翻译不是指一些禁忌或禁止的东西。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]]**]

[参见 ： 圣灵]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:01-16|[01-16]]]** He (God) blessed the seventh day and made it **holy**, because on this day he rested from his work.
  * 祂（神）赐福给第7日，把这一天定为圣日，因为在这一天神歇了祂的工作而安息了。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-12|[09-12]]]**"You are standing on **holy**  ground."
  * “你所站的是圣地。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-01|[13-02]]]**"If you will obey me and keep my covenant, you will be my prized possession, a kingdom of priests, and a **holy**  nation."
  * “如果你顺服我并遵守我的约，你将作属我的珍宝，一个祭司的国度，圣洁的国民。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-05|[13-05]]]**"Always be sure to keep the Sabbath day **holy**."
  * “当把安息日守为圣日。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]**"So the baby will be **holy**, the Son of God."
  * “所以这个婴孩将是圣洁的，是神的儿子。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-02|[50-02]]]** As we wait for Jesus to return, God wants us to live in a way that is **holy**  and that honors him.
  * 在等待主耶稣回来的日子，神要我们过一个圣洁且荣耀祂的生活。
