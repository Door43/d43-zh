====== kingdom of God天国/神之国/神的国度 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

The kingdom of God refers to God's rule and authority over his people. It can also refer to the people who live under God's authority.

神的国（天国）是指神对他的子民的统治和权柄。它也指在神的权柄下生活的子民。

  * The prophets said that God would establish his kingdom by sending the Messiah to rule with righteousness and to conquer his enemies.
  * 先知们说过，神将通过差遣弥赛亚去统治公义，征服他的敌人，来建立他的国。
  * Jesus is the Son of God, and the Messiah who will rule over the kingdom of God.
  * 耶稣是神之子，就是那个将统治神的天国的弥赛亚
  * The spiritual kingdom of God has always existed in heaven because God has always reigned there.
  * 神的属灵天国一直存在天上中，因为神永远在那宝座上。
  * The physical kingdom of God will be established on earth when Jesus, the Messiah, returns from heaven to rule as the Messiah.
  * 当耶稣，弥赛亚，从天上作为弥赛亚回来统治时，神的实际的国将被建立在地上。
  * John the Baptist and Jesus both announced that the kingdom of God was near when Jesus was living on earth. However, Jesus came that first time in order to save sinners, rather than to establish his earthly kingdom.
  * 施洗约翰和耶稣两个都宣布，当耶稣在世时，神的国近了。
  * The kingdom of God will conquer Satan and his forces of evil. The kingdom of God will never be conquered and it will never end.
  * 神的国将征服撒旦和他的邪恶势力。神的国将将永远不会被征服，而且它将永远不会终结。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:24-02|[24-02]]]** He (John) preached to them, saying, "Repent, for the **kingdom of God**  is near!"
  * 他（约翰）向他们传道，说，“忏悔吧，因为神的国斤了！”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:28-06|[28-06]]]** Then Jesus said to his disciples, "It is extremely hard for rich people to enter into the **kingdom of God**! Yes, it is easier for a camel to go through the eye of a needle than for a rich man to enter the **kingdom of God**."
  * 于是耶稣对他的门徒说，“对于有钱人来说，进入神的国是何等得难啊！是的，骆驼进入针眼比有钱人进入天国还容易。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:29-02|[29-02]]]** Jesus said, "The **kingdom of God**  is like a king who wanted to settle accounts with his servants.“
  * 耶稣说，”神的国就好像一个想要和他的奴仆算账的国王。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-01|[34-01]]]** Jesus told many other stories about the **kingdom of God**. For example, he said, "The **kingdom of God**  is like a mustard seed that someone planted in his field."
  * 耶稣讲了很多关于天国的故事。例如，他说，“天国就好像是一粒芥菜种子，有人想把它种在他的地里。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-03|[34-03]]]** Jesus told another story, "The **kingdom of God**  is like yeast that a woman mixes into some bread dough until it spreads throughout the dough."
  * 耶稣讲了另一个故事，“天国就好像酵母，一个女人把它和到面团里，直到它把面团发起来。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-04|[34-04]]]**"The **kingdom of God**  is also like hidden treasure that someone hid in a field.. Another man found the treasure and then buried it again."
  * “天国就好像是一个人藏在田里的隐藏宝藏。另外的人发现了宝藏，然后又把它埋上了。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:34-05|[34-05]]]**"The **kingdom of God**  is also like a perfect pearl of great value."
  * 神的国也好比是一个极具价值的珍贵完美珍珠。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-09|[42-09]]]** He proved to his disciples in many ways that he was alive, and he taught them about the **kingdom of God**.
  * 他向他的门徒们用很多方式证明，他 是活着的，并且他告诉他们关于耶稣的故事。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-05|[49-05]]]** Jesus said that the **kingdom of God**  is more valuable than anything else in the world.
  * 耶稣说，天国比这世上的任何东西更有价值。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-02|[50-02]]]** When Jesus was living on earth he said, "My disciples will preach the good news about the **kingdom of God**  to people everywhere in the world, and then the end will come."
  * 当耶稣在地上的时候，他说，“我的门徒们将想世上的任何地方的人，宣传关于神的国的福音，然后末世将来到。”
