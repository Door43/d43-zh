====== Jew猶太人 ======

猶太人

==== (Jew, Jewish) ====

（猶太人，猶太人的，猶太族的）

===== Facts: =====

實情：

Jews are people who are descendants of Abraham through his grandson, Jacob.

猶太人就是那種民族，他們是從亞伯拉罕算起再經過他的孫子，雅各，的後裔。

  * People began to call the Israelites "Jews" after they returned to Judah from their exile in Babylon.
  * 世人開始稱呼以色列人為“猶太人”是從他們被擄到巴比倫再回到猶大之後。
  * The word "Jew" comes from the word "Judah." The Israelites who had been taken to Babylon were from the southern kingdom of Judah.
  * “猶太人”這個名詞是來自於名詞“猶大人”。因為以色列人曾經從猶大南國中被擄到巴比倫去。
  * Jesus the Messiah was Jewish. However, the Jewish religious leaders rejected Jesus and demanded that he be killed.
  * 耶穌救世主（彌賽亞）是屬於猶太族的。可是，猶太人的宗教領袖們卻棄絕了耶穌而且還要求將他殺死。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事裡的例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-11|[20-11]]]** The Israelites were now called **Jews**  and most of them had lived their whole lives in Babylon.
  * 【20－11】以色列人就是現今所謂的猶太人，他們大部分都曾經在巴比倫居住生活過。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:20-12|[20-12]]]** So, after seventy years in exile, a small group of **Jews**  returned to the city of Jerusalem in Judah.
  * 【20－12】於是，經過七十年的流亡之後，有一小群的猶太人回到了在猶大的耶路撒冷城。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** Many of the **Jews**  believed in Jesus because of this miracle.
  * 【37－10】有許多猶太人因著這件神蹟就信了耶穌。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:37-11|[37-11]]]** But the religious leaders of the **Jews**  were jealous, so they gathered together to plan how they could kill Jesus and Lazarus.
  * 【37－11】但是那些猶太人的宗教領袖們忌妒耶穌，因此他們就聚集起來計劃要如何才能殺害耶穌和拉撒路。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:40-02|[40-02]]]** Pilate commanded that they write, "King of the **Jews**" on a sign and put it on the cross above Jesus' head.
  * 【40－02】彼拉多命令他們寫一個猶太人的王的標誌掛在耶穌頭上的十字架上。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-06|[46-06]]]** Right away, Saul began preaching to the **Jews**  in Damascus, saying, "Jesus is the Son of God!"
  * 【46－06】掃羅立刻開始向大馬士革的猶太人傳道，說，“耶穌是神的兒子”。
