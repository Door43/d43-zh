====== angel天使 ======

===== Definition: =====

定义：

An angel is a powerful spirit being whom God created. These spirit beings exist to serve God by doing whatever he tells them to do. They serve God in different ways：

天使是神造的一个能力强大的神灵。这些灵体的存在是来服侍神的，做他告诉天使要做的事。 他们用不同的方式服侍神：

  * They give people messages from God, including commands and other instructions.
  * 他们给人从神那里传递讯息，包括命令和其它指令。
  * They protect and strengthen people.
  * 他们保护人，是之强大。
  * They guard people and places.
  * 他们保卫人和他们（居住的）地方。

Sometimes in the Bible "the angel of Yahweh" is used to refer to God himself.

有时在圣经里“耶和华的天使”就是指神他自己。

The opposite of "angel" is a "demon."

“天使”的反义词是“恶魔”。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:demon|demon 惡魔]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事里的一些例子：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:02-12|[02-12]]]** God placed large, powerful **angels**  at the entrance to the garden to keep anyone from eating the fruit of the tree of life.
  * 神安置天使们把守园子的入口，防止任何人偷吃生命树上的果子。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:22-03|[22-03]]]** The **angel**  responded to Zechariah,"I was sent by God to bring you this good news."
  * 天使回应了撒迦利亚，“我被神派遣来带给你福音。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:23-06|[23-06]]]** Suddenly, a shining **angel**  appeared to them (the shepherds), and they were terrified. The **angel**  said, "Do not be afraid, because I have some good news for you."
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:23-07|[23-07]]]** Suddenly, the skies were filled with **angels**  praising God…
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:25-08|[25-08]]]** Then **angels**  came and took care of Jesus.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:38-12|[38-12]]]** Jesus was very troubled and his sweat was like drops of blood. God sent an **angel**  to strengthen him.
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:38-15|[38-15]]]**"I could ask the Father for an army of **angels**  to defend me."
