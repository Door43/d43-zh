====== worship 崇拜/敬拜/供奉 ======

===== Definition: 定义 =====

"To worship" means to honor, praise and obey someone, especially God.

“敬拜”就是指荣耀、赞美和服从某人，特别是指神。

  * We worship God when we serve and honor him, by praising him and obeying him.
  * 我们敬拜神，我们通过赞美和遵从神，来服侍和荣耀他。
  * When the Israelites worshiped God, it often included sacrificing an animal on an altar.
  * 当以色列人敬拜了神，这通常是包括在祭坛上祭祀一个动物。
  * Some people worship false gods.
  * 一些人敬拜假神。

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:sacrifice|sacrifice]]**, **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:praise|praise]]**] [参见：祭祀。赞美]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:13-04|[13-04]]]** Then God gave them the covenant and said, "I am Yahweh, your God, who saved you from slavery in Egypt. Do not **worship**  other gods."
  * 于是神给他们立约时，说：“我是耶和华，你们的神，就是那个拯救你们脱离在埃及奴役的神。不要敬拜受它的神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** The Canaanites did not **worship**  or obey God. They **worshiped**  false gods and did many evil things.
  * 迦南人不敬拜或服从神。他们敬拜假神，并行了很多恶事。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:17-06|[17-06]]]** David wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could **worship**  God and offer him sacrifices.
  * 大卫想要建造一座神殿，在那里所有的以色列人都可以敬拜神，并祭祀他。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:18-12|[18-12]]]** All of the kings and most of the people of the kingdom of Israel **worshiped**  idols.
  * 以色列的所有的王和王国里的大多数人都拜偶像。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:25-07|[25-07]]]** Jesus replied, "Get away from me, Satan! In God's word he commands his people, '**Worship**  only the Lord your God and only serve him.'"
  * 耶稣回答说，“离开我，撒旦！他用神的话命令他的子民，”只敬拜主你的神，并服侍他”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** On the Sabbath, he (Jesus) went to the place **worship**.
  * 在安息日，他（耶稣）去了敬拜之地。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** There they met a woman named Lydia who was a merchant. She loved and **worshiped**  God.
  * 在那里他们遇到了一个叫吕底亚的女商人。她爱神并敬拜神。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to pray, to study his word, to **worship**  him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
  * 神告诉你要祷告，要学习他的话语，要和其他的基督徒敬拜他，并告诉其他人神为你所做的事。
