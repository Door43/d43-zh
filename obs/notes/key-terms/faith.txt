====== faith ======

信心

===== Definition: =====

Faith is trust or confidence in someone or something.

定义：信心是信赖或对某人某事有信心。

  * Faith is the practice of believing in, trusting in, or relying on someone or something.
  * 信心是因对某人或某事的相信，依靠或依赖而有的实际行动。
  * Faith in someone is believing that he always says what is true and he always does what he says he will do.
  * 对某人有信心是相信此人所说的都是真实的，并且他总是言行一致的。
  * When a person believes in God and trusts Jesus to save him, that person has faith.
  * 当一个人相信神，且相信耶稣能拯救他，那个人是有信心的。

If someone truly has faith in God, that person will act on that faith by obeying God.

如果有人确实对神有信心，那个人的信心会表现出顺服神的行动。

  * 以撒年轻时，为了试验亚伯拉罕的信心，神说：“把以撒，你唯一的儿子，杀了作为祭物献给我。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:31-07|[31-07]]]** Then he (Jesus) said to Peter, "You man of little **faith**, why did you doubt?"
  * 然后，他（耶稣）对彼得说：“你这个小信的人啊，为什么怀疑呢？”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:32-16|[32-16]]]** Jesus said to her, "Your **faith**  has healed you. Go in peace."
  * 耶稣对她说：“你的信救了你，平平安安地去吧。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-09|[38-09]]]** Then Jesus said to Peter, "Satan wants to have all of you, but I have prayed for you, Peter, that your **faith**  will not fail.
