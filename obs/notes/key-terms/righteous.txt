====== righteous正义 义 合乎道德的 合乎正道的 义士 ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "righteous" means morally good and without fault. A righteous person does what is right, good, and proper. However, no one except God is completely righteous.

这个词“正义的意思是品德高尚而且没有任何过失，一个正义的人，行的正，做的端，和适当的。无论如何，除了神没有一个人是完全的义人？

  * Righteous people obey God and live in a way that pleases him.
  * 义人顺服神，活在他所喜悦的道上。
  * God judges people as righteous when they trust in Jesus to save them and cleanse them from their sins.
  * 神判定人们成为义人当他们相信耶稣拯救他们并且洗净他们的罪。
  * Jesus lived a completely righteous life on earth. When people trust in him and follow him in obedience, God gives them Jesus' righteousness.
  * 耶稣在地球上过着完全正义的生活！当人们在信任他跟从他顺服他的时候，天父给予他们耶稣的公义。

"Righteous" is the opposite of "wicked."正义是邪恶的反
