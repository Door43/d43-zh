====== suffer受苦W ======

==== (to suffer, suffering) ====

[受苦，苦难]

===== Definition: =====

定义：

To suffer is to experience something very unpleasant, such as illness, pain, or great hardship.

受苦就是经历一些非常不愉快的事情，如疾病，疼痛，或极大的艰难。

  * Suffering is often necessary to accomplish a specific purpose.
  * 为了达到一个特殊的目的，受苦通常是必要的。
  * Some suffering is involuntary–it happens to us outside of our control.
  * 有些苦难是非自愿的—— 它发生在我们身上非人力所能掌控。
  * People can also choose to suffer for a reason, such as when the Messiah decided to suffer in order to pay for our sins.
  * 人也可以有目的地去选择受苦，如，弥赛亚选择受苦，为我们的罪付代价。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

圣经故事例证：

  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:09-13|[09-13]]]** God said, "I have seen the **suffering**  of my people."
  * 神说，“我已经看见了我百姓的苦难”。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:38-12|[38-12]]]** Jesus prayed three times, "My Father, if it is possible, please let me not have to drink this cup of **suffering**."
  * 耶稣祈求了三次，“我的父啊，如果可能，请让我不要喝这苦杯。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-03|[42-03]]]** He (Jesus) reminded them that the prophets said the Messiah would **suffer**  and be killed, but would rise again on the third day.
  * 他（耶稣）提醒他们，先知们说弥赛亚将要受苦并被杀害，但三天后要复活。
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:42-07|[42-07]]]** He (Jesus) said, "It was written long ago that the Messiah would **suffer**, die, and rise from the dead on the third day."
  * 他（耶稣）说，“经上早已记载，弥赛亚将会受苦，受死，并且三天后要从死里复活。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:44-05|[44-05]]]**"Although you did not understand what you were doing, God used your actions to fulfill the prophecies that the Messiah would **suffer**  and die."
  * ”尽管你们不明白你们所做的是什么，但神是用你们所做的来应验弥赛亚必受苦、受死的许多预言。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:46-04|[46-04]]]** God said, "I have chosen him (Saul) to declare my name to the unsaved. I will show him how much he must **suffer**  for my sake."
  * 神说，”我已拣选他（扫罗）去把我的名传给那些尚未蒙拯救的人。我将向他显明，他必须为我的名而受许多的苦。”
  * **[[:zh:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** He (Jesus) will wipe away every tear and there will be no more **suffering**, sadness, crying, evil, pain, or death.
  * 他（耶稣）将擦干每一滴泪水，不再有苦难，悲伤，哭泣，罪恶，痛苦或死亡。
