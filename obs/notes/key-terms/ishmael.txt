====== Ishmael以实玛利 ======

===== Facts: =====

事实依据：

Ishmael was the son of Abraham and Sarah's servant, Hagar.

以实玛力是亚伯拉罕和撒拉的女仆夏甲的儿子。

  * Ishmael was not the son through whom God would fulfill his promise to Abraham.
  * 以实玛利不是神履行对亚伯拉罕应许的那个儿子。
  * Ishmael means, "God hears."
  * 以实玛利意思是，“神听到。”

[See also: **[[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:abraham|Abraham]], [[:zh:obs:notes:key-terms:sarah|Sarah]]**]
