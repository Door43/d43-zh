  
===== Door43 (Mandarin) =====

Welcome to Door43, an openly-collaborative platform for creating, translating, and distributing open-licensed discipleship resources in any language.

==== Resources ====

These are the resources currently available for translation and use:

  * **[[:zh:obs|打开圣经故事]]** – A visual mini-Bible in any language, in text, audio, and video formats.​
  * [[:zh:obs:notes|translationNotes]] – Translation and exegetical notes for Open Bible Stories.
  * [[:zh:lexicon|Open Bible Lexicon]] – A Biblical lexicon, intended for integration with Open Bible Stories and Bible translations.
  * [[:zh:library|Open Christian Library]] – A growing catalog of open-licensed Biblical resources.
  * [[:zh:ta|translationAcademy]] – A modular, media-based, self-paced training course for translators and translation facilitators. 
  * [[https://www.dropbox.com/s/d1z69wi1rfnke1b/Translation Textbook.pdf?dl=0|圣经翻译原则]]（译自Katy Barnwell的著作）
  * [[https://www.dropbox.com/s/53036yobhijpzsh/Semantics Textbook.pdf|语义学与翻译导论，译自SIL教材（zh）]]
  * [[https://www.dropbox.com/s/hanto91kydvigw1/BTandUse_JChung_BT2011_TransformChiDiasporaLaityViaBT_Chin.pptx|动员华人信徒参与圣经翻译]]
  * [[http://www.ubscp.org/|Bibles In China(United Bible Society) 該頁的中文內容跟英文的內容不同]]
  * [[http://tushuoyiwen.blogspot.hk/|話題：聖經、猶太文化、希伯來文、希臘文、翻譯、編輯]]
  * [[http://bibletranslation-hk.blogspot.hk/p/blog-page_4.html|一個香港律師看中文聖經翻譯]]
  * [[http://www.translatebible.com|聖经、中文、翻译（繁体）]]
  * [[http://bible.fhl.net/gbdoc/new/fhlwhparsing.html|中文线上希腊语圣经WH新约parsing]]
  * [[http://www.themelios-genji.net/index.html|中文网上新约希腊语教程]]
