====== curtain ======

===== Definition: =====

Curtains were thick, heavy pieces of material used in the construction of Yahweh's place of worship during Israel's desert wanderings.

  * Heavy curtains were made of materials such as animal skins and formed the top and sides of the temple.
  * Curtains of thinner materials also were used as separators between the inner chambers of the temple.
  * A curtain called a veil separated the inner sanctuary of God's presence called the Holy of Holies.
