====== Asa ======

===== Facts: =====

Asa was King of Judah, 913-873 BC, successor of his father Abijah.

  * He attempted an effetive religious reform by ridding the Kingdom of false gods and practices and restoring the worship of Yahweh.
  * Asa formed an alliance with Syria against Egypt and continue warfare with Israel.
  * He died of disease and was buried in a royal tomb in Jerusalem.
