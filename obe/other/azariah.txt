====== Azariah ======

===== Facts: =====

Azariah was a young man who was among the captives taken to Babylon with Daniel.

  * In Babylon Azariah was given the name Abednego and served in the royal court with his two close friends Shadrach and Meschach.
  * Azariah chose to honor and serve the God of Abraham even though worshipping Him was forbidden by Babylonian law under the penalty of death.
