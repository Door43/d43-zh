====== Amaziah ======

===== Facts: =====

Amaziah was the King of Judah, 800-783 BC, succeding his father Joash.

  * He put to death all those responisble for the murder of his father.
  * He won a decisive military victory over Edom and returned it to the Kingdom of Judah.
  * His challenge to Israel had disastrous results, reducing Judah to a vassal state of Israel.
  * He fell victim to a court plot and was assasinated,
